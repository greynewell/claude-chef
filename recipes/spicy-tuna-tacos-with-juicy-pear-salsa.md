## Ingredients

- ½  pear, diced
- ½ medium red onion, diced
- ½  carrot, peeled and finely chopped
- ¼  red bell pepper, diced
- ¼  yellow pepper, diced
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1  green onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon cayenne powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 pound yellowfin tuna
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 4 (6 inch) flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. Combine pear, red onion, carrot, bell peppers, jalapeno pepper, green onion, cilantro, lime juice, garlic, and cayenne powder in a bowl.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Set pear salsa aside to allow flavors to meld.
4. Heat olive oil in a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat.
5. Season tuna with chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper; add to the skillet.
6. Sear for 3 minutes.
7. Splash 1 tablespoon lemon juice over the tuna.
8. Turn tuna over and splash remaining lemon juice on the second side.
9. Continue cooking until center is medium-rare, about 3 minutes more.
10. Transfer tuna to a plate and let rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
11. Slice and serve over tortillas.
12. Top with pear salsa.
