## Ingredients

- ½ pound bulk Italian sausage
- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup chopped yellow onion
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 3 ½ cups Swanson® Unsalted Chicken Broth
- ½ cup chopped spinach
- ½ cup canned diced tomatoes
- 1 (16 ounce) package potato gnocchi
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese (or more to taste),  for garnish

## Instructions

1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook ground sausage until browned, breaking it up into small pieces, about 7 minutes. Transfer sausage to a bowl. Drain fat from skillet; wipe out skillet with a paper towel.
2. Melt butter in skillet over medium heat, Stir in onion and saute until translucent, 2 or 3 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another minute. Whisk in flour until evenly mixed into butter, 1 minute. Slowly pour in cream and Swanson® Unsalted Chicken Broth. Whisk mixture until it comes to a boil, then immediately reduce heat to low. Simmer until mixture thickens, whisking occasionally, about 10 minutes.
3. Return sausage to the skillet; add spinach, tomatoes, and gnocchi. Cook over medium heat until gnocchi is heated through, 2 to 3 minutes. Add dash of salt and pepper to taste. Garnish with Parmesan cheese.
