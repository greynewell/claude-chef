## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers, drained
- ½ (4 ounce) can diced jalapeno peppers, drained
- 5 slices cooked bacon, crumbled
- 1 bunch cilantro leaves, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Mix cream cheese, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, chile peppers, jalapeno peppers, bacon, and cilantro together in a bowl until well combined. Transfer to a baking dish.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until hot and bubbly, about 30 minutes. Stir and serve.
