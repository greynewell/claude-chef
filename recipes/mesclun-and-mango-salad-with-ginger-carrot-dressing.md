## Ingredients

- ¼ cup rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
- 1 tablespoon shredded carrot
- ¼ teaspoon lime juice
- ½ pound mixed baby greens
- 1  mango - peeled, seeded and diced
- 2 teaspoons sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, carrot, and lime juice.
2. In a medium bowl, toss rice vinegar mixture with baby greens and mango.
3. Sprinkle with sesame seeds to serve.
