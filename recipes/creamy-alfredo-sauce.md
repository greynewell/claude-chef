## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter, or more to taste
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese (such as Kraft®)
- 2 cups milk
- 1 ½ teaspoons Italian seasoning
- 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 1 pinch ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over medium-high heat; add garlic and sauté until golden, 2 to 3 minutes. Reduce heat to medium and gradually stir in flour until smooth, 3 to 5 minutes.
2. Add Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and mixture is thickened, 2 to 3 minutes. Slowly pour in milk, stirring constantly, until smooth, about 5 minutes.
3. Season with Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Cook and stir until sauce reaches desired consistency, 5 to 10 minutes.
