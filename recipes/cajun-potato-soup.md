## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ½  onion, diced
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 pounds andouille sausage, sliced into rounds
- 6  russet potatoes, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
- 3 cups chicken broth
- 2 cups milk
- 1 ¾ cups heavy cream
- 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
- 1 bunch fresh spinach, chopped
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat the butter and oil together in a large stock pot over medium heat.
2. Cook the onion and garlic in the butter and oil until the onions are translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add the sausage slices; cook and stir another 5 minutes.
4. Stir in the potatoes; cook and stir 15 minutes.
5. Pour in the chicken broth, milk, heavy cream, and Italian seasoning.
6. Bring to a simmer and cook 10 to 12 minutes until the potatoes are tender.
7. Mix in the spinach.
8. Remove from heat.
9. Top with Parmesan cheese.
