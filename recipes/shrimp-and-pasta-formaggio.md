## Ingredients

- 220 grams angel hair pasta, uncooked
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (398 mL) can no-salt-added diced tomatoes
- 8 cups baby spinach leaves
- 1 pound frozen cooked cleaned (with tails left on) medium shrimp
- ½ cup heavy cream
- 1 cup CRACKER BARREL Shredded 4 Cheese Italiano Cheese
- 3 tablespoons KRAFT 100% Parmesan Grated Cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta as directed on package, omitting salt.
2. Meanwhile, heat oil in large skillet on medium heat.
3. Add garlic; cook and stir 1 minute or just until fragrant and tender.
4. Stir in tomatoes; simmer 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.
5. Add spinach; cook and stir 1 minute or just until wilted.
6. Add shrimp and cream; cook 4 minutes or until heated through, stirring occasionally.
7. Drain pasta.
8. Add to skillet; toss to coat.
9. Top with cheeses; cover.
10. Remove from heat; let stand 5 minutes or until shredded cheese is melted.
