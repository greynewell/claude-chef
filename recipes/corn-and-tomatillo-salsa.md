## Ingredients

- 4 ears fresh corn kernels
- 2  seeded and finely chopped jalapeno peppers
- ½ pound husked, cored and chopped tomatillos
- ½  chopped green bell pepper
- 2  thinly sliced green onions
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons water
- ½ teaspoon ground coriander
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 (14.5 ounce) package tortilla chips

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet, combine the corn kernels and jalapeno peppers.
2. Cook and stir for 10 minutes, until the jalapenos are soft.
3. In a saucepan over high heat, combine the corn and jalapeno mixture with the tomatillos, green bell peppers, green onions, lime juice, water and coriander.
4. Cover and bring to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to low and simmer 5 minutes, stirring once.
6. Remove from heat.
7. Cool for 10 minutes.
8. Stir in the cilantro.
9. Allow the mixture to cool in the refrigerator.
10. Serve with tortilla chips.
