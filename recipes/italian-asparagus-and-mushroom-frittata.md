## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, peeled
- ½ bunch asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 (9 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- ⅓ cup dry white wine
- 4  eggs
- ⅓ cup milk
- 3 tablespoons freshly grated Parmesan cheese, divided
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Heat oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium heat and cook garlic until it starts to sizzle, about 1 minute.
3. Add asparagus and mushrooms and cook until softened, 4 to 5 minutes.
4. Season with salt and pour in white wine.
5. Discard garlic.
6. Mix eggs, milk, 2 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
7. Pour into the skillet over the asparagus and mushrooms.; stir gently.
8. Cook until eggs start to set, about 3 minutes.
9. Sprinkle with remaining 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese and transfer to the oven.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until frittata has risen and is set, about 20 minutes.
11. Cool slightly before serving.
