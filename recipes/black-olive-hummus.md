## Ingredients

- 1 clove garlic
- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained and rinsed
- 1 (6 ounce) can black olives, drained and liquid reserved
- ¼ cup olive oil, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Place garlic in food processor and pulse until garlic is minced.
2. Add garbanzo beans; puree until chickpeas have broken down into a paste.
3. Pour in liquid from olives, 1 tablespoon at a time; puree until fully incorporated, about 2 to 3 tablespoons liquid in total.
4. Keep food processor running and slowly drizzle olive oil into hummus until smooth and desired consistency is reached.
5. Add olives to the hummus and pulse 3 to 7 times until olives are chopped and evenly distributed.
