## Ingredients

- 8 ounces dry fettuccini pasta
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 cup cottage cheese
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 3 teaspoons minced onion
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups diced, cooked chicken breast meat

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook pasta in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain, and set aside.
3. In a food processor, place the milk, flour, cottage cheese, garlic powder, onion, salt and pepper, and Parmesan cheese.
4. Blend until smooth.
5. Transfer the blended mixture to a saucepan over medium heat.
6. Mix in the chicken, and cook until heated through.
7. Serve hot over pasta.
