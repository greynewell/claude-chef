## Ingredients

- 2  cucumbers - halved, seeded, and cut into 1/8-inch slices
- 2 cups blueberries
- 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
- ½ cup chopped scallions
- ¼ cup finely chopped flat-leaf parsley
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- 1 teaspoon honey
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumbers, blueberries, feta cheese, scallions, and parsley in a bowl.
2. Mix oil, vinegar, honey, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
3. Pour dressing over salad; toss to combine.
