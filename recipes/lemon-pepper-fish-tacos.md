## Ingredients

- 1 square Lemon Pepper Saute Express® Saute Starter
- ½ pound tilapia fillets
- 3 cups coleslaw mix
- ½ cup cilantro leaves
- 8  hard corn taco shells, warmed
- ¼ tablespoon Pico de gallo
- ¼ tablespoon Guacamole
- ¼ teaspoon Sour cream

## Instructions

1. Melt Saute Express® square in 10-inch nonstick skillet over medium-low heat just until bubbles begin to form.
2. Add fish.
3. Saute 7-10 minutes or until golden brown on both sides and flaky.
4. Remove from skillet.
5. Stir coleslaw and cilantro into skillet until coated and heated through.
6. Break fish into bite-sized pieces.
7. Fill each taco shell with coleslaw mixture, fish and additional toppings.
