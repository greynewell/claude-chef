## Ingredients

- 8 cups water
- 1 pound raw Virginia peanuts in shells
- ⅓ cup salt
- 1 tablespoon Creole seasoning (such as Tony Chachere's®)
- 1 tablespoon liquid crab boil seasoning

## Instructions

1. Place water, peanuts, salt, Creole seasoning, and liquid crab boil in a multi-functional pressure cooker (such as Instant Pot®) and stir to combine. Close and lock the lid. Select high pressure according to manufacturer's instructions; set timer for 180 minutes. Allow 25 minutes for pressure to build.
2. Release pressure using the natural-release method according to manufacturer's instructions, about 25 minutes. Unplug the cooker but keep the lid on. Let peanuts sit for 2 hours for maximum flavor.
