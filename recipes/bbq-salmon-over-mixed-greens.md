## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground allspice
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
- 1 tablespoon paprika
- 6 (6 ounce) fillets salmon
- olive oil
- 1 ½ cups tomato-vegetable juice cocktail
- 1 tablespoon balsamic or cider vinegar
- ½ cup chopped tomatoes
- 4 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 pound mixed salad greens, rinsed and dried

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix together chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, sugar, salt, allspice, cumin, white pepper, and paprika.
2. Reserve 1 1/2 tablespoons of the mixture for the vinaigrette, and sprinkle remaining spice mixture over salmon fillets.
3. Cover, and refrigerate for 6 hours.
4. Preheat grill for high heat.
5. Lightly oil grill grate, and spread a small amount of olive oil on salmon fillets.
6. Cook salmon 4 to 5 minutes per side, or until easily flaked with a fork.
7. In a small bowl, mix together tomato-vegetable juice cocktail, vinegar, tomatoes, olive oil, and reserved spice mixture to make vinaigrette.
8. Place salad greens in a large bowl, drizzle with vinaigrette, and toss to coat.
9. Divide greens among individual serving plates.
10. Top each plate with a salmon fillet, and spoon any remaining vinaigrette over the salmon.
