## Ingredients

- ¾ cup cooked truRoots® Organic Germinated Brown Rice
- ¾ cup black beans, rinsed and drained
- ½ cup prepared salsa
- ¼ cup finely shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½  avocado, pitted and sliced
- 1 wedge Lime wedge, cilantro sprigs and hot sauce for garnish

## Instructions

1. Spoon rice, beans and salsa into bowl.
2. Sprinkle with cheese.
3. Top with avocado slices.
4. Garnish with lime, cilantro and hot sauce, if desired.
