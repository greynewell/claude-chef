## Ingredients

- 6 cups water
- 1 ½ cups split black lentils
- ½ small onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2 teaspoons white sugar
- 2 teaspoons kosher salt
- 2 teaspoons curry powder, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
- 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger root, chopped
- ½ cup heavy whipping cream

## Instructions

1. Combine water, lentils, onion, garlic, sugar, salt, curry powder, turmeric, and ginger root in slow cooker.
2. Cook on High for 5 hours. Stir cream into lentils until warmed through.
