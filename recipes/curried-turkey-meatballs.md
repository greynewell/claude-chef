## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground turkey
- 1  yellow onion, finely chopped
- ⅓ cup dry bread crumbs
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 ½ teaspoons Madras curry powder
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 pinch ground cinnamon
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine turkey, onion, bread crumbs, egg, curry powder, salt, cumin, ginger, and cinnamon together in a bowl using your hands; form into about 20 balls.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Cook meatballs, working in batches, in hot oil until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes.
5. Transfer browned meatballs to a baking dish.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, about 15 minutes.
7. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
