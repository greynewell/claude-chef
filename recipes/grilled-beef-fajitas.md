## Ingredients

- 1 (1 ounce) package fajita seasoning mix
- 1 pound boneless beef top round steak
- Reynolds Wrap® Heavy Duty Aluminum Foil
- 1 medium red or yellow bell pepper, cut into strips
- 1 medium green bell pepper, cut into strips
- 1 medium onion, cut into 1/4-inch slices
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
- Salsa

## Instructions

1. Prepare fajita seasoning mix following package directions and pour over steak.
2. Cover with Reynolds Wrap® Heavy Duty Aluminum Foil and marinate steak 1 to 2 hours.
3. Make a grill pan by shaping two layers of foil over the outside of 13x9x2inch baking pan.
4. Remove foil and crimp the edges to form a tight rim, making a pan with one inch sides.
5. Place on cookie sheet.
6. Preheat grill to high.
7. Remove steak from marinade discard marinade.
8. Add peppers, onion and oil to foil pan.
9. Slide foil pan from cookie sheet onto grill and place steak beside pan on grill.
10. Grill 8 to 10 minutes on high in covered grill.
11. Stir vegetables and turn steak after 5 minutes.
12. Slide foil pan from grill onto cookie sheet.
13. Slice grilled steak into thin strips.
14. Wrap tortillas in foil add to grill to heat while slicing steak.
15. Serve beef, peppers and onions wrapped in warm tortillas with salsa.
