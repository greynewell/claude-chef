## Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa, rinsed and drained
- 1 ¾ cups vegetable broth
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- ½  large onion, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
- 1  tomato, seeded and chopped
- 3 tablespoons annatto paste (achiote)
- 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- 1  dried chipotle chile pepper
- kosher salt, to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Cover quinoa with vegetable broth in an oven-proof saucepan.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. Cover the saucepan with aluminum foil.
5. Bake in preheated oven until quinoa is dry and fluffy, about 15 minutes.
6. Keep covered.
7. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
8. Pour in vegetable oil.
9. Cook and stir onion and garlic until soft, about 5 minutes.
10. Stir in tomatoes, annatto paste, vinegar, paprika, oregano, cumin, and chipotle pepper.
11. Cook until mixture is thick and stew-like, 10 to 15 minutes.
12. Toss the mixture with the baked quinoa.
13. Let cool, about 10 minutes.
14. Season with salt.
