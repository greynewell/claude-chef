## Ingredients

- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ cup light mayonnaise (such as Hellmann's Light®)
- ½ cup salsa
- 1 teaspoon vegetable bouillon (such as Knorr®)), or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix (such as Old El Paso®)
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar-Monterey Jack cheese blend

## Instructions

1. Stir sour cream, mayonnaise, salsa, vegetable bouillon, and taco seasoning mix together in a bowl until smooth. Fold cheese into the creamy mixture.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, at least 15 minutes.
