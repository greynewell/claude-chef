## Ingredients

- ½ cup light mayonnaise (such as Fred Meyer®)
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 tablespoon Madras curry powder
- 1 tablespoon ground turmeric
- ½ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- 1 pound cubed cooked chicken breast tenders (such as Foster Farms®)
- 1  Honeycrisp apple, chopped
- ½ cup raw slivered almonds, chopped
- 5  whole-wheat tortillas (such as Mission®)

## Instructions

1. Combine mayonnaise, lime juice, curry powder, turmeric, and cayenne pepper in a bowl.
2. Fold in chicken, apple, and almonds.
3. Spoon a scoop of chicken salad mixture in a line across the middle of each tortilla.
4. Fold opposing edges of the tortillas to overlap the filling.
5. Roll 1 of the opposing edges around the filling.
