## Ingredients

- 4 (3 ounce) fresh tilapia fillets
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup fresh lime juice
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- 2  fresh jalapeno peppers, sliced into rings
- 8 sprigs cilantro leaves
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Lay tilapia fillets in a glass baking dish.
2. Drizzle olive oil and lime juice evenly over the fish.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Allow to sit at room temperature for 1 hour.
5. Transfer tilapia to a non-stick skillet and pour the lime juice mixture over the fish.
6. Scatter the tomatoes, jalapenos, and cilantro over the fillets.
7. Cook over medium heat for about 4 minutes per side.
