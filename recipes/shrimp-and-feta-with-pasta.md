## Ingredients

- 8 ounces orzo pasta
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ small onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans diced tomatoes in juice
- ½ cup water
- ½ cup dry white wine
- ½ teaspoon chopped fresh oregano
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ pounds large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 4 ounces crumbled feta cheese
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook orzo in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion until slightly softened, about 3 minutes.
5. Add garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
6. Mix tomatoes in juice, water, wine, oregano, and salt into onion mixture; cook and stir until sauce is thickened, about 10 minutes.
7. Mix shrimp into sauce and cook until shrimp are cooked through, 2 to 3 minutes.
8. Stir feta cheese and parsley into sauce; serve over orzo.
