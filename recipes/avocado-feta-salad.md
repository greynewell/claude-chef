## Ingredients

- 3 cups cubed avocado
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 3 tablespoons lime juice
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 6 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup diced feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Place avocado cubes in a bowl.
2. Whisk olive oil, lime juice, lemon juice, garlic, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; mix in feta cheese. Pour dressing over avocado cubes; toss to coat.
