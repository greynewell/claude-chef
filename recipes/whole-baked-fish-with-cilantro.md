## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 (5 pound) whole trout
- 3  tomatoes, chopped
- 1 large onion, roughly chopped
- 2  lemons, juiced
- 1 small bunch cilantro sprigs
- 3 cloves garlic, halved
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ½ cup water
- 1  potatoes, cut into 1/4-inch slices, or more to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Drizzle a large oven-safe pan with oil.
3. Rinse trout and pat dry with a paper towel.
4. Place on a flat work surface.
5. Cut trout from one side through the middle horizontally to within one-half inch of the other side.
6. Open the two sides and spread them out like an open book; lay onto the prepared pan.
7. Place tomatoes, onion, lemon juice, cilantro, garlic, coriander, cumin, salt, pepper, and cinnamon in a food processor.
8. Pulse until chopped fine but not pureed.
9. Pour 1/2 the mixture on top of 1/2 of the trout; fold the other side of trout on top.
10. Spoon the remaining 1/2 of the mixture on and around the sealed trout.
11. Add water to the pan; arrange potatoes around the trout.
12. Cover pan with aluminum foil.
13. Bake in the preheated oven until trout flakes easily with a fork, about 45 minutes.
