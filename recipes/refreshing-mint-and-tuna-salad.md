## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna in water, drained
- 1 (6 ounce) package crumbled garlic and herb feta cheese
- 3  hearts of romaine lettuce, torn into pieces
- 1  cucumber, peeled and chopped
- 4  green onions, chopped
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ cup chopped fresh mint leaves
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large serving bowl, toss together the tuna, feta cheese, lettuce, cucumber and green onion.
2. In a smaller bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, parsley, mint leaves, salt and pepper.
3. Pour over salad and toss gently.
4. Serve immediately.
