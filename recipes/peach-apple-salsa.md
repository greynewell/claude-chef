## Ingredients

- 1 cup diced peaches
- ½ cup diced apple
- ½ cup diced avocado
- ½ cup diced tomato
- ⅓ cup chopped green onion
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix peaches, apple, avocado, tomato, green onion, cilantro, lemon juice, olive oil, sesame oil, cumin, jalapeno pepper, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
2. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate for flavors to blend, at least 3 hours.
