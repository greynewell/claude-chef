## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1 cup quinoa
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- ⅔ cup halved grape tomatoes
- ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
- ¼ cup roasted unsalted sunflower seeds
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
- 1 tablespoon minced shallot
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- ¼ teaspoon sea salt
- ¼ teaspoon fresh ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring water, quinoa, and 1/2 teaspoon sea salt to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is tender and water has been absorbed, 15 to 20 minutes.
3. Spread quinoa on a baking sheet and cool, about 30 minutes.
4. Mix quinoa, tomatoes, feta cheese, sunflower seeds, parsley, olives, and shallot together in a large bowl.
5. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, mustard, garlic, 1/4 teaspoon sea salt, and pepper together in a bowl; pour over quinoa mixture and toss to coat.
