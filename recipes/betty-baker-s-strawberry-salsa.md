## Ingredients

- 2 cups strawberries, sliced
- 1 cup grape tomatoes, coarsely chopped
- ½ cup green onions, chopped
- 2 tablespoons jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- ¼ cup fresh mint, chopped
- 2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped
- 1 teaspoon grated lime zest
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon coarse salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Place the strawberries, tomatoes, green onions, jalapeno pepper, mint, cilantro, and lime zest into a mixing bowl.
2. Stir in the lime juice, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to serve.
