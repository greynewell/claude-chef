## Ingredients

- 3 large mangoes, diced
- 1 (15 ounce) can sweet corn, drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 2  limes, juiced
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix mangoes, sweet corn, and black beans in a large bowl; add lime juice and stir.
2. Sprinkle cilantro over the mango mixture; toss to combine.
