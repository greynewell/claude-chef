## Ingredients

- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- salt and pepper to taste
- 2 pounds cube steak
- ¼ cup vegetable oil for frying
- 1 (32 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, combine the bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
2. Dredge the meat in the crumbs.
3. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Place the breaded meat in the oil, and saute for 5 to 10 minutes, or until well browned on both sides.
5. Drain excess oil, and pour in the spaghetti sauce.
6. Reduce heat to low, and simmer for 30 minutes.
