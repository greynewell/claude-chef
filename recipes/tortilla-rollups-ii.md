## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 4  green onions, finely chopped
- ¼ cup salsa
- 4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
- 10 (10 inch) flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the cream cheese, green onions, salsa and Cheddar cheese.
2. Spread the cream cheese mixture on the tortillas.
3. Roll the tortillas and refrigerate about 4 hours before slicing into 1 inch long pieces.
