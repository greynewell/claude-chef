## Ingredients

- ½ (12 ounce) package fully-cooked ham steak, cut into strips
- ½ cup chopped celery
- ½ cup chopped sweet onion (such as Vidalia®)
- ¼ cup chopped red bell pepper
- ¼ cup chopped sweet pickle, or more to taste
- 1 (8 ounce) can diced pineapple, fully drained
- 2 tablespoons creamy salad dressing
- 1 tablespoon honey mustard
- 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
- ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pinch curry powder, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 pinch garlic powder, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Toss ham, celery, sweet onion, red bell pepper, sweet pickle, pineapple, creamy salad dressing, honey mustard, dill weed, black pepper, curry powder, and garlic powder together in a large bowl.
2. Refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
