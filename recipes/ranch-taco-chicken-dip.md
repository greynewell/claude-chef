## Ingredients

- 2 (12.5 fl oz) cans white meat chicken
- 1 cup sharp Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
- ¼ cup taco sauce
- 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- ½ (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix

## Instructions

1. Mix chicken, Cheddar cheese, Greek yogurt, taco sauce, taco seasoning mix, and ranch dressing mix in a slow cooker.
2. Cook on Low until warmed through, about 30 minutes.
