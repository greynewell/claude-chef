## Ingredients

- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 2 pounds leeks, white parts only, finely chopped
- 1  lemon, juiced
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large frying pan over medium-low heat; cook and stir garlic and sugar in the hot oil until garlic is lightly browned, 3 to 5 minutes.
2. Mix leeks into garlic mixture; cook and stir until leeks are lightly browned, about 10 minutes.
3. Drizzle lemon juice over leek mixture; season with salt and pepper.
4. Cover pan and cook over low heat until leeks are tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
