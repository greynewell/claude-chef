## Ingredients

- ½ cup soy sauce
- ⅓ cup mushroom stock
- 1 teaspoon miso paste, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon oyster sauce, or to taste
- 1 ½ pounds bok choy, chopped
- 10  fresh shiitake mushrooms, sliced

## Instructions

1. Pour soy sauce and mushroom stock into a large skillet over high heat; whisk in miso paste and oyster sauce until dissolved, 2 to 3 minutes.
2. Add bok choy and mushrooms; bring to a rolling boil.
3. Reduce heat to medium, cover skillet, and simmer until vegetables are tender, about 15 minutes.
