## Ingredients

- 1  orange, peeled and cut into small chunks
- 2  green onions, diced
- ½  jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced
- 4 (6 ounce) tilapia fillets
- ½  lemon, juiced, or more to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 teaspoon oil

## Instructions

1. Combine orange chunks, green onions, and jalapeno pepper together in a small bowl.
2. Let sit until flavors combine, about 10 minutes.
3. Season tilapia fillets with lemon juice, salt, and black pepper.
4. Heat butter and oil in a large grill pan over medium-high heat.
5. Cook fish until opaque on one side, about 3 minutes.
6. Flip fish and cook until lightly browned and fish flakes easily with a fork, about 3 minutes more.
7. Top fish with orange salsa and a sprinkle of lemon juice.
