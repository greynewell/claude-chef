## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) package Johnsonville® Jalapeno and Cheese Smoked Sausage, finely diced
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup sour cream
- 1 (15 ounce) can BUSH'S® Black Beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- 1 cup salsa verde
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Tortilla chips
- jalapeno peppers, sliced
- tomatoes, chopped
- chopped fresh cilantro
- lime

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, blend softened cream cheese and sour cream until smooth.
2. Add diced sausage, black beans, shredded cheese, salsa, and garlic; mix gently to combine.
3. Transfer to a 2-quart slow cooker.
4. Cover and cook on LOW for 2 1/2 hours, or until heated through, stirring every 30 minutes.
5. Reduce heat to WARM to hold for serving.
6. Serve with tortilla chips.
7. If desired, garnish with jalapenos, tomatoes, cilantro, or a squeeze of fresh lime juice.
