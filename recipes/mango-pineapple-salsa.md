## Ingredients

- 1  fresh pineapple, diced
- 2 cups diced fresh mango
- ½ cup minced red onion
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- 3 tablespoons lime juice
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Stir pineapple, mango, onion, jalapeno, lime juice, and cilantro together in a bowl; season with salt.
2. Let salsa sit until flavors blend, at least 30 minutes.
