## Ingredients

- 6 ounces penne pasta
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1  green bell pepper, sliced
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- 1 ½ cups dry hard cider
- 1 ½ tablespoons tomato paste
- salt to taste
- ground black pepper to taste
- ¾ pound fresh prawns

## Instructions

1. In a large skillet over medium heat cook onion in olive oil for 2 minutes.
2. Add garlic, green bell pepper, celery and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
3. Stir in tomatoes, tomato puree, dry cider, salt and pepper and bring to a boil.
4. Add pasta, mixing in well.
5. Cover and let simmer for 15 minutes.
6. Remove lid, add prawns and cook for 5 minutes.
7. Adjust seasoning, if necessary.
8. Serve warm.
