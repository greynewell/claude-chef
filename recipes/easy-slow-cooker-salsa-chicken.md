## Ingredients

- 3  chicken breasts
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 (15.5 ounce) jar salsa
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers

## Instructions

1. Place chicken in the bottom of a slow cooker. Sprinkle with chili powder, garlic powder, cumin, and onion powder. Top evenly with salsa and green chile peppers.
2. Cook on Low until chicken breasts are very tender, 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken breast with 2 forks.
