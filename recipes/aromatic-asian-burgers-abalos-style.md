## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon ground ginger
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1  green onion, chopped
- 1 (5 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and diced
- 1 tablespoon peanut oil

## Instructions

1. Knead together the sesame oil, soy sauce, garlic powder, ground ginger, onion powder, pepper, ground beef, green onion, and water chestnuts in a bowl until evenly combined; form into four patties.
2. Heat the peanut oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Cook in the skillet until the burgers are cooked to your desired degree of doneness, about 5 minutes per side for well done.
4. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
