## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  Campbell's® Condensed Tomato Soup (Regular or 25% Less Sodium)
- ½ cup salsa
- ½ cup water
- 6 (6 inch) flour tortillas, cut into 1-inch pieces
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook beef in 10-inch skillet until well browned, stirring to break up meat.
2. Pour off fat.
3. Stir in soup, salsa, water, tortillas.
4. Heat to a boil.
5. Reduce heat to low and cook for 5 minutes Stir.
6. Top with cheese.
