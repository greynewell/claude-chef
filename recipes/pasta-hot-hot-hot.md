## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. In a small saucepan over low heat place olive oil, garlic and peppers and simmer.
4. Pour olive oil mixture over cooked pasta and serve with Parmesan cheese.
