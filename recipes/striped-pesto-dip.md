## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ⅓ cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 6 tablespoons pesto
- ¼ cup chopped toasted walnuts

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, blend cream cheese, sour cream and Parmesan cheese.
2. In a small clear glass serving dish, spread 1/3 the cream cheese mixture.
3. Top with 2 tablespoons pesto.
4. Repeat layering, ending with a topping of pesto.
5. Sprinkle with walnuts and serve.
