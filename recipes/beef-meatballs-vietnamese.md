## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 cup finely chopped red onion
- ⅓ cup roughly chopped cilantro
- 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon peeled and finely chopped ginger
- 1 ½ tablespoons fish sauce
- ½ tablespoon honey
- 1  green chile pepper, chopped  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons canola oil
- ¼ cup hoisin sauce
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- 1  Thai chile pepper, finely chopped  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine ground beef, onion, cilantro, garlic, ginger, fish sauce, honey, and chile pepper in a food processor and blend until mixed and minced.
2. Transfer to a bowl.
3. Coat fingers and palms lightly with oil and scoop tablespoons of beef mixture to create equal-sized meatballs and place them on a plate.
4. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
5. Add meatballs; cover and cook 5 minutes.
6. Turn meatballs over, cover pan, and cook until golden brown, about 5 minutes more.
7. Combine hoisin sauce, soy sauce, water, fish sauce, and chile pepper in a bowl and serve with meatballs.
8. Pour over meatballs or use as a dipping sauce.
