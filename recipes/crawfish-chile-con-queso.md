## Ingredients

- ¼ cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 cup cooked and peeled whole crawfish tails, chopped, plus more for garnish
- 8 ounces white American cheese, cubed
- 8 ounces Monterey Jack cheese, cubed
- 1 (4 ounce) can diced green chiles
- 1 teaspoon Creole seasoning (such as Tony Chachere's®), or more to taste
- 1 sprig fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Pour heavy cream into a deep skillet over low heat.
2. Add crawfish tails, American cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, green chiles, and Creole seasoning.
3. Cook, stirring every 2 minutes, until cheese is melted, about 15 minutes.
4. Pour queso into a warm bowl.
5. Garnish with cilantro and a few pieces of crawfish tails.
6. Continue to stir queso every 5 to 10 minutes to prevent the cheese from solidifying on top.
