## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) package rotini pasta
- 1 cup frozen petite peas, or more to taste
- 1 (8 ounce) package imitation crabmeat, flaked
- ⅔ cup shredded Swiss cheese
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese, or more to taste
- ⅔ (8 ounce) bottle bottled oil and vinegar salad dressing (such as Newman's Own®), or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook rotini at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
3. Put frozen peas into a large colander.
4. Drain rotini over the peas.
5. Fill the now-empty pot with cold water.
6. Plunge pasta and peas into the cold water to chill completely; drain.
7. Mix imitation crabmeat, Swiss cheese, and parmesan cheese in a large bowl; add pasta mixture and toss to mix.
8. Pour dressing over the salad and toss to lightly coat.
