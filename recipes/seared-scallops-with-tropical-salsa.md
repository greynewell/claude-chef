## Ingredients

- ½ cup diced fresh pineapple
- ½ cup diced fresh mango
- ½ cup peeled and diced cucumber
- ½ cup diced red bell pepper
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 4 teaspoons fresh lime juice
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 pound sea scallops, rinsed and drained

## Instructions

1. Combine the pineapple, mango, cucumber, bell pepper, bell pepper, cilantro, lime juice and jalapeno.
2. Season to taste with salt and pepper, and set aside.
3. Heat a large, nonstick fry pan over medium-high heat.
4. Coat the pan with cooking spray.
5. Season the scallops with salt and pepper.
6. Add half of the scallops to the pan; sear until golden brown on both sides, about 2 minutes per side.
7. Transfer the scallops to a warmed plate; set aside.
8. Repeat with the remaining scallops.
9. Divide the scallops among four warmed plates.
10. Spoon the salsa over the scallops, and serve immediately.
