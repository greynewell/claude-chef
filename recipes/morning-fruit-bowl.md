## Ingredients

- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons coconut oil, melted
- 2 tablespoons agave nectar
- ½ cup rolled oats
- ½ cup toasted walnuts
- 1  mango, chopped
- 1  Gala apple, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chia seeds
- 2 tablespoons hemp seeds

## Instructions

1. Stir yogurt, coconut oil, and agave nectar together in a bowl.
2. Divide yogurt mixture evenly between 2 serving bowls.
3. Layer oats, walnuts, mango, and apple over yogurt; top with chia seeds and hemp seeds.
