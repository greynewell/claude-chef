## Ingredients

- 10  egg whites
- 6  eggs
- 2 teaspoons vegetable oil, or as needed, divided
- ½ (16 ounce) package frozen hash brown potatoes, thawed
- ½ pound ground turkey
- 1 cup chopped red onion
- 6 slices hickory ham (such as Farmland®), diced
- 1 ½ cups shredded fat-free Cheddar cheese
- 10  low-carb, high-fiber tortillas, warmed

## Instructions

1. Whisk egg whites and eggs together in a large bowl.
2. Heat 1 teaspoon oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
3. Cook and stir hash browns until browned and crispy, about 5 minutes.
4. Transfer hash browns to a large bowl.
5. Heat 1 teaspoon oil in the same frying pan over medium heat.
6. Add turkey, onion, and ham.
7. Cook and stir until turkey is crumbly and onion softens, about 5 minutes.
8. Add eggs; stir until scrambled and set, 5 to 7 minutes.
9. Add cheese and let melt, about 3 minutes.
10. Pour mixture into the bowl with the hash browns.
11. Mix everything together.
12. Plate tortillas.
13. Add about 3 tablespoons of filling to each tortilla.
14. Fold opposing edges of a tortilla to overlap the filling.
15. Roll 1 of the opposing edges around the filling, creating a burrito.
16. Repeat for remaining tortillas.
