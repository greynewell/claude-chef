## Ingredients

- 1 ½ quarts olive oil
- ⅓ cup garlic powder
- ⅓ cup dried oregano
- ⅓ cup dried basil
- ¼ cup pepper
- ¼ cup salt
- ¼ cup onion powder
- ¼ cup Dijon-style mustard
- 2 quarts red wine vinegar

## Instructions

1. Mix olive oil, garlic powder, oregano, basil, pepper, salt, onion powder, and Dijon-style mustard together in a very large container.
2. Pour in vinegar slowly while mixing vigorously until well blended. Store tightly covered at room temperature.
