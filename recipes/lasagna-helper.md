## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 4 cups water
- 3  beef bouillon cubes
- 7  dry lasagna noodles, broken into pieces
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed tomato soup
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 ½ tablespoons Italian seasoning
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Cook and stir the ground beef in a large skillet over medium heat until the meat is browned, about 10 minutes; drain excess grease.
2. Pour in the water, bring to a boil, and stir in the bouillon cubes until the cubes are dissolved.
3. Mix in broken lasagna noodles, tomato soup, onion, garlic, Italian seasoning, and black pepper.
4. Reduce heat to a simmer, and cook until the lasagna noodle pieces are tender, about 10 minutes.
5. Remove about 1 cup of sauce from the skillet, and mix with the cornstarch; return mixture to skillet, and simmer to thicken, about 3 minutes.
6. Season to taste with salt.
