## Ingredients

- 2 (10 ounce) cans diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes with chipotle
- ¼  onion, coarsely chopped
- 1 clove garlic
- 1  lime, juiced
- 3 slices pickled jalapeno peppers, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 bunch fresh cilantro leaves

## Instructions

1. Place the canned tomatoes, onion, garlic, lime juice, and optional jalapenos into a blender.
2. Cover, and blend until it reaches the desired consistency.
3. Add cilantro and pulse until combined.
