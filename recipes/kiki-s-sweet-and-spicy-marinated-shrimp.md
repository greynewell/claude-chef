## Ingredients

- 2 cups red wine vinegar
- 1  lemon, sliced
- 2  fresh jalapeno peppers, seeded, deveined, and finely minced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 4 tablespoons dried minced onion flakes
- ¾ cup superfine sugar
- 4 pounds large cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Pour vinegar into a pot over high heat.
2. Stir in lemon slices, jalapeno peppers, garlic, onion flakes, and sugar.
3. Bring to a rapid boil, stirring until sugar is dissolved.
4. Remove from heat, and cool to lukewarm.
5. Place the shrimp and cilantro into a resealable plastic bag.
6. Pour the lukewarm sauce into the bag, seal tightly; refrigerate overnight, turning the bag over several times.
7. Drain liquid from resealable bag, and place shrimp in a large bowl.
8. Season with salt and pepper, and toss well.
