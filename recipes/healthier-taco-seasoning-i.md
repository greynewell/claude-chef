## Ingredients

- 1 clove garlic, peeled
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon paprika
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Crush garlic with salt until it forms a paste.
2. Stir in chili powder, red pepper flakes, oregano, paprika, cumin, and pepper in a small bowl.
3. Alternatively, combine all ingredients in a bowl of a mini-food processor until well mixed.
4. Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator.
