## Ingredients

- Reynolds Wrap® Non Stick Aluminum Foil
- 4 (4 ounce) tilapia fillets, fresh or frozen (thawed)
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 8 (8 inch) flour tortillas
- Shredded lettuce
- Mango salsa
- Sliced avocado

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
2. Line a 13x9x2-inch baking pan with Reynolds Wrap® Non-Stick Foil with non-stick (dull) side toward food.
3. Place tilapia in foil-lined pan.
4. Mix lime juice, olive oil, seafood seasoning, garlic, salt and red pepper flakes in a medium bowl until well blended.
5. Pour over fish.
6. Bake 18 to 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily with fork.
7. Serve 1/2 fillet in each tortilla with lettuce, salsa and avocado.
