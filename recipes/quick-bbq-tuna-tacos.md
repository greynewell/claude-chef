## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can chunk light tuna in water, drained
- 2 tablespoons light sour cream
- 2 tablespoons barbeque sauce (such as Sweet Baby Ray's® Sweet 'n Spicy)
- 1 tablespoon chopped dill pickle
- 1 tablespoon chopped celery
- 3  flour tortillas
- 3 tablespoons sweet corn relish (such as H-E-B Relish that Corn®)

## Instructions

1. Mix tuna, sour cream, barbeque sauce, dill pickle, and celery in a bowl; scoop onto tortillas.
2. Garnish each taco with 1 tablespoon corn relish.
