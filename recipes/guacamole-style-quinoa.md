## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- ½  sweet onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
- 1 cup quinoa
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 ¾ cups chicken stock, or as needed
- 6 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 6  grape tomatoes, quartered
- 1  avocado, cubed
- 1  jalapeno pepper, diced  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir onion and garlic in the hot oil until slightly browned, about 5 minutes.
2. Stir quinoa into onion mixture and cook until quinoa is lightly browned, about 30 seconds.
3. Squeeze lime juice into a 2-cup measuring cup; pour in enough chicken stock to make 2 cups liquid.
4. Pour chicken stock mixture over quinoa and bring to a boil.
5. Reduce heat, cover saucepan, and simmer until liquid is absorbed and quinoa is tender, about 15 minutes.
6. Remove saucepan from heat and chill in the refrigerator, about 1 hour.
7. Fold cilantro, tomatoes, avocado, and jalapeno pepper into quinoa mixture.
8. Return quinoa to refrigerator for flavors to blend or serve immediately.
