## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons peanut butter
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables

## Instructions

1. Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, peanut butter, and garlic powder in a small bowl.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add frozen mixed vegetables; cook and stir until just tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Remove from heat and fold in soy sauce mixture.
