## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 8 ears  corn, unhusked
- 4  limes
- ½ cup crumbled Cotija cheese (queso anejado)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Mix together chili powder, kosher salt, and cayenne in a small bowl.
3. Set mixed seasonings aside.
4. Remove most of the silk while leaving 2 to 3 layers of husk on each ear of corn.
5. Cook corn on the preheated grill until husks are slightly charred, turning every 3 to 4 minutes, for 12 to 14 minutes.
6. Let cool slightly.
7. Remove husks carefully, leaving stems on as handles.
8. Squeeze 1/2 lime over each ear of corn.
9. Sprinkle each with mixed seasonings to taste and top generously with Cotija cheese.
