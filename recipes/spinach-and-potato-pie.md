## Ingredients

- 3 medium potatoes
- 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 8 ounces ricotta cheese
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 4  matzo sheets

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place the potatoes in the preheated oven, and bake for 45 minutes, or until tender.
3. Turn once or twice.
4. Cool, peel and cut into 1/4 inch slices.
5. Reduce the oven temperature to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
6. In a medium bowl, stir together the spinach, green onion, ricotta cheese, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
7. Wet the matzo sheets under warm running water briefly on each side, just until pliable.
8. Place one in the bottom of a 9 inch square baking dish.
9. Spread 1/4 of the ricotta mixture over it, followed by a layer of potato slices.
10. Sprinkle 1/4 of the mozzarella cheese over the potatoes.
11. Repeat the layers, and finish with mozzarella cheese on top.
12. Bake for 35 minutes in the preheated oven, until the cheese on top is bubbly and golden brown.
13. Keep warm until you are ready to serve.
14. Cut into squares.
