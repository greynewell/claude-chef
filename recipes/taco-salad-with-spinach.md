## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup chopped red bell pepper
- ½ cup chopped mushrooms
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 (10 ounce) package spinach leaves
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
- 1 large tomato, chopped

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Cook and stir beef, onion, red bell pepper, mushrooms, salt, and chili powder together in the hot skillet until the beef is completely browned, 7 to 10 minutes; drain and discard grease.
3. Spread spinach leaves into a salad bowl; top with beef mixture.
4. Add black beans and chopped tomato.
