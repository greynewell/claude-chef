## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- ¼ cup tahini paste
- ¼ cup plain yogurt
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon olive oil, for drizzling

## Instructions

1. Combine the black beans, tahini, yogurt, garlic, lemon juice, 1 tablespoon olive oil, and salt in the bowl of a blender or food processor; blend until smooth.
2. Chill 1 hour before serving.
3. Drizzle with 1 teaspoon olive oil to garnish.
