## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh garlic
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- salt and black pepper to taste
- 2 quarts chicken stock
- 1 cup shrimp stock
- 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon hoisin sauce  (Optional)
- 20  peeled and deveined medium shrimp
- 1 (6.75 ounce) package long rice noodles (rice vermicelli)
- 2  green onions, chopped  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot over medium heat.
2. Stir in the garlic and ginger; cook and stir 1 minute.
3. Add the spinach and season with salt and pepper.
4. Cover, and cook until the spinach is hot, about 3 minutes.
5. Pour in the chicken stock, shrimp stock, hot pepper sauce, and hoisin sauce.
6. Recover, and bring to a simmer over medium-high heat.
7. Once the soup reaches a simmer, stir in the shrimp and noodles.
8. Cover, and cook 4 minutes, then stir in the green onions, and cook 5 minutes more.
9. Season to taste with salt and pepper before serving.
