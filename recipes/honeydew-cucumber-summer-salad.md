## Ingredients

- 1  honeydew melon - peeled, seeded, and cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 large cucumber, peeled and cut into bite-sized pieces
- ½ cup diced red onion
- ½ cup sliced almonds
- 1 (4 ounce) container crumbled feta cheese
- ⅓ cup heavy whipping cream
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons poppy seeds

## Instructions

1. Mix honeydew melon, cucumber, red onion, almonds, and feta cheese together in a bowl.
2. Beat cream, olive oil, and poppy seeds in a bowl using an electric mixer until thickened.
3. Stir dressing into salad; refrigerate until flavors blend, 2 hours to overnight.
