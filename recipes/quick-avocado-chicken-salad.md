## Ingredients

- 20 ounces cooked chicken, shredded
- ½ cup plain fat-free Greek yogurt
- ¼ cup finely minced onion
- ¼ cup crumbled cotija cheese
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- ½  jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 pinch salt  (Optional)
- 1 pinch ground cumin

## Instructions

1. Stir chicken, yogurt, onion, cotija cheese, cilantro, and jalapeno pepper together in a large bowl.
2. Gently fold avocado into chicken mixture until salad is evenly distributed.
3. Stir lime juice, salt, and cumin into salad.
