## Ingredients

- ½ (12 ounce) box dry fettuccine pasta
- 1 (12 ounce) can albacore tuna in water, drained and flaked
- 3 medium tomatoes, cut into wedges
- ⅓ cup thinly sliced red onion
- ¼ cup pitted olives, halved
- ½ cup red wine vinegar
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 ½ teaspoons olive oil
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ¼ teaspoon dried basil
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 1 dash ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
2. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
3. Rinse under cold water and drain.
4. Place pasta in a large bowl; add tuna, tomatoes, red onion, and olives.
5. Combine vinegar, garlic, oil, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper in a jar with a tight-fitting lid; shake vinaigrette well.
6. Pour over salad and toss to coat.
7. Cover and refrigerate for 1 to 2 hours.
