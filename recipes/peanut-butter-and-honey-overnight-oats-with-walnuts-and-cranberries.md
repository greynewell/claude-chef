## Ingredients

- 3 cups milk
- 2 ½ cups rolled oats
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- ⅓ cup honey
- ⅓ cup peanut butter
- ⅓ cup flaxseed meal
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 ¼ cups dried cranberries
- 1 ¼ cups chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Combine milk, oats, yogurt, honey, peanut butter, flaxseed meal, vanilla extract, and salt in the bowl of a stand mixer.
2. Mix on low speed until oats are soft, about 30 minutes.
3. Pour mixture into 10 half-pint jars.
4. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons cranberries and 2 tablespoons walnuts into each jar.
5. Refrigerate before serving, 1 to 10 days.
