## Ingredients

- ½ pound ground beef
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1 quart beef broth
- 1 (24 ounce) jar marinara sauce
- ½ cup diced pepperoni
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 ½ tablespoons granulated garlic
- 1 ½ tablespoons dried minced onion
- 1 ½ tablespoons dried oregano
- 4 ounces dried cheese ravioli
- ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Cook and stir beef and onion in the hot skillet until beef is browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease.
3. Stir beef-onion mixture, beef broth, marinara sauce, pepperoni, Parmesan cheese, garlic, minced onion, and oregano together in a large pot; bring to a boil, reduce heat to low, and simmer until flavors blend, about 20 minutes.
4. While soup is simmering, bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
5. Cook the ravioli at a boil, stirring occasionally, until cooked through yet firm to the bite, about 14 minutes; drain.
6. Stir ravioli into soup and cook until heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
7. Ladle soup into bowls and top with mozzarella cheese.
