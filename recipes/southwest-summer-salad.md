## Ingredients

- 2 cups chilled cooked rice
- 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
- 1 large tomato, chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 bunch green onions, sliced
- 1  avocado, diced
- ⅓ cup canola oil
- 3 tablespoons lime juice
- 1 tablespoon lime zest
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  jalapeno pepper, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground oregano
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon chili powder
- 1 head lettuce, chopped

## Instructions

1. Combine rice, chicken, tomato, cilantro, green onions, and avocado together in a bowl.
2. Whisk canola oil, lime juice, lime zest, garlic, jalapeno pepper, salt, oregano, cumin, and chili powder together in a bowl; pour over rice mixture and toss to coat.
3. Chill in refrigerator, about 1 hour.
4. Place lettuce in a serving bowl; top with rice mixture.
