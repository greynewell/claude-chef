## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 4  fresh sardines
- 3 tablespoons sake
- 2  spring onions, finely chopped
- 1 ½ tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 ½ tablespoons mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
- 2 teaspoons chopped ginger

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over high heat.
2. Add sardines; cook, flipping occasionally, until golden, about 3 minutes per side.
3. Transfer to a serving plate.
4. Mix sake, spring onions, soy sauce, mirin, and chopped ginger together in a small bowl to make sauce.
5. Pour over sardines.
