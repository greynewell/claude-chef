## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons canola oil, divided
- 1 ¼ pounds skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into bite-size pieces
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
- 2 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons teriyaki sauce
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1 pound dark sweet cherries, pitted and halved
- 1 ½ cups shredded carrots
- ½ cup chopped green onion
- ⅓ cup toasted and sliced almonds
- 12  lettuce leaves

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Saute chicken and ginger in hot oil until chicken is cooked through, 7 to 10 minutes.
3. Set aside.
4. Whisk vinegar, teriyaki sauce, remaining 1 tablespoon oil, and honey together in a bowl.
5. Add chicken mixture, cherries, carrots, green onion, and almonds; toss to combine.
6. Spoon 1/12 the chicken/cherry mixture onto the center of each lettuce leaf; roll leaf around filling and serve.
