## Ingredients

- ¾ cup white vinegar
- ¾ cup water
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 bunch radishes, cubed
- ½ teaspoon whole mustard seeds
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 1  bay leaf
- 1 pinch coarse sea salt
- 1 pinch furikake (Japanese nori seasoning)  (Optional)
- 1 pinch toasted sesame seeds
- 1  avocado, cut into 1/4-inch dice
- 2 slices whole-grain bread
- 1 bunch sprouted cilantro
- 1  lemon wedge

## Instructions

1. Bring vinegar, water, honey, and salt to a boil in a small saucepan.
2. Remove from heat.
3. Put radishes, mustard seeds, black pepper, and bay leaf in a pint jar with a lid.
4. Pour hot vinegar mixture over the radishes.
5. Let the radishes come to room temperature.
6. Cover with lid and place in the refrigerator for at least 2 hours.
7. Mix coarse sea salt, furikake, and sesame seeds together in a small bowl to create the seasoning mix.
8. Toast bread until lightly brown, about 2 minutes.
9. Top each slice with half the avocado.
10. Top avocado with a portion of the pickled radishes.
11. Sprinkle half the seasoning mix over radishes.
12. Place a small handful of cilantro on top.
13. Squeeze lemon over the toast.
