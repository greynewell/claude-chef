## Ingredients

- ½  chipotle chile in adobo sauce
- 1 teaspoon adobo sauce
- ½  fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded
- 1 cup raspberry preserves

## Instructions

1. Place the chipotle chile, adobo sauce, jalapeno pepper, and raspberry preserves in a food processor.
2. Pulse until peppers are chopped and sauce is smooth, about 5 or 6 pulses.
3. Refrigerate until ready to use.
