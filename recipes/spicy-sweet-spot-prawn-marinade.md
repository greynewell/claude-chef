## Ingredients

- 3 ½ fluid ounces canola oil
- 1 ¾ fluid ounces mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
- 1 ½ teaspoons red pepper flakes
- 2 large garlic cloves, crushed
- 1  lemon, zested
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 24  spot prawns

## Instructions

1. Stir canola oil, mirin, pepper flakes, garlic, lemon zest, and salt together in a small bowl.
2. Place prawns in a resealable plastic bag; pour in marinade.
3. Press out excess air; seal the bag.
4. Marinate prawns in the refrigerator for at least 1 hour.
5. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
6. Thread prawns onto skewers; discard marinade.
7. Grill prawns until opaque, about 3 minutes each side.
