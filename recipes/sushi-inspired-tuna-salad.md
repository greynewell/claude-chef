## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can solid white tuna packed in water, drained
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon wasabi paste, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
- 1 tablespoon minced green onion

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together the tuna, mayonnaise, wasabi paste, ginger and green onion.
2. Serve on lettuce leaves or make a sandwich with white bread.
