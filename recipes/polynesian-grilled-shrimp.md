## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup mango nectar
- 2 ½ tablespoons lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger
- 2 teaspoons sweet chili sauce
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 24  (21 to 25 per lb) large shrimp, peeled and deveined, tails left on

## Instructions

1. Whisk together mango nectar, lemon juice, honey, soy sauce, oil, garlic, ginger, chili sauce, and cayenne in a bowl.
2. Add shrimp, turning to coat, and marinate, covered with plastic wrap, 30 minutes at room temperature or chilled up to 4 hours.
3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, 375 to 450 degrees F (200 to 230 degrees C ).
4. Lightly oil the grate.
5. Lift shrimp from marinade, shaking off excess.
6. Grill shrimp until just cooked through, about 1 minute per side.
