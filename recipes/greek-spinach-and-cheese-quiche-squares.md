## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 3  eggs
- 1 cup flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 teaspoon MAGIC Baking Powder
- 2 (300 g) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed, squeezed dry
- 2 cups CRACKER BARREL Crumbled Feta Cheese with Oregano, Sun Dried Tomatoes and Black Pepper
- 2 cups CRACKER BARREL Shredded Old Cheddar Cheese
- 1 tablespoon dried minced onion

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C).
2. Bake butter in 9-inch square baking dish 2 minutes or until melted.
3. Remove dish from oven; brush butter onto bottom and sides of dish.
4. Beat eggs with mixer in large bowl.
5. Add flour, milk and baking powder; mix until blended.
6. Stir in spinach, cheeses and onion.
7. Spoon into prepared dish.
8. Bake 35 minutes.
9. Let stand 15 minutes before cutting into squares.
