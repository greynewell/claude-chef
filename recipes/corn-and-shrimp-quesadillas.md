## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons butter
- 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- ½ cup 14 Hands Chardonnay
- 24  shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ¼ cup parsley, chopped
- 1  lemon, juiced
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 12  corn tortillas
- 1 cup mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 1 cup corn
- ½ cup green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a pan over medium-high heat.
2. Add the garlic and cook for 2 minutes while stirring.
3. Add wine and cook for 3 minutes.
4. Add the shrimp and cook each side for 3 minutes.
5. Take off the heat and add the parsley and lemon juice.
6. Salt and pepper to taste.
7. Put cooked shrimp in a bowl and set aside.
8. Warm up tortillas in the microwave.
9. Place 6 tortillas on a work surface to start building quesadillas.
10. Evenly divide cheese and corn between tortillas; place 4 shrimp over each.
11. Sprinkle green onions on top and cover with another tortilla.
12. Cook in a nonstick pan, flipping the tortillas over until each side is lightly brown, 4 to 5 minutes per quesadilla.
