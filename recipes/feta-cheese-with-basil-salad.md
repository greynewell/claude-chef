## Ingredients

- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- 1 pint cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1 bunch fresh basil leaves, sliced
- 1 bunch green onions, sliced
- 1  avocado, peeled and diced
- 1 (6 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese with basil and sun-dried tomatoes
- ½ cup kalamata olives, pitted and chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and diced
- garlic salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the walnuts in a small skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook, stirring constantly, until golden brown.
3. In a bowl, gently mix the walnuts, tomatoes, basil, green onions, avocado, feta cheese with basil and sun-dried tomatoes, kalamata olives, and red bell pepper.
4. Season with garlic salt, and drizzle with balsamic vinegar and extra-virgin olive oil.
5. Allow to sit about 15 minutes, and toss again just before serving.
