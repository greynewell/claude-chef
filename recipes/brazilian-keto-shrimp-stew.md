## Ingredients

- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup onion, diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup diced roasted red peppers
- 1 (14 ounce) can diced tomatoes with green chile peppers
- 1 ½ pounds raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 cup coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons sriracha sauce
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a medium-sized saucepan.
2. Add onion and cook until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Add garlic and red peppers; cook 2 to 3 minutes more.
4. Add tomatoes, shrimp, and cilantro and simmer gently until shrimp are opaque, about 5 minutes.
5. Pour coconut milk into the saucepan, add sriracha sauce, and cook until just heated through without boiling.
6. Add lime juice and season with salt and black pepper to taste.
