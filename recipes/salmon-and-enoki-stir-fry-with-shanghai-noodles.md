## Ingredients

- 1 large lemon, zested and juiced
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon grated ginger
- 3 cloves crushed garlic
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper to taste
- ½ pound Atlantic salmon - skinless, boneless, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 3 cups vegetable stock
- 1 tablespoon walnut oil, or as needed
- 2 medium carrots, thinly sliced on the diagonal
- 3 stalks celery, thinly sliced on the diagonal
- 1 medium onion, halved lengthwise and thinly sliced
- 16 ounces fresh Shanghai-style noodles
- 1 ½ cups bean sprouts
- 1 cup enoki mushrooms, roots removed and stalks separated
- 3  green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Combine lemon zest, lemon juice, honey, soy sauce, grated ginger, crushed garlic, sesame oil, cayenne pepper, and black pepper in a small, high-sided bowl.
2. Whisk marinade to combine.
3. Place salmon in a bowl; just before starting to cook, pour 1/2 the marinade over the fish.
4. Pour stock into a large pot and bring to a simmer.
5. Heat oil in a wok over medium-high heat.
6. Add carrots and stir-fry for 3 minutes.
7. Add 1 tablespoon of stock.
8. Add celery, onion, and an additional tablespoon or 2 of stock.
9. Stir-fry until starting to soften but still crisp, about 4 minutes.
10. Transfer vegetables to a warm bowl.
11. Pour salmon and marinade into the wok.
12. Increase heat to high and cook for 2 minutes.
13. Return vegetables to the wok and reduce heat to low.
14. Cook and flip the contents of the wok to gently, without stirring.
15. Add a small amount of stock and cook 2 minutes more.
16. Add noodles to the pot with the simmering stock; do not break them apart.
17. Add bean sprouts, mushrooms, and remaining marinade to the wok.
18. Simmer and flip to combine, 4 to 6 minutes more.
19. Serve salmon stir-fry over cooked noodles; garnish with green onions.
