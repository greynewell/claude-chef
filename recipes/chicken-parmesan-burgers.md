## Ingredients

- 4 small skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cubed
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 cup bread crumbs
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ cup butter, divided
- 4 slices bread
- ⅓ cup chopped onion
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 4  mushrooms, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 (8 ounce) can spaghetti sauce

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Combine chicken breasts, parsley, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper in a food processor; process into a smooth paste.
3. Shape chicken mixture into 2 round patties.
4. Mix bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese together in a shallow bowl.
5. Dredge patties to coat both sides.
6. Arrange on an ungreased baking sheet.
7. Spread 1 1/2 teaspoon butter over each slice of bread; sprinkle remaining bread crumb-Parmesan mixture on top.
8. Arrange near the chicken patties on the baking sheet.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until bread is browned, 10 to 15 minutes.
10. Transfer bread to a serving plate.
11. Continue baking chicken until an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center reads 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), 10 to 15 minutes more.
12. Melt remaining 2 tablespoons butter in a saucepan.
13. Cook and stir onion and garlic in the butter until onion is translucent, about 5 minutes.
14. Add mushrooms and flour; cook and stir until starting to brown, about 5 minutes.
15. Stir in spaghetti sauce; simmer until thickened, 5 to 10 minutes.
16. Spoon sauce over 2 bread slices.
17. Place a chicken patty in the center; cover with remaining bread slices.
