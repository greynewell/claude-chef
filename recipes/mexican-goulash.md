## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- ½  onion, chopped
- 1 (12 ounce) package elbow macaroni
- ½ pound processed cheese (such as Velveeta®), diced
- 1 ½ cups salsa
- 1 (15 ounce) can pinto beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 (14 ounce) can whole kernel corn, drained

## Instructions

1. Cook the ground beef and onion into a large skillet over medium heat until browned and crumbly, about 10 minutes.
2. Break the meat apart as it cooks.
3. Drain the excess grease.
4. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.
5. Once the water is boiling, stir in the macaroni, and return to a boil.
6. Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta has cooked through, but is still firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
7. Drain well in a colander set in the sink.
8. Add the macaroni to the the beef and onion and place over medium-low heat; stir in the processed cheese until melted, about 3 minutes.
9. Add the salsa, pinto beans, and corn to the mixture; stir and heat until the mixture bubbles slightly, another 5 minutes.
