## Ingredients

- 2  avocados - peeled, pitted and diced
- ¾ cup sour cream
- ¾ cup salsa
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 dash ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mash the avocados with a potato masher.
2. Mix in the sour cream, salsa, cumin, garlic powder, salt and pepper.
3. Chill at least 30 minutes before serving.
