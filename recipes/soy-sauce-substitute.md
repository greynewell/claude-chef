## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons beef bouillon
- 4 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 teaspoons dark molasses
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 pinch white pepper
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- 1 ½ cups water

## Instructions

1. Stir water, beef bouillon, balsamic vinegar, molasses, ginger, white pepper, and garlic powder in a saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil and simmer until liquid is reduced to about 1 cup, about 10 minutes.
2. Store in the refrigerator and shake before using.
