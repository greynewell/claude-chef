## Ingredients

- 2 cups fresh basil leaves
- 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
- ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ cup pine nuts, toasted
- 1  artichoke heart, roughly chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped oil-packed sun-dried tomatoes
- ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 pinch salt and black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a food processor, combine the basil, feta cheese, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, artichoke heart, and sun-dried tomatoes.
2. Cover, and pulse, adding oil as needed to facilitate blending until smooth.
3. Taste, and season with salt and pepper.
