## Ingredients

- 30  large shrimp, peeled and deveined, or more to taste
- 1 drizzle olive oil
- 1  ripe tomato
- 1 small jalapeno pepper, stem removed
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro, divided
- 2 cloves garlic
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 tablespoon hot pepper sauce
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 package mini skewers

## Instructions

1. Place shrimp into a bowl.
2. Drizzle with olive oil and set aside.
3. Blend tomato, jalapeno pepper, 1/2 the cilantro, garlic, lime juice, and hot sauce together in a blender until smooth with pieces of cilantro still visible.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Add 1/2 the remaining cilantro to mixture and stir to combine.
6. Pour 1/2 the sauce over the oil-drizzled shrimp.
7. Let stand and marinate in the refrigerator, 20 to 30 minutes.
8. Remove shrimp from the marinade on thread onto mini skewers.
9. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
10. Pour remaining sauce into a small saucepan over medium heat.
11. Bring to a boil, about 5 minutes.
12. Remove from heat and pour into small serving dishes.
13. Garnish with remaining cilantro.
14. Grill shrimp kabobs on the preheated grill until they are bright pink on the outside and the meat is opaque, making sure not to overcook, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
15. Place cooked shrimp into a bowl on on a serving platter.
16. Align mini skewers so guests can dip shrimp into prepared sauce.
