## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons white vinegar
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons garlic paste, divided
- 1 teaspoon ginger paste
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- 3  potatoes, cut into fries
- oil for frying
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 8  pita breads, split
- 1 cup pickled cucumbers

## Instructions

1. Mix vinegar, soy sauce, 1 teaspoon garlic paste, ginger paste, salt, and pepper together in a shallow dish.
2. Add chicken breasts; turn to coat.
3. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
4. Heat 2 inches oil in a heavy pot to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
5. Fry potatoes in batches until golden brown, about 5 minutes.
6. Transfer to paper towels to drain.
7. Heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
8. Remove chicken breasts from the marinade and cook until browned and no longer pink in the center, 6 to 8 minutes per side.
9. Transfer to a cutting board; slice into thin strips.
10. Mix remaining 1 teaspoon garlic paste with mayonnaise in a bowl.
11. Spread garlic mayonnaise inside pita breads.
12. Fill each pita with French fries, chicken slices, and pickles.
