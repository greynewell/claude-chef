## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 3 bunches green onions, chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa
- 2  tomatoes, diced
- 2 (6 ounce) cans sliced black olives, drained
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. In a medium serving dish, mix the cream cheese, sour cream and green onions.
2. Top with salsa, and layer with tomatoes, black olives and Cheddar cheese.
