## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) container Shamrock Farms® Original Cottage Cheese
- 1 bunch green onions
- 3  hot yellow chile peppers
- 2  jalapeno peppers
- 1 bunch cilantro
- 8  firm, ripe tomatoes
- 3 cloves garlic, chopped
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Chop onions, peppers, cilantro, and tomatoes by hand. Place in a large bowl; stir in garlic and salt.
2. Mix in Shamrock Farms Cottage Cheese.
