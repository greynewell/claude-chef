## Ingredients

- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ cup plain low-fat yogurt
- ½ cup cucumber, peeled, grated and squeezed as dry as possible
- 2 teaspoons red or rice wine vinegar
- 2  garlic cloves, minced
- ½ teaspoon Salt and pepper, to taste
- 4 large pitas
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 large onion, peeled, halved and cut into chunky wedges
- 3 cups leftover roast turkey, pulled into bite-sized pieces
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- 1 ½ cups shredded lettuce (preferably romaine)
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved and lightly salted

## Instructions

1. Adjust oven rack to middle position.
2. Heat oven to 300 degrees.
3. In a small bowl, mix sour cream, yogurt, cucumber, vinegar, 1 minced garlic clove, and salt and pepper to taste; set aside.
4. Place pitas in oven; bake until warm and pliable, 7 minutes.
5. Cut in half.
6. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet over high heat.
7. Carefully add onion; saute until spotty brown but still crisp, 2 to 3 minutes.
8. Add turkey, oregano and remaining minced garlic; continue to saute until heated through, another 2 minutes.
9. Serve, letting guests fill their own pitas with lettuce first, followed by turkey, tomatoes and cucumber sauce.
