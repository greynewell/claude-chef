## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) package mixed baby greens
- 1  pomegranate, peeled and seeds separated
- 1 (8 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the lettuce, pomegranate seeds, and feta cheese into a large mixing bowl; set aside.
2. Whisk together the lemon juice and zest, mustard, vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a separate bowl.
3. Pour over the salad and toss to coat.
4. Serve immediately.
