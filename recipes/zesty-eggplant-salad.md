## Ingredients

- 2  onion, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 1 large large eggplant, peeled and sliced into long strips
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
- 2 large pita bread rounds, cut into 1-inch squares
- 2  tomatoes, cut into thin strips
- 1 large green bell pepper, sliced into thin strips
- ½ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup sumac powder
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Place onion in a bowl and add vinegar and 1 tablespoon salt.
2. Let onion soak for 1 hour.
3. Set oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
4. Arrange eggplant on a baking sheet and brush with 1 tablespoon olive oil until evenly coated.
5. Broil eggplant until flesh is pink, about 5 minutes.
6. Flip eggplant and broil until other side is pink, about 5 minutes more.
7. Cool eggplant.
8. Reduce oven's temperature to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
9. Spread pita bread on a baking sheet.
10. Cook pita bread in preheated oven until toasted and crispy, about 10 minutes.
11. Combine tomatoes and green bell pepper in a large bowl; add eggplant and onion with vinegar and salt.
12. Mix olive oil and sumac into tomato mixture; season with salt.
13. Toss pita bread pieces into salad just before serving.
