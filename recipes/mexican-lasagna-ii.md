## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- 2 (16 ounce) cans refried beans
- 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2  green onions, chopped
- 2  roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. In large skillet over medium heat, cook beef until browned.
3. Drain.
4. Combine with   and refried beans.
5. Spread half of mixture in 9x13 inch baking dish.
6. Top with two tortillas, trimming if necessary, and half of cheese.
7. Repeat layers.
8. Bake 35 to 45 minutes until heated through and cheese is bubbly.
9. Top with green onions and tomatoes.
10. Let cool 5 minutes before serving.
