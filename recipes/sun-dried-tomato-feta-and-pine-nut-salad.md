## Ingredients

- 1 loaf Italian bread, cubed
- 3 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- ½ teaspoon seasoned salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ cup pine nuts
- 1 cup sun-dried tomatoes
- 2 (16 ounce) packages mixed salad greens
- 4  green onions, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
- 8 ounces feta cheese, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Preheat broiler.
2. Toss 3 cups cubed Italian bread with olive oil.
3. Season with seasoned salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
4. Spread out in a single layer in a well oiled 9x13 inch baking dish.
5. Broil until toasted, turning to brown evenly.
6. Set croutons aside to cool.
7. Spread pine nuts on a baking sheet, place on bottom rack  of the oven, and toast under broiler.
8. This should only take a few minutes, so watch carefully to prevent burning.
9. Set aside to cool.
10. Soak sun-dried tomatoes in hot water for 5 to 10 minutes, or until soft.
11. Drain, and slice.
12. In a large salad bowl, toss mixed greens with green onions.
13. Top with croutons, sun-dried tomatoes, pine nuts, and feta.
