## Ingredients

- 1 cup dried soybeans
- hot water to cover
- 2 ½ cups water
- sea salt to taste
- 1 (9 ounce) package Korean noodles
- 2  tomato wedges
- 1  cucumber, thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoon black sesame seeds, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place soybeans in a bowl; add enough hot water to cover.
2. Soak soybeans for 1 to 2 hours; drain.
3. Transfer soybeans to a pot and add enough water to cover; bring to a boil.
4. Drain and rinse well under cold water.
5. Bring a pot of water to a boil; add noodles and cook until tender yet firm to the bite, 3 to 4 minutes.
6. Drain.
7. Combine soybeans and 2 1/2 cups water in a blender; blend until smooth.
8. Add water or strain water until desired consistency is reached.
9. Place noodles in a bowl and top with pureed soybeans, tomato, cucumber, and sesame seeds; season with salt.
