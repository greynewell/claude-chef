## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- ½ pound seashell pasta
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  cream of onion soup
- 1 slice shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup milk
- ½ cup reduced-fat mayonnaise
- 6 tablespoons butter, divided
- 4 cups chopped fresh broccoli
- 1 pound imitation crabmeat, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- ½ cup fine dry bread crumbs
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
3. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
4. Stir in pasta and return to a boil.
5. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 9 to 11 minutes.
6. Drain.
7. Combine cream of onion soup, Cheddar cheese, milk, and mayonnaise in a large bowl.
8. Heat 1/4 cup butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
9. Add broccoli; cook and stir until just tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
10. Transfer broccoli to onion soup mixture; stir in pasta, imitation crabmeat, and tomatoes.
11. Spread mixture into prepared baking dish.
12. Mix bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, and remaining butter together in a small bowl; sprinkle over crabmeat mixture.
13. Bake in the preheated oven until brown and bubbly, 35 to 45 minutes.
