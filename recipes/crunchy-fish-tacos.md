## Ingredients

- 9 each Old El Paso® taco shells
- 2 teaspoons Old El Paso® taco seasoning mix
- 1 pound tilapia fillets
- 8 (6 inch) Old El Paso® flour tortillas for soft tacos & fajitas
- 1 cup pico de gallo salsa
- ½ cup sour cream

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F.
2. Line cookie sheet with foil.
3. Place taco shells in 1-gallon resealable food-storage plastic bag.
4. Seal bag; crush shells with rolling pin.
5. Add   mix; shake until well mixed.
6. Cut tilapia fillets into 3x1-inch pieces.
7. Add fillet pieces to bag, pressing crumbs onto pieces to coat.
8. Place on cookie sheet.
9. Bake 15 minutes, turning halfway through bake time.
10. Place 2 pieces on each tortilla.
11. Add toppings.
12. Roll up tortillas.
