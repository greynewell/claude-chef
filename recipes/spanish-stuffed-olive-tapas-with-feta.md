## Ingredients

- 30  blanched almonds
- 1 (4 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
- ½  onion, minced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 30  green olives, pitted

## Instructions

1. Toast almonds in a skillet over medium heat, stirring constantly, until lightly browned, about 3 minutes.
2. Allow to cool, about 15 minutes.
3. Combine feta cheese, onion, and garlic in a small bowl.
4. Add olive oil and mix into a smooth paste.
5. Spoon a little bit of feta paste into each olive and stick an almond inside.
6. Cut very large almonds in half.
7. Refrigerate before serving, 8 hours to overnight.
8. Serve with toothpicks stuck into the olives.
