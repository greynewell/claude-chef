## Ingredients

- ½ cup Marie's® Garlic Parmesan Italian Vinaigrette
- 2 pounds cooked seafood (bay scallops and small shrimp)
- 1  red onion, chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- 1  yellow bell pepper, chopped
- 2 tablespoons cilantro, chopped, or more to taste
- Salt and pepper
- 1 (12 ounce) bag prepared assorted greens

## Instructions

1. Combine the first six ingredients and mix thoroughly. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper. Lay assorted greens onto a plate and place shrimp and scallop mixture on top. Garnish with fresh chopped cilantro if desired.
2. Serve chilled with additional Marie's Garlic Parmesan Italian Vinaigrette on the side.
