## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package pork breakfast sausage
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or as needed
- ½ cup milk
- 1 (16 ounce) package egg roll wrappers
- 2 cups spaghetti sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Cook and stir sausage until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Transfer sausage to a bowl.
4. Wipe out skillet.
5. Pour milk into a small bowl.
6. Place 1 egg roll wrapper on a plate; top with 1 tablespoon each cooked sausage, spaghetti sauce, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
7. Brush edges of wrapper with a light coating of milk.
8. Fold sides over filling as if wrapping a small burrito.
9. Repeat with remaining wrappers, sausage, spaghetti sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
10. Heat the same skillet over medium heat; lightly coat with vegetable oil.
11. Place 3 to 4 pizza rolls in the hot skillet.
12. Cook until the rolls are browned on the bottom, about 1 minute; flip and cook 1 minute more.
13. Repeat with remaining rolls, adding more oil to the skillet as necessary.
