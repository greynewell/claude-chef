## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup corn kernels
- 3  roma (plum) tomatoes, seeded and chopped
- ½  green bell pepper, chopped
- ½ cup salsa
- ⅓ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- ¼ cup lime juice
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder

## Instructions

1. Mix black beans, garbanzo beans, corn, tomatoes, bell pepper, salsa, cilantro, lime juice, jalapeno pepper, salt, garlic powder, black pepper, cumin, and onion powder together in a bowl.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
