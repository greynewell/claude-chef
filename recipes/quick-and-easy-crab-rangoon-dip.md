## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
- 1 clove garlic, peeled and crushed
- ½ teaspoon dried parsley
- 1 pound cooked crabmeat
- 1 (14 ounce) package wonton wrappers
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 (7 ounce) jar duck sauce

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a large baking sheet.
3. In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese, soy sauce, fresh ginger root, garlic, parsley and crabmeat.
4. Cut wonton wrappers diagonally into triangles.
5. Arrange triangles on the prepared baking sheet in a single layer.
6. Sprinkle with paprika, increasing the amount as desired.
7. Bake wrappers in the preheated oven until crisp and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
8. Pour approximately 1/2 the duck sauce over cream cheese mixture.
9. Serve the rest with the toasted won ton wrapper triangles for dipping.
