## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen bite-sized potato nuggets (such as Tater Tots®)
- ¼ cup Buffalo wing sauce (such as Frank's® RedHot®)
- 1/4 cup vegan ranch salad dressing for dipping

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place frozen potato nuggets in the basket of the air fryer. Cook for 8 minutes, shaking halfway through the cook time.
3. Transfer potatoes to a large bowl. Drizzle wing sauce over top and toss until evenly combined. Return to the air fryer basket.
4. Cook for 10 minutes undisturbed. Transfer to a serving plate and serve with ranch dressing for dipping.
