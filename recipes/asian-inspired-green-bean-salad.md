## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 slices fresh ginger root, minced
- 1  dried red chile pepper
- ¾ pound chopped fresh green beans
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons oyster sauce

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
2. Stir in garlic, ginger, and chile pepper.
3. As the garlic and ginger begin to sweat, add beans, toss to mix, cover, and reduce heat.
4. Steam for 5 to 8 minutes.
5. Add 1/4 cup of water if necessary.
6. Remove cover, increase heat to high; add soy and oyster sauce and stir until sauce thickens, about 2 minutes more.
7. Serve warm.
