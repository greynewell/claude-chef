## Ingredients

- 1  cucumber, chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, cut into thin strips
- ½  red onion, roughly chopped
- ¼ cup rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce
- 2 bunches watercress, stems removed

## Instructions

1. Combine cucumber, red bell pepper, red onion, rice vinegar, sesame oil, sugar, soy sauce, red pepper flakes, and hot pepper sauce in a bowl.
2. Place watercress on a serving platter or bowl. Pour cucumber mixture over watercress.
