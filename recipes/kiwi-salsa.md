## Ingredients

- 6  kiwis, peeled and diced
- 1 small onion, diced
- 1  jalapeno pepper, diced
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon honey
- ½ teaspoon cumin
- ½ teaspoon curry powder

## Instructions

1. Mix kiwi, onion, jalapeno pepper, lime juice, olive oil, honey, cumin, and curry powder together in bowl.
2. Cover and allow to rest for 1 hour at room temperature.
3. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
