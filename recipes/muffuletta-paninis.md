## Ingredients

- 1 cup pimento-stuffed green olives, drained
- ½ cup chopped Kalamata olives
- ¼ cup diced red onions
- ¼  lemon, juiced
- 2  marinated artichoke hearts, drained
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh basil
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons capers, drained
- ½ teaspoon roasted garlic
- cooking spray
- 8 slices provolone cheese, or to taste
- 8 thin slices  ham, or to taste
- 8 slices Genoa salami, or to taste
- 1 loaf crusty Italian bread, sliced

## Instructions

1. Place stuffed olives, Kalamata olives, red onions, lemon juice, artichoke hearts, basil, olive oil, capers, and garlic in a food processor.
2. Pulse until coarsely chopped.
3. Refrigerate olive salad 8 hours to overnight.
4. Spray a panini press with cooking spray and preheat the grill.
5. Layer 1 slice provolone cheese, 1 spoonful olive salad, 2 slices ham, 2 slices salami, another spoonful olive salad, and 1 additional slice provolone cheese on 4 slices bread; top with 4 additional slices to create 4 sandwiches.
6. Place sandwiches in the preheated panini press and grill until bread is toasted, 3 to 5 minutes each.
7. Cut sandwiches in half.
