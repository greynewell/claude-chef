## Ingredients

- 4 cups chopped tomatoes
- 2 cups green bell pepper, chopped
- ¾ cup chopped onion
- 1 cup jalapeno pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 ¼ cups cider vinegar

## Instructions

1. Place tomatoes, bell peppers, onion, hot peppers, salt, garlic, and vinegar in a saucepan or pot Bring mixture to a simmer.
2. Cover, and let simmer 50 to 60 minutes.
3. The longer the salsa simmers, the spicier it will become.
