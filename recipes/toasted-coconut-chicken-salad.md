## Ingredients

- ¼ cup unsweetened flaked coconut
- ¼ cup slivered almonds
- 6 ounces plain non-dairy yogurt or Greek yogurt
- ⅓ cup Silk® Unsweetened Coconutmilk
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 pound cooked chicken breast, cubed
- 1 medium apple, cored and chopped
- 4 stalks celery, sliced
- ¾ cup green or red seedless grapes, halved
- ¼ cup thinly sliced scallions
- Salt and pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Lightly toast coconut and almonds for about 5 minutes.
3. Check often--coconut may brown first.
4. Set aside.
5. Whisk together yogurt, Silk, garlic powder, lemon juice, cider vinegar and mustard in a large bowl.
6. Add remaining ingredients (except toasted coconut and almonds), tossing to combine.
7. Top with toasted coconut and almonds to serve.
