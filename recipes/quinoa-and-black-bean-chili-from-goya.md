## Ingredients

- 1 cup GOYA® Organic Quinoa
- 1 tablespoon GOYA® Extra Virgin Olive Oil
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon GOYA® Minced Garlic
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 tablespoon GOYA® Ground Cumin
- 1 teaspoon GOYA® Oregano Leaves
- 1 (8 ounce) can GOYA® Tomato Sauce
- 2 (15.5 ounce) cans GOYA® Low Sodium Black Beans, drained and rinsed
- ½ large green bell pepper, seeded and diced
- ½ large red bell pepper, seeded and diced
- 1 tablespoon GOYA® Chipotle Peppers, in Adobo Sauce, finely chopped
- 1 (15.5 ounce) can GOYA® Corn Kernels, drained and rinsed
- Fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. In a medium pot, bring quinoa, 1 1/2 cups water and 1/2 of olive oil to a boil.
2. Reduce heat, cover and let simmer for 20 minutes, until water is absorbed and quinoa is tender.
3. Set aside.
4. Meanwhile, in a large pot, heat remaining olive oil on medium-high heat.
5. Add onion and garlic, and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
6. Add chili powder, cumin and oregano, and stir for about 1 minute.
7. Stir in tomato sauce, black beans, green and red peppers, chipotle peppers and 1 cup water.
8. Bring to a boil; then reduce heat, cover and simmer for 20 minutes.
9. After 20 minutes, stir in reserved quinoa and corn.
10. Cook for another 5 minutes.
11. Serve and top with fresh cilantro sprigs.
