## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 3 stalks celery, minced
- 1 small onion, minced
- ½ teaspoon minced garlic
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons curry powder
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 1 (14 ounce) can coconut milk
- 1 cup creamy peanut butter
- ½ cup chopped peanuts

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter in a large sauce pan over medium heat.
2. Cook the celery and onion in the butter, stirring frequently, for 5 minutes.
3. Add the garlic and cook another 2 minutes.
4. Stir in the flour, curry powder, and red pepper; cook and stir 1 minutes.
5. Pour in the chicken broth and bring to a boil.
6. Reduce heat to low and simmer 20 minutes, stirring frequently.
7. Mix in the coconut milk and peanut butter; continue to heat while stirring until completely heated through.
8. Remove from heat.
9. Pour the soup in small batches into a blender or food processor and blend until smooth.
10. Pour into bowls and garnish with chopped peanuts to serve.
