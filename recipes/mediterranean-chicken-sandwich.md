## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves , cut into cubes
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1  yellow bell pepper, diced
- 1  onion, diced
- ¼ cup pitted kalamata olives, diced
- ¼ cup capers, drained
- 1 cup halved cherry tomatoes
- 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 4  Italian-style hoagie buns, split lengthwise and toasted
- 1/4 cup artichoke aioli (see footnote for recipe link)
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken, olive oil, garlic, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl; toss to coat.
2. Heat a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
3. Add chicken mixture and cook, stirring frequently, until chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes.
4. Add yellow bell pepper and onion; cook until onion begins to soften, about 5 minutes.
5. Mix in olives and capers until combined.
6. Stir in mozzarella cheese until melted.
7. Remove from heat and stir in feta cheese.
8. Spread each hoagie roll with 1 tablespoon artichoke aioli.
9. Evenly distribute the chicken mixture among the 4 sandwiches.
10. Sprinkle each sandwich with 1 tablespoon basil.
