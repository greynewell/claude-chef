## Ingredients

- 12 ounces raw shrimp (26 to 30 count),  peeled and deveined
- 1 teaspoon Cajun blackened seasoning, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

## Instructions

1. Place shrimp in a bowl. Season with blackened seasoning.
2. Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium-high heat. Cook shrimp until opaque, about 1 minute per side.
