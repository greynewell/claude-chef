## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups self-rising flour, plus more for kneading
- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
- cooking spray

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C).
2. Mix flour and Greek yogurt together in a bowl; transfer to a work surface floured with self-rising flour. Knead dough, adding more flour as needed to keep dough from being too sticky, for 8 to 10 minutes.
3. Spray a 12-inch pizza pan with cooking spray and spread dough to edges of pan.
4. Parbake the pizza crust for 5 to 7 minutes before adding your favorite toppings.
5. Return to the oven and bake until crust is lightly browned, 5 to 7 minutes.
