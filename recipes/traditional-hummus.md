## Ingredients

- 3 cloves garlic, peeled
- 2 (15 ounce) cans garbanzo beans, drained
- 1 cup olive oil
- 1 cup tahini
- 3  lemons, juiced
- salt to taste
- ¼ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Place garlic in a food processor and chop thoroughly.
2. Add garbanzo beans and blend into a paste.
3. Pour in 1 cup olive oil, tahini, lemon juice, and salt.
4. Blend until smooth and creamy.
5. Place hummus in a bowl and top with 1/4 cup olive oil.
