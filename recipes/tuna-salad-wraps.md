## Ingredients

- ½ cup light mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons lime juice
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped, seeded fresh jalapeno pepper, or more to taste
- 1 (6 ounce) can water-packed, chunk light tuna, drained
- 6 (7 inch) flour tortillas
- 2 cups thinly sliced iceberg lettuce
- ½ cup shredded Mexican blend cheese
- 1  plum tomato, seeded and diced, or more to taste
- 1  avocado, diced  (Optional)
- 1  green onion, diced  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine mayonnaise, lime juice, and jalapeno pepper in a medium bowl; mix well. Stir in tuna.
2. Spread tuna mixture to within 1/4 inch of the edge of each tortilla. Top with lettuce, cheese, and tomato; press lightly to flatten a bit. Top with avocado, green onion, and cilantro. Roll up each tortilla tightly or fold in half. Serve immediately.
