## Ingredients

- 1 (3 ounce) package ramen noodles
- ½ cup peanut oil
- 3 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 ½ tablespoons white sugar
- ½ cup diced cooked chicken breast meat
- 3  green onions, minced

## Instructions

1. Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan; add noodles and cook, stirring occasionally, for 3 minutes. Drain.
2. Mix peanut oil, rice vinegar, soy sauce, and sugar together in a bowl.
3. Toss dressing with chicken, noodles, and green onions. Chill for at least 15 minutes before serving.
