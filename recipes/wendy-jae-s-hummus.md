## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons chopped roasted garlic
- ¼ cup chopped red onion
- 2 (15 ounce) cans garbanzo beans, drained
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried sage
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried oregano
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried parsley
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. In a food processor or blender, pulse garlic and onions until finely chopped.
2. Add garbanzo beans, one can at a time, and pulse to puree.
3. Blend in sage, basil, oregano, parsley, lemon juice and oil; puree until smooth.
