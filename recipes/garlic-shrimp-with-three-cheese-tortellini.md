## Ingredients

- 1 (9 ounce) package BUITONI® Refrigerated Three Cheese Tortellini
- 6  raw large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 teaspoons finely chopped garlic
- ½ cup BUITONI® Refrigerated All Natural Pesto with Basil
- ½ cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- 2 tablespoons BUITONI® Refrigerated Freshly Shredded Parmesan Cheese

## Instructions

1. Prepare pasta according to package directions.
2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook shrimp, oil, and garlic, stirring frequently, until shrimp turn pink. Remove from heat.
3. Toss pasta with pesto. Add sun-dried tomatoes and basil; toss well. Top with shrimp and cheese.
