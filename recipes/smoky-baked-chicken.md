## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- 2 teaspoons mesquite seasoning
- ½ (8 ounce) package shredded Mexican cheese blend
- ⅓ cup real bacon bits
- ⅓ cup sliced green onions
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 ½ teaspoons chipotle-flavored hot sauce (such as Tabasco® Chipotle Pepper Sauce)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a baking dish with aluminum foil; brush with olive oil.
3. Season chicken breasts with mesquite seasoning and place in the prepared baking dish.
4. Mix cheese blend, bacon bits, green onions, mayonnaise, and chipotle-flavored hot sauce together in a small bowl.
5. Spread mixture evenly over chicken.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer pink in the center and an instant-read thermometer inserted into the center reads at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C), about 16 minutes.
7. Turn off oven; leave chicken inside for 2 minutes.
8. Turn on broiler; broil chicken on low until topping is golden, 2 to 3 minutes.
