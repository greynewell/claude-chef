## Ingredients

- ½ cup vegetarian refried beans
- 1 large whole-wheat tortilla
- 2  romaine lettuce leaves
- ½  avocado, peeled and sliced
- ¼ cup fresh pico de gallo

## Instructions

1. Stir refried beans in a saucepan over medium-low heat until hot, 2 to 4 minutes.
2. Warm tortilla in a skillet over low heat until softened, 1 to 2 minutes.
3. Transfer tortilla to a plate.
4. Layer lettuce onto tortilla.
5. Spread warmed refried beans, avocado slices, and pico de gallo on top of lettuce.
6. Roll tortilla around the fillings into a burrito shape.
