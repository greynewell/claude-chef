## Ingredients

- 1 pound large cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined, tails removed
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and cubed
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 (12 ounce) bottle cocktail sauce
- ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce  (Optional)
- 8 wedges fresh lime
- 1 (10 ounce) bag corn tortilla chips

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, combine the shrimp, avocado and cilantro.
2. Stir in the cocktail sauce and hot pepper sauce until evenly coated.
3. Divide into 8 small portions and serve with lime wedges and tortilla chips.
