## Ingredients

- 1 pound seedless red grapes, halved
- ½ pint cherry tomatoes, halved
- 1  red bell pepper, diced
- 1  orange bell pepper, diced
- 1  yellow bell pepper, diced
- ½ cup edamame (green soybeans)
- ½ cup crumbled feta cheese with herbs
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon lemon-pepper seasoning
- salt to taste
- 1  avocado, diced
- ¾ cup cashews
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon red wine vinegar, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix grapes, tomatoes, red bell pepper, orange bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, edamame, and feta cheese with herbs in a bowl.
2. Add basil, lemon pepper, and salt to vegetable-grape mixture; toss to coat.
3. Add avocado and cashews to vegetable-grape mixture just before serving.
4. Drizzle olive oil and vinegar over salad; toss to coat.
