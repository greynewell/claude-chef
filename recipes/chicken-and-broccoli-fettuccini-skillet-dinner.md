## Ingredients

- 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts, trimmed
- 1 pinch Salt and pepper
- 3 tablespoons Mazola® Corn Oil
- 1 cup diced onion
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon Italian herb seasoning
- 1 quart water
- 1 tablespoon chicken-flavored bouillon powder
- 8 ounces fettuccine noodles, broken in half
- 3 cups broccoli florets, fresh or frozen (thawed)
- 1 cup half-and-half
- 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Pat chicken dry using paper towels.
2. Cut chicken into 1- to 2-inch cubes and season with salt and pepper, if desired.
3. Heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Cook chicken for 2 minutes or until lightly browned (chicken will not be fully cooked at this point).
5. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside.
6. Add onions to pan and cook for 2 minutes.
7. Add garlic and Italian herb seasoning.
8. Stir in water and bouillon powder; bring to a boil.
9. Add fettuccini and stir gently.
10. Reduce heat to medium and cover pan with lid.
11. Cook for 6 to 7 minutes, stirring once.
12. Add chicken, broccoli and half and half, stirring to combine.
13. Reduce heat to low, cover and continue cooking for 5 to 7 minutes or until chicken is cooked through and broccoli is tender.
14. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.
15. Garnish with additional Parmesan cheese, if desired.
