## Ingredients

- 1 pound cooked chicken breast, chopped
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- ½ cup sliced almonds
- ¾ cup Greek yogurt
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 ½ teaspoons curry powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken with celery and almonds in a bowl.
2. Mix Greek yogurt, honey, lemon juice, curry powder, salt, and pepper together in a small bowl. Pour dressing over the chicken salad and stir to combine.
