## Ingredients

- 6 cups water
- 1 cube chicken bouillon
- 1 pound dried black-eyed peas, sorted and rinsed
- 1  onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, diced
- 1  red bell pepper, stemmed, seeded, and diced
- 1  jalapeno chile, seeded and minced
- 8 ounces diced ham
- 4 slices bacon, chopped
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons cumin
- salt, to taste
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Pour water into a slow cooker; add bouillon cube and stir to dissolve. Stir in black-eyed peas, ham, bacon, onion, bell pepper, jalapeño, garlic, cumin, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and salt.
2. Cover and cook on Low until beans are tender, 6 to 8 hours.
