## Ingredients

- 3  oranges, juiced
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced
- 1 (1 1/2 inch) piece ginger, grated
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
- 4 (4 ounce) fillets swai fish
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons canola oil, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Stir orange juice, lemon juice, lemon zest, ginger, and soy sauce together in a bowl; pour into a resealable plastic bag.
2. Add swai fillets, coat with the liquid, squeeze bag to remove excess air, and seal the bag.
3. Marinate in the refrigerator for 1 to 2 hours.
4. Remove fillets from marinade and shake to remove excess moisture.
5. Discard remaining marinade.
6. Season fish with salt and pepper.
7. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
8. Fry fish in hot oil until the flesh flakes easily with a fork, 3 to 4 minutes per side.
