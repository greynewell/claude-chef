## Ingredients

- ¼ cup rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons wasabi paste
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 1 head napa cabbage, shredded
- 1  cucumber, peeled and sliced
- 1  red bell pepper, cut into matchstick-size pieces
- 1 large carrot, shredded
- 3 tablespoons sesame seeds
- 1  avocado, diced

## Instructions

1. Whisk rice vinegar, honey, ginger, soy sauce, wasabi paste, and sesame oil together in a bowl until vinaigrette is smooth.
2. Combine cabbage, cucumber, red bell pepper, and carrot in a large bowl.
3. Pour vinaigrette over cabbage mixture and toss.
4. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
5. Heat a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir sesame seeds until toasted, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Garnish salad with toasted sesame seeds and avocado.
