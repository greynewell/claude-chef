## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package angel hair pasta
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 cups milk
- ½ teaspoon ground paprika
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ¾ cup shredded Swiss cheese
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 pound cooked shrimp

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook angel hair in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, 4 to 5 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
5. Add garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
6. Stir in flour until a thick paste forms.
7. Whisk in milk gradually until fully combined; bring to a simmer.
8. Whisk paprika, black pepper, and red pepper flakes into the sauce.
9. Add Swiss cheese and Parmesan cheese a little at a time, whisking constantly, until melted into the sauce, 3 to 5 minutes.
10. Combine angel hair pasta and shrimp in a large bowl; pour cheese sauce on top.
