## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
- ¾ cup chopped fresh parsley
- ½  onion, chopped
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon lemon juice
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 1 ½ cups vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Place garbanzo beans, parsley, onion, flour, garlic, cumin, baking powder, coriander, salt, lemon juice, and black pepper in a food processor or blender.
2. Pulse until combined and a chunky, slightly sticky mixture forms.
3. Roll mixture into small balls and place on a plate.
4. Flatten into patties that are 2 inches in diameter and 1/2-inch thick.
5. Place patties in refrigerator or freezer to help hold together, about 10 minutes.
6. Heat 1 1/2- to 2-inches vegetable oil in large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
7. Fry patties, working in batches to avoid crowding, until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side.
8. Drain patties on paper towel-lined plate.
