## Ingredients

- 4  pita bread rounds
- ½ cup pesto
- 2  tomatoes, sliced
- 1 (4 ounce) container crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Arrange pita bread on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in preheated oven until pita is lightly toasted, about 4 minutes.
4. Spread pesto evenly over toasted pita bread and arrange tomato slices in a single layer.
5. Top with feta cheese.
6. Continue baking until feta cheese is browned and pita bread is crisp, about 11 minutes more.
