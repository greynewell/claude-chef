## Ingredients

- 3 small zucchini, coarsely chopped
- 1  onion, coarsely chopped
- 5  eggs
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ cup crumbled reduced-fat feta cheese
- ½ teaspoon dried parsley
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 pinch ground black pepper
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded fat-free mozzarella cheese
- paprika for garnish

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a quiche dish or a 9-inch pie pan, and set aside.
3. Place the zucchini, onion, eggs, Parmesan cheese, feta cheese, parsley, salt, and pepper into the work bowl of a food processor, and process for about 1 minute, until all the ingredients are finely chopped and blended.
4. Add the flour and baking powder, and process 1 more minute, scraping the side of the bowl if necessary.
5. Place the mozzarella cheese into the work bowl, and process for about 1 minute, to combine the cheese thoroughly into the mixture.
6. Scrape the mixture into the prepared quiche dish, sprinkle with paprika, and bake for 1 hour, or until set.
7. The quiche is done when a knife inserted into the center comes out clean.
