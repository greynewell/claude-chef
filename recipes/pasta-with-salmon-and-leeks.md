## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1  leek, chopped
- ½ cup dry white wine
- 4 tablespoons sour cream
- 7 ounces salmon fillets
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- ½ teaspoon minced fresh savory   (Optional)
- ½ teaspoon minced fresh thyme
- 2 tablespoons grated fresh Parmesan cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
3. While spaghetti is cooking, melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Cook and stir leek until soft, about 5 minutes.
5. Pour in white wine and simmer until almost all the wine has evaporated, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Stir in sour cream and 1 ladleful of spaghetti cooking water and mix well.
7. Reduce heat to low.
8. Add salmon and cook until it is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
9. Season with salt and pepper.
10. Stir in savory and thyme.
11. Drain spaghetti and toss with the creamy salmon.
12. Serve sprinkled with Parmesan cheese.
