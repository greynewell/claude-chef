## Ingredients

- 4 cups shrimp stock
- 1 teaspoon freshly-grated ginger
- 1 pound asparagus spears, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 cup cooked crabmeat

## Instructions

1. Combine shrimp stock and ginger in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
2. Bring to a rolling boil.
3. Stir in asparagus and reduce heat to medium.
4. Cover and simmer until asparagus are partially cooked, about 3 minutes.
5. Reduce heat to medium-low.
6. Combine cornstarch and water in a small bowl.
7. Whisk cornstarch mixture into soup.
8. Stir sesame oil and soy sauce into soup.
9. Simmer, stirring constantly, until soup thickens, about 1 minute.
10. Slowly add beaten egg, while gently stirring soup to form threads of cooked egg.
11. Stir in crab meat and simmer until crab is thoroughly heated, about 5 minutes.
