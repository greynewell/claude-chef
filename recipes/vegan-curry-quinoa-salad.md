## Ingredients

- 2 ⅓ cups water
- 1 ⅓ cups quinoa
- 6 ounces vegan sour cream (such as Tofutti®)
- ¼ cup curry powder
- 2 tablespoons freshly squeezed lime juice
- 2 teaspoons finely grated peeled fresh ginger
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 6 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 (1 pound) firm-ripe mango, cut into 1/2-inch chunks
- 1 cup salted roasted peanuts, chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- ⅔ cup chopped fresh mint
- 1  jalapeno chile, seeded and minced

## Instructions

1. Combine water and quinoa in a saucepan over high heat.
2. Cover and bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat to low.
4. Cook until water has been absorbed, about 15 minutes.
5. Whisk sour cream, curry powder, lime juice, ginger, salt, and black pepper together in a large bowl.
6. Add oil; whisk thoroughly.
7. Toss with the quinoa, mango, peanuts, red bell pepper, mint, and jalapeno.
