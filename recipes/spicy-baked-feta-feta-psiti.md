## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) slice feta cheese
- 2 teaspoons olive oil  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons crushed red pepper flakes, or as needed
- 1 pinch dried oregano

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place the feta in an ovenproof baking dish. Drizzle with olive oil. Cover liberally with pepper flakes, and sprinkle with oregano.
3. Bake, uncovered, in the preheated oven until feta is soft, about 10 minutes.
