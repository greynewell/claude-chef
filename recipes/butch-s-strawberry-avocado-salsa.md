## Ingredients

- 1 cup hulled strawberries, coarsely chopped
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and coarsely chopped
- ¼ cup chopped red onion
- ½  jalapeno pepper, seeded and finely chopped - or to taste
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a serving bowl, lightly mix together the strawberries, avocado, red onion, jalapeno pepper, cilantro, lime juice, and black pepper.
2. Serve immediately.
