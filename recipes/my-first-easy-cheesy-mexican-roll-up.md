## Ingredients

- 1 (8.8 ounce) pouch UNCLE BEN'S® Ready Rice® Garden Vegetable
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 (6 ounce) package fully cooked oven-roasted diced chicken breast (like Tyson® Grilled and Ready®)
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 8  flour tortillas
- 2 tablespoons salsa  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Prepare rice according to package directions.
2. Top each tortilla with rice, black beans, diced chicken breast, and shredded cheese.
3. Add salsa, if desired.
4. Fold tortilla in half or roll burrito style and heat in microwave for about 30 seconds for each individual burrito.
