## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups seasoned rice vinegar
- ½ cup water
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 2 large carrots, peeled and cut into 3x1/8-inch matchstick strips
- ½  daikon radish, peeled and cut into 3x1/8-inch matchstick strips
- 3 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon white sugar
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 pound flat iron steaks
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil, or as needed
- 6  (8 1/2-inch) rice paper wrappers
- 6  large soft-leaf lettuce leaves (such as Bibb or Boston)
- 3 cups loosely packed fresh cilantro sprigs
- ¼ cup peanut butter
- 1 teaspoon Sriracha hot sauce

## Instructions

1. Combine vinegar, water, 2 tablespoons sugar, fish sauce, and red pepper flakes in a glass, ceramic, or stainless steel bowl; stir until sugar is dissolved.
2. Add carrots and daikon radish.
3. Put a plate on top to weigh down vegetables and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
4. Combine soy sauce, 1 tablespoon sugar, and garlic in a shallow baking dish; stir until sugar is dissolved.
5. Add steak, pressing down until steak is covered in marinade, and refrigerate for 4 hours or overnight.
6. Heat a ridged grill pan over high heat; add oil.
7. Remove steak from marinade and discard marinade.
8. Grill steak until desired doneness is reached, 6 to 10 minutes per side.
9. Transfer steak to a cutting board and cool.
10. Slice across grain into 24 thin slices.
11. Drain carrot and daikon radishes, reserving 1/2 cup pickling liquid.
12. Fill a shallow bowl with warm water.
13. Dip 1 rice paper sheet into water to soften, about 5 seconds.
14. Transfer sheet to a work surface.
15. Arrange 2 pieces of lettuce over the half of wrapper closest to you.
16. Top each lettuce leaf half with 2 slices of steak.
17. Add about 1/4 cup carrot and daikon pickles and a few cilantro sprigs.
18. Starting with bottom edge, begin to roll up rice paper sheet tightly around filling.
19. After 1 roll, fold in ends of rice paper like a burrito, then finish rolling.
20. Repeat with remaining wrappers and filling.
21. Diagonally cut rolls in half crosswise and arrange on a serving platter.
22. Whisk peanut butter, reserved pickling liquid, and sriracha hot sauce together in a bowl; serve on the side with rolls.
