## Ingredients

- 4  tortilla wraps
- Roast beef:
- 4 slices roast beef cooked rare
- 2 teaspoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons sesame oil
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 1  avocado, sliced in 1/2-inch wide strips
- 4  Maille® cornichons sliced in half lengthwise
- Maille® Old Style mustard cream:
- ½ cup whipping cream
- 2 tablespoons Maille® Old Style Mustard
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Roast beef: Mix the soy sauce and the sesame oil together and cover each slice of roast beef with a brush.
2. Season roast beef with ground pepper.
3. Maille® Old Style mustard cream: Whip the whipping cream to make it rise.
4. Then mix in Maille® Old Style mustard and season with salt and pepper.
5. Generously cover the inside of tortilla wraps with Maille® Old Style mustard cream.
6. Lay in the center of each tortilla one slice of roast beef.
7. Add the avocado and Maille® cornichons on top of the meat.
8. Roll it up tightly together.
9. Cut the wrap in the middle at an angle.
10. Serve cold.
