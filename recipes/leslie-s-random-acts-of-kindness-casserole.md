## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen petite peas
- 1 (16 ounce) package fusilli pasta
- 1 pint heavy whipping cream
- 2 (3 ounce) packages smoked wild salmon, pulled into small pieces
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 cup plain bread crumbs
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- ⅓ cup chopped Italian flat-leaf parsley
- 1  lemon, zested and juiced

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Place peas in a large colander in the sink.
3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
4. Cook fusilli in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until chewy and still firm to the bite, about 9 minutes.
5. Drain fusilli over peas.
6. Return fusilli and peas to the pot.
7. Add heavy cream and smoked salmon; mix well to combine.
8. Season with salt and black pepper.
9. Pour into a 9x13-inch casserole dish.
10. Combine bread crumbs, olive oil, parsley, lemon zest, and lemon juice in a small bowl; mix lightly with a fork.
11. Scatter topping over casserole.
12. Bake in the preheated oven until golden, about 20 minutes.
