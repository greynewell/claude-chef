## Ingredients

- 2 (15 ounce) cans brown lentils
- 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
- ½ (16 ounce) bottle Greek, or other vinaigrette salad dressing
- 1  English cucumber, peeled and diced
- 1  beefsteak tomato, diced
- 1 large red onion, diced

## Instructions

1. Strain lentils and place in a large bowl.
2. Pour in 1/2 of the salad dressing, and stir to coat.
3. Place in the refrigerator while preparing other vegetables.
4. Add feta cheese, cucumber, tomato, and red onion.
5. Cover, and refrigerate for 1 hour.
6. Stir in remaining dressing when ready to serve.
7. May be kept, covered, in the refrigerator for up to 3 days.
