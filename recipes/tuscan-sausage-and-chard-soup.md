## Ingredients

- ½ pound bulk mild Italian sausage
- ½  onion, chopped
- ½  red bell pepper, chopped
- 6 cups water
- 1 bunch Swiss chard, chopped
- 2 cubes  chicken bouillon (such as Knorr®)
- 1 teaspoon garlic salt
- ½ teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 2 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat and stir in the sausage.
2. Cook and stir until sausage is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, about 5 minutes.
3. Drain and discard any excess grease.
4. Stir onion and red bell pepper into sausage; cook and stir until vegetables begin to soften, about 3 minutes.
5. Pour water into the pot; add Swiss chard, chicken bouillon, garlic salt, and Italian seasoning.
6. Cover and reduce heat to medium-low; Simmer until vegetables are very tender, about 20 minutes.
7. Sprinkle soup with Parmesan cheese before serving.
