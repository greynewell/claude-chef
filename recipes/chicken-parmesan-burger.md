## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground chicken
- 1  egg
- ½ cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons tomato paste, divided
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 cup crushed tomatoes
- 1 cup chicken stock
- 4 slices provolone cheese
- 4  whole wheat hamburger buns, split

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken, egg, bread crumbs, Parmesan cheese, 1 tablespoon tomato paste, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, and onion powder in a large bowl.
2. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Form mixture into 4 patties.
4. Press gently to flatten.
5. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large skillet.
6. Cook patties until browned, about 4 minutes per side.
7. Transfer to a large plate.
8. Cook and stir onion in the drippings left in the skillet until translucent, 3 to 4 minutes.
9. Stir in remaining 2 tablespoons tomato paste, crushed tomatoes, and chicken stock.
10. Cook until thickened and reduced by half, about 5 minutes.
11. Return patties to the skillet; cover with tomato sauce.
12. Place 1 slice of provolone cheese over each patty.
13. Cover skillet and cook until cheese melts slightly, about 1 minute.
14. Place patties on buns; spoon tomato sauce on top.
