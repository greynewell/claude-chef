## Ingredients

- 12 ounces spaghetti
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - diced
- ¼ cup Italian seasoning
- 2 teaspoons bottled minced garlic
- 1 (15.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
- ½ cup tequila
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly-salted water to a boil; add the pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
2. Place spaghetti in a large bowl.
3. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
4. Cook and stir the chicken in the hot oil until browned; stir in the Italian seasoning and garlic and cook until the chicken is cooked through, about 5 minutes.
5. Add the tomatoes and tequila; simmer another 5 minutes.
6. Season with salt and pepper.
7. Pour into the bowl with the spaghetti and stir to combine; serve immediately.
