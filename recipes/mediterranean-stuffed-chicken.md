## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded thin
- 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
- â…“ cup feta cheese
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons dried dill weed
- 2 tablespoons melted butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. TO MAKE FILLING:  In a large bowl beat the cream cheese until smooth.
3. Add the feta cheese, dill weed and garlic powder.
4. Beat until smooth and creamy.
5. Put a dollop of 1/4 of the filling on each breast, then roll and tie the breasts with cooking string to seal.
6. Brush each breast with melted butter/margarine and sprinkle with salt and pepper to taste.
7. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven for 20 minutes or until the juices run clear.
