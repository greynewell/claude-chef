## Ingredients

- 1 cup light mayonnaise
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- ¼ cup coarsely chopped fresh cilantro
- 3 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1 teaspoon white sugar
- 1 teaspoon grated lime zest
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 12 cups water, or as needed
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 6  jalapeno peppers, seeded and sliced
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 1  lime, halved
- 4 (2 pound) lobsters

## Instructions

1. Combine mayonnaise, minced jalapeno pepper, cilantro, lime juice, sugar, lime zest, cumin, and 1/2 teaspoon salt in a bowl; stir until smooth.
2. Chill until serving.
3. Combine water, chicken broth, sliced jalapeno peppers, 3 tablespoons salt, and lime in a large Dutch oven; bring to a boil.
4. Add lobsters; cook until bright red, 8 to 10 minutes.
5. Drain and cool until easily handled, 5 to 10 minutes.
6. Remove meat from lobster shells, tails, and claws.
7. Serve with mayonnaise-cilantro mixture.
