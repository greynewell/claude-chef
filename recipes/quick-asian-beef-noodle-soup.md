## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 4 cups water
- 1 medium head bok choy, chopped
- 2 (3 ounce) packages beef flavored ramen noodles
- 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce

## Instructions

1. Brown meat in a large skillet.
2. Drain off fat, and rinse meat using a colander.
3. Transfer meat to a large cooking pot.
4. Stir in onion, garlic, and ginger.
5. Add water, and bring soup to a boil.
6. Stir in bok choy.
7. Reduce heat ,and simmer for about 3 minutes.
8. Stir in noodles.
9. Simmer 3 minutes longer, or until bok choy and onions are crisp tender and noodles are soft.
10. Stir in seasoning packets, oil, and soy sauce.
