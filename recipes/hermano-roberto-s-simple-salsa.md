## Ingredients

- 3  jalapeno peppers, or more to taste
- 1 (28 ounce) can whole peeled tomatoes with juice
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon ground white pepper
- 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro

## Instructions

1. Bring a saucepan of lightly salted water to a boil; add jalapeno peppers.
2. Reduce heat to medium and boil peppers for 15 minutes; drain.
3. When cool enough to handle, remove stems of peppers.
4. Place jalapeno peppers, tomatoes, garlic salt, cumin, black pepper, white pepper, and cilantro in a blender.
5. Blend to your desired texture, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
