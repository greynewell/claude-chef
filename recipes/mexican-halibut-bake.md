## Ingredients

- ¼ cup butter, melted
- 2 pounds skinless halibut fillets
- lemon pepper to taste
- ¾ cup salsa
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ¾ cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon garlic oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Pour the butter in the bottom of a  baking dish.
3. Arrange the halibut fillets in the dish, and season with lemon pepper.
4. In a bowl, mix the salsa, mayonnaise, sour cream, and garlic oil.
5. Spoon over the halibut fillets.
6. Bake halibut 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until easily flaked with a fork.
