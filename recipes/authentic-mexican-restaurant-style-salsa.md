## Ingredients

- 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
- 10 slices pickled jalapeno peppers with juice, or to taste
- 10  cilantro leaves
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder

## Instructions

1. Blend crushed tomatoes, jalapeno peppers with juice, cilantro leaves, salt, and garlic powder in a blender until smooth.
2. Refrigerate 8 hours to overnight.
