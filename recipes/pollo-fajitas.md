## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon cider vinegar
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 dash hot pepper sauce
- 1 ½ pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into strips
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1  onion, thinly sliced
- 1  green bell pepper, sliced
- ½  lemon, juiced

## Instructions

1. Combine Worcestershire sauce, vinegar, soy sauce, chili powder, garlic, and hot pepper sauce in a medium bowl. Place chicken in sauce, turning to coat. Marinate at room temperature for 30 minutes or cover and refrigerate for several hours.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over high heat. Add chicken strips; cook and stir in hot oil for 5 minutes. Add onion and green pepper; sauté for 3 minutes. Remove from heat and sprinkle with lemon juice.
