## Ingredients

- 1  habanero pepper, halved and seeded  (Optional)
- 1  jalapeno pepper, halved and seeded
- 2  fresh red chile peppers, halved and seeded
- 2 (16 ounce) cans diced tomatoes
- 1  onion, cut into chunks
- 1 bunch fresh cilantro
- 4  green onions, roughly chopped
- 3 cloves garlic

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Arrange habanero pepper, jalapeno pepper, and red chile peppers on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in preheated oven until browned, about 15 minutes.
4. Transfer peppers to a food processor; add tomatoes, onion, cilantro, green onions, and garlic.
5. Pulse until desired consistency.
