## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- 1 cup chopped celery
- 1 cup dry lentils
- ½ cup quinoa
- ½ cup chopped carrots
- ½ cup mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 tablespoon cumin
- 1 tablespoon ground ginger

## Instructions

1. Combine water, celery, lentils, quinoa, carrots, mushrooms, chili powder, cumin, and ginger in a saucepan.
2. Cover saucepan and cook soup over medium-low heat until vegetables and lentils are tender, 45 to 60 minutes.
