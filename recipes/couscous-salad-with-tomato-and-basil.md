## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can fat-free, reduced-sodium chicken broth
- 1 ½ cups couscous
- 2 cups chopped tomato
- ⅓ cup thinly sliced fresh basil
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Mix 1 tablespoon olive oil and garlic in a large microwave-safe bowl; heat on high until fragrant, about 45 seconds.
2. Add chicken broth and heat on high until simmering, about 4 minutes.
3. Gradually stir in couscous.
4. Cover and let stand until liquid is absorbed, about 5 minutes.
5. Fluff couscous with a fork.
6. Stir tomato, basil, vinegar, salt, and black pepper into couscous; drizzle with remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil and sprinkle with feta cheese.
