## Ingredients

- ½ tablespoon vinegar
- ⅓ cup milk, or as needed
- 2 tablespoons dried parsley
- 1 teaspoon dried dill
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon dried basil
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon ground paprika
- 5  fresh chives, finely chopped  (Optional)
- salt to taste
- 1 cup Greek yogurt

## Instructions

1. Pour vinegar into a 1-cup liquid measure. Add enough milk to make 1/3 cup. Let sit until curdled, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Combine chives, parsley, dill, garlic powder, onion powder, basil, pepper, paprika, and salt in a small bowl. Mix in yogurt until smooth.
3. Gradually stir buttermilk into dressing to thin.
