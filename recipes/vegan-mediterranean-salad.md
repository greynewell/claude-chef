## Ingredients

- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder, or more to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  red bell pepper, diced
- 1  green bell peppers, diced
- 3  Persian cucumbers, diced
- 4  tomatoes, chopped
- 4  radishes, chopped, or more to taste
- 2  kosher dill pickles, chopped
- 3  green onions, sliced, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, lemon, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl.
2. Add red bell pepper, green bell pepper, cucumbers, tomatoes, radishes, dill pickles, and green onions; toss to combine.
3. Let marinate in the refrigerator until flavors combine, 10 to 15 minutes.
