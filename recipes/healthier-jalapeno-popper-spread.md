## Ingredients

- 2 (8 ounce) packages reduced-fat cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup light mayonnaise
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies, drained
- 2 ounces canned diced jalapeno peppers, drained
- ¼ cup chopped cilantro
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Stir cream cheese and mayonnaise in a large bowl until smooth.
2. Stir in green chilies, jalapeno peppers, and cilantro.
3. Pour mixture into a microwave safe serving dish and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
4. Microwave on High until hot, about 3 minutes.
