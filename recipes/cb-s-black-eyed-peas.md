## Ingredients

- 4 slices bacon, chopped
- 1 pound dry black-eyed peas
- 6 cups water
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
- 1  jalapeno pepper, finely chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the bacon in a large, deep skillet, and cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until evenly browned, about 10 minutes.
2. Place the dried peas, water, onion, tomatoes, jalapeno pepper, garlic, and chili powder into a slow cooker, and stir to combine.
3. Stir in the bacon and bacon grease, and set the cooker on High.
4. Cook until peas are tender, about 4 hours.
5. Season to taste with salt, and serve.
