## Ingredients

- 6 ounces mussels
- ½ cup chicken stock
- 2 stalks lemongrass
- 1 small bunch Thai basil, chopped

## Instructions

1. Place mussels in a pan of cold water.
2. Discard any that float.
3. Drain, clean, and debeard remaining mussels.
4. Place stock and lemongrass in a saucepan over medium heat; bring to a boil.
5. Add mussels and let steam, covered, until they open, 1 to 4 minutes.
6. Discard any mussels that do not open.
7. Transfer mussels to a bowl and sprinkle basil on top.
8. Serve with another bowl alongside for discarded shells.
