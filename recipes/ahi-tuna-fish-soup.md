## Ingredients

- 4 cups water
- ½ pound Chinese long beans, cut into halves
- 1  chayote squash, cut into small wedges
- 1 (1 inch) piece ginger, peeled and sliced thick
- 1  green onions, chopped
- 2  frozen thick ahi tuna steaks, thawed
- 2 heads   bok choy, chopped
- 1  tomato, chopped

## Instructions

1. Put water in a pot; add long beans, chayote, ginger, and green onion.
2. Bring the water to a boil, place a lid on the pot, and reduce heat to medium.
3. Continue cooking until the vegetables are tender, about 8 minutes.
4. Gently drop tuna steaks into the water; add bok choy and tomato.
5. Return cover to pot, reduce heat to low, and cook until the tuna is cooked through, about 20 minutes.
6. Remove pot from heat; cool 5 minutes before serving.
