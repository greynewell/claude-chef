## Ingredients

- 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons honey
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh dill
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
- ½ cup celery, diced

## Instructions

1. Combine Greek yogurt, mustard, lemon juice, honey, parsley, dill, salt, pepper, and onion powder in a small bowl. Mix well until dressing is smooth and creamy.
2. Combine shredded chicken and celery in a large bowl. Top with dressing and mix to combine. Chill for 1 hour before serving.
