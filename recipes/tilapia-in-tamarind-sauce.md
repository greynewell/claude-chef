## Ingredients

- 1 pound tilapia fillets
- 1 teaspoon tamarind paste
- ¼ cup hot water
- 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger, grated
- 2 cloves garlic, pressed
- salt to taste
- 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon ground coriander
- ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Rinse tilapia fillets and dry with paper towels.
2. Dissolve tamarind paste in hot water in a bowl; stir ginger, garlic, salt, black pepper, coriander, turmeric, and cumin into tamarind mixture to make a paste.
3. Rub paste over fish and let stand 15 minutes to absorb flavors (or longer in refrigerator if desired).
4. Heat vegetable oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat.
5. Cook fish with tamarind paste in the hot oil until the flesh is opaque and flaky, about 4 minutes on the first side and 2 minutes on the second side.
6. Drizzle with lemon juice.
