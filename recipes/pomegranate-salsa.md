## Ingredients

- 2  pomegranates, peeled and seeds separated
- 2 (8 ounce) cans mandarin orange segments, drained and coarsely chopped
- 1  red onion, chopped
- 1  jalapeno pepper, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced pickled jalapeno pepper
- 1 bunch cilantro, minced
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 pinch ground cumin

## Instructions

1. Mix pomegranate seeds, mandarin oranges, onion, fresh jalapeno, pickled jalapeno, cilantro, lime juice, and cumin together in a bowl.
2. Cover bowl and refrigerate for several hours or overnight to let flavors blend.
