## Ingredients

- 2 large ripe red tomatoes, sliced
- 10 thin slices  fresh mozzarella cheese
- 10  fresh basil leaves, chopped
- 10  kalamata olives
- extra virgin olive oil
- salt to taste
- cracked black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Alternate and overlap the tomato slices and mozzarella cheese slices on a platter.
2. Sprinkle the basil over the tomato and mozzarella layers.
3. Scatter the olives over the salad.
4. Drizzle the olive oil over everything; season with salt and pepper.
5. Allow to sit at room temperature 15 minutes before serving.
