## Ingredients

- 24  long cocktail toothpicks
- 48  cherry or grape tomatoes, rinsed and dried
- 6 thin slices  prosciutto, each cut into quarters
- 24  bite-size marinated mozzarella balls*
- 24  fresh basil leaves
- ¼ cup Italian salad dressing

## Instructions

1. Thread each toothpick with: one tomato, 1/4 of a slice of prosciutto folded into a small square, one mozzarella ball, one basil leaf (folded if it is large), and one more tomato.
2. Arrange appetizers on a serving platter.
3. Drizzle with your favorite Italian salad dressing.
