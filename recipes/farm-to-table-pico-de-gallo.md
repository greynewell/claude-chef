## Ingredients

- 4  Roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
- 1 cup diced onion
- 1  carrot, peeled and chopped
- ¼ cup fresh cilantro leaves, or to taste
- 3 small jalapeno peppers
- 2  green chile peppers
- ¼ large poblano chile
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
- 1 teaspoon sea salt

## Instructions

1. Mix tomatoes and onion in a bowl.
2. Process carrot, cilantro, jalapeno peppers, green chile peppers, poblano chile, lime juice, and garlic in a food processor to your preferred texture; add to tomato mixture and stir.
3. Season the mixture with sea salt.
4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 2 hours before serving.
