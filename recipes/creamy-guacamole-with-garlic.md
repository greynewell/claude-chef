## Ingredients

- 2  avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
- 4 teaspoons lime juice
- ½ cup salsa
- 3 ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1 clove garlic, pressed
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce

## Instructions

1. Beat avocado with lime juice in a small mixing bowl; add salsa, cream cheese, garlic, salt, and hot pepper sauce.
2. Transfer mixture to a food processor; blend until smooth.
