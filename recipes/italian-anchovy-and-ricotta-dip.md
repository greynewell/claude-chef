## Ingredients

- 1 (2 ounce) can anchovy fillets with oil
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 5 ½ ounces ricotta cheese
- 5 ½ ounces cream cheese, softened
- 1 tablespoon sour cream
- ½  lemon, juiced and zested
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley, or more to taste
- ¼ cup chopped pecans
- 1 tablespoon whole pecans

## Instructions

1. Combine anchovies and capers in a blender; blend into a paste.
2. Add ricotta cheese, cream cheese, sour cream, lemon juice, and parsley; mix well.
3. Spoon into a serving dish and refrigerate for 1 hour.
4. Fold in lemon zest and pecan nuts before serving.
5. Garnish with more parsley, if desired, and whole pecans.
