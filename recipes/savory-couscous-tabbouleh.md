## Ingredients

- 1 cup low-sodium chicken broth
- ½ cup water
- 1 cup couscous
- 1  cucumber, seeded and diced
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 1  carrot, grated
- 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring chicken broth and water to a boil in a saucepan; remove from heat and stir in couscous.
2. Cover saucepan and let couscous stand until the liquid is absorbed and the couscous is tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Fluff with a fork.
4. Place couscous, cucumber, green onions, carrot, and parsley into a large salad bowl.
5. Combine olive oil and lemon juice in a small bowl and pour over the couscous and vegetables; stir cumin, salt, and black pepper into the tabbouleh.
6. Mix in feta cheese just before serving.
