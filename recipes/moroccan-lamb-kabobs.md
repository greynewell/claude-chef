## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ground lamb
- 1 cup raisins
- 5 ounces goat cheese
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- 1  red onion, finely chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, finely chopped
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- ¾ tablespoon ground cayenne pepper
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon ground coriander
- salt to taste
- coarsely ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
2. In a medium bowl, mix together ground lamb, raisins, goat cheese, mayonnaise, red onion, garlic, cilantro, cayenne pepper, cumin, ground coriander, salt and black pepper.
3. Divide the mixture into approximately 6 even portions, and press around skewers.
4. Place skewers on the grill.
5. Cook approximately 4 minutes per side, or until the cheese has melted, the raisins are tender and lamb has reached desired doneness.
