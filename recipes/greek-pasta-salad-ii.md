## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package rotini pasta
- 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 7 ounces crumbled feta cheese
- 1 tablespoon dried dill weed
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and reserve.
3. Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan over medium heat, bring water to a boil.
4. Add spinach and cook for 5 minutes or until spinach is tender; drain and reserve.
5. In a large pot over medium heat, warm olive oil and saute garlic until golden; add pasta and spinach and mix.
6. Remove from heat and let cool for ten minutes.
7. Add feta and dill; mix well and serve.
