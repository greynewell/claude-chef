## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti (such as Dreamfields®)
- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 pound asparagus, cut into pieces
- 1  red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- 4  shallots, thinly sliced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh basil
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ⅛ teaspoon oregano
- 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes; drain.
3. Transfer to a large mixing bowl.
4. Melt butter with olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat.
5. Cook and stir asparagus, red bell pepper, shallots, garlic, basil, red pepper flakes, and oregano in the butter mixture until the pepper is softened, about 5 minutes.
6. Add shrimp and continue cooking until the shrimp are bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, 3 to 5 minutes more.
7. Pour shrimp and vegetable mixture over the spaghetti and toss to mix.
8. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese over the pasta mixture and season with salt and pepper.
