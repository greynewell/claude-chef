## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon hot paprika
- 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon chili powder
- 2  egg whites, slightly beaten
- 2 pounds potatoes, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Prepare a large baking pan with cooking spray.
3. Whisk together the olive oil, cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, oregano, pepper, and chili powder in a large bowl.
4. Place the egg whites in a separate large bowl.
5. Toss the potato wedges first with the egg whites and then with the olive oil mixture.
6. Arrange the seasoned wedges in a single layer on the prepared baking pan.
7. Bake in the preheated oven, turning occasionally, until crispy, about 40 minutes.
