## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) container nonfat Greek yogurt
- ¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon, or to taste
- stevia powder, to taste
- 1  apple, cored and cut into chunks

## Instructions

1. Stir yogurt, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and stevia together in a small bowl.
2. Add apples and toss to coat.
3. Refrigerate for about 5 minutes before serving.
