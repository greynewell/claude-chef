## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 4 cups corn kernels
- 1 ½ tablespoons fajita seasoning
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- ½ cup chopped green onion
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced, or to taste
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- ¼ cup fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons orange juice
- 1 pinch salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir corn, fajita seasoning, and black pepper in the hot oil until corn is lightly browned, 6 to 8 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and set aside to cool.
4. Mix corn mixture, black beans, red bell pepper, green onion, jalapeno pepper, cilantro, lime juice, orange juice, and salt together in a bowl; cover and refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
