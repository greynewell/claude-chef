## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package JENNIE-O® Lean Ground Turkey
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 tablespoon fresh grated ginger
- 2 tablespoons HOUSE OF TSANG® ginger-flavored soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- ¼ cup chopped green onions
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- ½ cup chopped fresh mint leaves
- 1 head butter lettuce

## Instructions

1. Cook turkey as specified on the package.
2. Always cook to well done, 165 degrees F as measured by a meat thermometer.
3. Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce, brown sugar, green onions and red bell pepper.
4. Cook 7 minutes or until garlic is soft.
5. Stir in mint leaves.
6. Immediately transfer meat to a shallow dish and chill 1 hour.
7. Remove core from head of lettuce; separate, wash and dry leaves.
8. Spoon chilled turkey mixture in lettuce cups.
