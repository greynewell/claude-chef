## Ingredients

- 1 head cauliflower, broken into small florets
- 1 (8 ounce) bottle Italian dressing
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup bacon bits
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Place cauliflower in a large bowl and cover with Italian dressing. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
2. Drain cauliflower and stir in mayonnaise, bacon bits and cheese.
