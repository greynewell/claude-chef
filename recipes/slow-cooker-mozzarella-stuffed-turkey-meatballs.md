## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package low-moisture mozzarella cheese
- 1 pound ground turkey
- ½ pound bulk Italian turkey sausage
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- ½ cup whole milk
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 2  eggs
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 (24 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce

## Instructions

1. Cut mozzarella cheese into 3/4-inch cubes.
2. Store in the refrigerator while preparing the meat.
3. Combine turkey, turkey sausage, bread crumbs, milk, Parmesan cheese, parsley, eggs, salt, pepper, and garlic powder in a large mixing bowl.
4. Mix well by hand.
5. Roll meat mixture into golf ball-sized meatballs.
6. Squish a mozzarella cube into the center of each and pull the edges of the meat around it to enclose.
7. Pour 1 jar of spaghetti sauce into the bottom of a slow cooker.
8. Arrange meatballs on top and cover with the second jar of sauce.
9. Cover and cook on High until no longer pink in the centers, 2 to 2 1/2 hours.
