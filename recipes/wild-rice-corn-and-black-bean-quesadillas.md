## Ingredients

- 1 (8.8 ounce) package UNCLE BEN'S® Ready Rice® Long Grain & Wild
- 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup reduced-sodium black beans, rinsed and drained
- ½ cup frozen sweet corn, thawed
- 2 each green onions, chopped
- 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- ½ cup mild salsa, plus more for serving if desired

## Instructions

1. Prepare rice according to package directions.
2. Mix rice, shredded cheese, beans, corn, green onions, together in a bowl.
3. Place a quarter of the mixture on one side of each tortilla.
4. Top with about 2 tablespoons of mild salsa.
5. Gently fold each tortilla in half.
6. Spray a small saute pan with non-stick cooking spray; set over medium-high heat.
7. Place one quesadilla in saute pan.
8. Cook until lightly golden brown, 1 to 2 minutes on each side.
9. Spray the pan with additional oil, as needed, to cook the next 3 quesadillas.
10. Let quesadillas cool slightly and cut in half.
11. Serve with additional mild salsa, if desired.
