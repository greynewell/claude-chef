## Ingredients

- 10  large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 3 ounces fresh crabmeat
- ½ (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 8  flat taco shells
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup sour cream, for topping
- ¼ cup salsa, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Bring 1 quart of water to a boil in a large pot.
3. Place shrimp and crab in a steamer basket on top of the pot without touching water; cover.
4. Steam until shrimp are pink and cooked through, about 5 minutes.
5. Remove shrimp and crab meat from steamer and chop.
6. Combine seafood mixture and cream cheese in a bowl.
7. Lay taco shells on a baking sheet and top with seafood-cream cheese mixture.
8. Sprinkle Cheddar cheese on top.
9. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is completely melted, about 15 minutes.
10. Remove from oven.
11. Cut each taco into 4 pieces using a pizza cutter and top with sour cream and salsa.
