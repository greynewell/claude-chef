## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds seashell pasta
- 1 (6 ounce) can pitted black olives, chopped
- 2 large tomatoes, chopped
- 4 ounces sliced pepperoni sausage, each slice cut in half
- ½ cup chopped green olives
- 3 bunches green onions, chopped
- 2 cups chopped ham
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle zesty Italian dressing

## Instructions

1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.
2. In a large bowl, combine the pasta, black olives, tomatoes, pepperoni, green olives, green onions and ham or turkey.
3. Before serving, add shredded mozzarella and dressing.
4. Toss well and serve.
