## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground chicken
- ¼ cup panko bread crumbs
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- 1  egg, beaten
- 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
- 1 cup Ragu® Old World Style® Traditional Pasta Sauce
- 12  slider rolls, halved horizontally

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Combine chicken, bread crumbs, garlic, oregano, salt, egg and mozzarella together in a bowl.
3. Mix well and roll into 12 (1 1/2-inch) meatballs.
4. Place meatballs in an oven-proof skillet.
5. Bake until meatballs are cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes.
6. Heat Ragu® Old World Style® Pasta Sauce until warmed through.
7. Toast the rolls.
8. To make a slider, spoon sauce on the bottom of a slider bun.
9. Top with meatball and top of bun.
10. Serve.
