## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- ¼ cup plain Greek yogurt
- ½ teaspoon stevia brown sugar blend
- ⅛ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon chocolate chips, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Microwave peanut butter in a microwave-safe bowl until liquid and smooth, about 20 seconds.
2. Stir Greek yogurt into the peanut butter with a spoon until smooth, scraping off any peanut butter stuck to the spoon. Stir in stevia and vanilla extract. Fold in chocolate chips.
