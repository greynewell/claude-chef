## Ingredients

- 3 pounds chicken wings, cut apart at joints, wing tips discarded
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1 ¾ cups Swanson® Chicken Stock
- 3 tablespoons red curry paste (such as Thai Kitchen®)
- 1 tablespoon sriracha hot chile sauce, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon fish sauce  (Optional)
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ cup chopped cilantro  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Line a rimmed baking sheet with foil.
3. Place the wings on a baking rack and set on prepared baking sheet.
4. Bake until wing pieces are crispy and golden brown, 60 to 70 minutes.
5. Whisk cornstarch and Swanson® Chicken Stock together in a bowl until smooth.
6. Pour sauce mixture into a saucepan and bring to a simmer; add red curry paste, sriracha, brown sugar, fish sauce, and garlic powder.
7. Whisk until smooth; bring to a boil.
8. Reduce heat and simmer until sauce reaches your desired thickness, about 10 minutes.
9. Toss wings in sauce, garnish with cilantro, and serve immediately.
