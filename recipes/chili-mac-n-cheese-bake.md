## Ingredients

- 8 ounces uncooked macaroni
- 1 (15 ounce) can HORMEL® Chili With Beans
- 1 ½ cups CHI-CHI's® Thick & Chunky Salsa
- 1 medium green pepper, chopped
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 2 cups shredded marble Jack cheese, divided

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Lightly grease 2 1/2-quart baking dish.
3. Cook macaroni according to package directions; drain well.
4. Pour into large bowl.
5. Toss chili, salsa, green pepper and chili powder with macaroni until well mixed.
6. Stir in 1 1/2 cups cheese.
7. Spoon into prepared casserole.
8. Top with remaining 1/2 cups cheese.
9. Bake 30 to 35 minutes or until hot and cheese is melted and lightly browned.
