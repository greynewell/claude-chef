## Ingredients

- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 2 teaspoons white wine vinegar
- 1 ½ teaspoons grated fresh ginger root
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- 4 (19 ounce) cans garbanzo beans, drained
- 2  yellow bell peppers, seeded and chopped
- 1 bunch green onions, thinly sliced
- ½ cup finely chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. In a bowl, crush together the garlic and salt.
2. To prepare the dressing, mix in olive oil, lemon juice, vinegar, ginger, cumin, and cayenne pepper.
3. In a separate bowl, stir together the garbanzo beans, yellow bell peppers, green onions, and cilantro.
4. Mix in the dressing.
5. Cover, and chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight.
