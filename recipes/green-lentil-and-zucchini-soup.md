## Ingredients

- 4 cups chopped zucchini
- 1  onion, minced
- 4  carrots, chopped
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 tablespoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
- 1 (28 ounce) can no-salt-added tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons beef bouillon granules
- 6 cups water
- 2 cups cooked green lentils
- 2 cups ditalini pasta, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix zucchini, onion, and carrots in a bowl.
2. Add enough water to cover and soak for 15 minutes; drain.
3. Heat butter in a large stockpot over high heat; cook and stir zucchini, onion, carrots, garlic powder, and Italian seasoning until onion is transparent, about 7 minutes.
4. Add tomatoes and beef bouillon to zucchini mixture; cook and stir until heated through, about 7 minutes.
5. Mix water, lentils, and ditalini pasta into zucchini mixture; reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until pasta is tender, 20 to 30 minutes.
