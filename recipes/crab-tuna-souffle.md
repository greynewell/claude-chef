## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) can crabmeat, drained and flaked
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
- 4 cups cubed French bread
- 8 ounces Muenster cheese, cubed
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 4  eggs
- 3 cups milk
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 2 teaspoons mustard powder
- 1 teaspoon grated onion

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, stir together the crabmeat and tuna.
2. Place a layer of bread cubes in the bottom of a 1 1/2 quart casserole dish.
3. Cover with a layer of the crab and tuna, then a layer of cheese and a sprinkle of parsley.
4. Repeat layers 2 more times ending with cheese and parsley on top.
5. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, butter, mustard powder and onion.
6. Pour over the layers in the dish.
7. Cover and refrigerate for at least 3 hours, or overnight.
8. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
9. Let the dish come to room temperature while the oven preheats.
10. Bake uncovered for 1 hour and 15 minutes in the preheated oven, or until puffed and golden.
