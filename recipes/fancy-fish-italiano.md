## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 cup thinly sliced onion
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 ½ teaspoons Italian seasoning, divided
- 1 cup 1/2-inch sliced zucchini
- 1 cup 1/2-inch sliced yellow squash
- ¼ cup chopped black olives
- ¼ cup capers  (Optional)
- 4 (4 ounce) cod fillets
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion in the hot oil until almost tender, about 5 minutes.
2. Add garlic and 1/2 teaspoon Italian seasoning to onion; cook and stir until garlic is fragrant, 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Stir zucchini, yellow squash, olives, and capers into onion mixture; top with cod fillets.
4. Sprinkle tomatoes, remaining 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper onto cod fillets.
5. Reduce heat, cover skillet, and cook until fish flakes easily with a fork and zucchini is tender, about 10 minutes.
6. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese over fish; cover skillet until cheese melts, about 2 minutes.
