## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground turkey
- 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
- ½ cup kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the grill to medium-high heat.
2. Combine turkey, feta cheese, olives, oregano, and pepper in a large bowl. Mix together, and form into patties.
3. Lightly oil the grate. Place patties on the grill. Cook for 10 to 12 minutes, turning halfway through.
