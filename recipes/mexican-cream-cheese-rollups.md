## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- ⅔ cup pitted green olives, chopped
- 1 (2.25 ounce) can black olives, chopped
- 6  green onions, chopped
- 8 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- ½ cup salsa

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix together cream cheese, mayonnaise, green olives, black olives and green onions.
2. Spread cream cheese mixture in a thin layer onto each tortilla.
3. Roll up tortillas.
4. Chill about 1 hour, or until the filling is firm.
5. Slice chilled rollups into 1 inch pieces.
6. Serve with salsa for dipping.
