## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 bunch kale, chopped
- 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
- 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas
- salt and freshly ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
3. Drain, reserving about 1 cup of cooking water.
4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat and cook garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute.
5. Add kale and cook, stirring constantly, until wilted, about 3 minutes.
6. Stir cooked spaghetti into the skillet.
7. Add nutritional yeast.
8. Add enough of the reserved cooking water to create a thick sauce.
9. Stir well.
10. Add chickpeas and heat until warmed, 2 to 4 minutes.
11. Season with salt and pepper.
