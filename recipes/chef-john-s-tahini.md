## Ingredients

- ½ cup tahini (sesame seed paste)
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil  (Optional)
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1 pinch salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons warm water, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Whisk tahini, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and salt together in a bowl until smooth and thick.
2. Add warm water a splash at a time, whisking after each addition, until desired consistency is reached.
3. Season with salt.
4. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until flavors blend, at least 3 hours.
