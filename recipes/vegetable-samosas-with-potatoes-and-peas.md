## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds russet potatoes, peeled and diced
- ½ cup frozen peas
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 6 tablespoons ice water, or more if needed
- ¼ cup butter
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or as needed
- ½ cup chopped yellow onion
- 4 teaspoons ginger-garlic paste
- 1 squeeze lemon juice
- 1 cup vegetable oil for frying, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Place potatoes into a large pot and cover with salted water; bring to a boil.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer until just tender, about 10 minutes.
3. Meanwhile, place peas in a microwave-safe bowl and cook in the microwave until heated through, 1 to 3 minutes.
4. Set aside.
5. Combine garam masala, salt, turmeric, and cayenne pepper in a bowl and set spice mixture aside.
6. Mix flour and salt together in a bowl.
7. Drizzle in oil and rub in using your fingers.
8. Add about 6 tablespoons ice water and work it into the dough.
9. Add additional ice water as necessary to create a firm dough.
10. Knead for 1 to 2 minutes and put aside to rest for 30 minutes.
11. Meanwhile, drain potatoes.
12. Heat butter and oil in a large frying pan.
13. Add onion and ginger-garlic paste; cook and stir until onions are translucent and beginning to brown, 5 to 10 minutes.
14. Add spice mixture and stir to combine.
15. Add cooked potatoes and stir.
16. Mash using a fork or potato masher to the desired consistency.
17. Add cooked peas and lemon juice; mix filling until well combined.
18. Knead dough and tear off approximately 10 equal-sized pieces.
19. Roll each piece into a ball and roll each ball into a thin circle about 6 inches in diameter.
20. Cut dough circles in half.
21. Add a spoon of filling to each half-circle; use a finger dipped in water to wet the edges of the dough and press edges together using a fork to seal.
22. Heat oil in a large frying pan over medium-high heat.
23. Fry samosas in the hot oil until golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes each.
