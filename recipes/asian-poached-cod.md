## Ingredients

- 1 pound cod, cut into 2-inch chunks
- 6 tablespoons water
- 5 tablespoons soy sauce
- 3  green onions, diced
- 1 (2 inch) piece ginger, cut into thin strips
- 4 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil
- ¾ teaspoon red pepper flakes, or more to taste
- ¼ teaspoon Chinese five-spice powder  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Arrange cod into the bottom of a saucepan.
2. Mix water, soy sauce, green onions, ginger, garlic, sesame oil, red pepper flakes, and Chinese five-spice powder together in a bowl; pour over the cod.
3. Place saucepan over high heat and bring liquid to a boil; reduce heat to low and cook at a simmer until cod flakes easily with a fork, 12 to 15 minutes.
4. Remove cod from liquid with a slotted spoon.
