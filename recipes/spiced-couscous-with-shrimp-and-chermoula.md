## Ingredients

- 2 ¼ teaspoons McCormick® Cumin, Ground
- ¾ teaspoon McCormick® Cinnamon, Ground
- ¾ teaspoon McCormick® Turmeric, Ground
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
- 1 clove garlic
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- 1 cup water
- ¾ cup couscous
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ½ pound shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. For the Spice Blend, mix all ingredients in small bowl.
2. Set aside.
3. For the Chermoula, place cilantro, garlic and lemon juice in blender container; cover.
4. Blend until almost smooth.
5. Gradually add oil with machine running.
6. Blend until smooth.
7. Mix in 1 1/4 teaspoons of the Spice Blend.
8. Keep refrigerated until ready to serve.
9. For the Couscous, bring water to boil in small saucepan.
10. Stir in remaining ingredients and another 1 1/4 teaspoons of the Spice Blend.
11. Cover.
12. Remove from heat.
13. Let stand 5 minutes.
14. Fluff with fork.
15. For the Shrimp, toss shrimp with remaining Spice Blend in medium bowl until evenly coated.
16. Heat oil in medium skillet on high heat.
17. Add shrimp; cook 1 minute per side or just until shrimp turn pink.
18. To serve, spoon Chermoula into serving bowl.
19. Top with couscous and shrimp.
20. Garnish with cilantro, if desired.
