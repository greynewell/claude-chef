## Ingredients

- 2 cups shredded cooked chicken
- 1  mango - peeled, seeded and diced
- 2 tablespoons minced red onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- ½  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- 2  limes, juiced, divided
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- kosher salt to taste
- 2  ripe avocados - halved, pitted, and peeled
- 24  large corn tortilla chips

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken, mango, onion, cilantro, and jalapeno together in a bowl.
2. Drizzle the juice from 1 lime, olive oil, and kosher salt over the chicken mixture and mix.
3. Mash avocados in a bowl with remaining juice from 1 lime and kosher salt to taste.
4. Top each tortilla chip with a dollop of the avocado mixture and a dollop of the chicken mixture and place on a platter.
