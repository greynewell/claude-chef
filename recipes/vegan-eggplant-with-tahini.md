## Ingredients

- 2 large eggplants
- ¼ cup tahini
- 2  lemons, juiced
- 1 clove garlic, grated
- 2 tablespoons pomegranate molasses, divided
- salt
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil, or more to taste
- ½ bunch fresh parsley, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Place eggplant on a baking sheet and poke numerous times with the tip of a sharp knife.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until eggplant is soft, mushy, and dark brown, about 45 minutes.
4. Allow to cool, about 1 hour.
5. Peel eggplant and cut flesh into large cubes.
6. Place into a bowl.
7. Mix tahini, lemon juice, and grated garlic in a cup.
8. Add 1 tablespoon pomegranate molasses and salt; mix well.
9. Pour tahini sauce over eggplant and mix well.
10. Drizzle with remaining 1 tablespoon pomegranate molasses and olive oil.
11. Sprinkle parsley on top.
