## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 large red bell pepper, chopped
- 4 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese, divided

## Instructions

1. Heat a large nonstick skillet over medium heat.
2. Add ground beef; cook and stir until browned, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Stir in black beans and red bell pepper; cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
4. Cut four 13x13-inch pieces of aluminum foil.
5. Place 1 tortilla on each piece of aluminum foil.
6. Spoon beef mixture in a line down the center of each tortilla.
7. Divide cheese evenly among tortillas.
8. Fold opposing edges of each tortilla to overlap the filling.
9. Roll up in the shape of a burrito, sealing ends.
10. Rinse and dry 4 half-gallon wax-lined milk cartons.
11. Cut several 1-inch diamond-shaped vents into 2 corners of each milk carton, near the bottom.
12. Place a wrapped burrito inside each carton.
13. Place milk cartons in a safe cooking area outdoors.
14. Light each on fire at one of the vents; cook until cartons burn down, about 2 minutes.
15. Gently unwrap burritos with heatproof gloves.
