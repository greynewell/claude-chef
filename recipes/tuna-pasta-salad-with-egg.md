## Ingredients

- ½ (16 ounce) package penne pasta
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 (7 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ½  lemon, juiced
- 2 tablespoons capers
- ⅓ cup sweet corn, drained
- ½  red bell pepper, finely chopped
- ¼  cucumber, finely chopped
- 2  hard-boiled eggs, quartered

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Place penne in a large mixing bowl and toss well with olive oil.
5. Combine tuna, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and capers in the bowl of a food processor; blend until smooth and creamy.
6. Add to the penne and mix until well combined.
7. Add corn, bell pepper, and cucumber and toss together.
8. Spoon into a serving dish.
9. Arrange quartered eggs on top.
