## Ingredients

- ¾ cup milk
- ¾ cup all-purpose flour
- 1  egg
- ½ cup scallions
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- 2 teaspoons sesame oil

## Instructions

1. Combine milk, flour, egg, scallions, water, baking powder, salt, and ground ginger in a bowl. Mix until batter is smooth.
2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Pour batter into the skillet to make 4 pancakes. Cook until bubbly, about 2 minutes. Flip and cook until desired brownness is reached, about 1 minute more.
