## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh garlic
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 small purple eggplants, cut into 1/2-inch slices
- 1  tomato, sliced 1/8-inch thick
- 1 (8 ounce) package fresh mozzarella, thinly sliced
- cooking spray
- 3 leaves fresh basil, cut into thin strips

## Instructions

1. Combine olive oil, garlic, and salt in a small bowl; set aside.
2. Preheat an outdoor grill to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C) and lightly oil the grate.
3. Put eggplant slices on the hot grill and cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Turn eggplant and brush with the oil mixture.
5. Place 1 slice of tomato and 1 slice of mozzarella cheese on each eggplant piece.
6. Close the lid and cook until cheese has melted, 3 to 4 minutes.
7. Transfer to a plate and sprinkle with basil.
