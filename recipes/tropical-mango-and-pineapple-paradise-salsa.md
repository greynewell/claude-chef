## Ingredients

- ½  fresh pineapple - peeled, cored, and chopped
- 1  mango, peeled, seeded and chopped
- ⅓ cup cilantro leaves
- ¼ cup key lime juice
- 1 small red onion, coarsely chopped

## Instructions

1. Place the pineapple, mango, cilantro, lime juice, and red onion in a food processor.
2. Pulse until ingredients are combined and chopped to desired texture, about 4 or 5 times.
