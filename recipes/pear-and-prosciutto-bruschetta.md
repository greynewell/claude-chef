## Ingredients

- 1  pear, cored and diced
- ⅓ cup diced red onion
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh thyme
- 1 (4 ounce) package crumbled blue cheese
- 1  French baguette, sliced and toasted
- ⅓ pound sliced prosciutto, cut into thin strips

## Instructions

1. Mix pear, onion, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, and thyme together in a bowl; set aside to marinate for 5 minutes.
2. Spread blue cheese onto toasted baguette slices.
3. Spoon pear mixture onto the blue cheese.
4. Top with prosciutto.
