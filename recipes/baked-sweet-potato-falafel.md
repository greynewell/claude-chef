## Ingredients

- 2  sweet potatoes
- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
- 1  egg
- 2 cups all-purpose flour, or more if needed
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon ground coriander

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease a baking sheet.
3. Puncture sweet potatoes several times with a fork.
4. Cook in microwave on High for 5 minutes.
5. Flip and cook until potatoes are easily pierced with a fork, about 3 to 5 more minutes; cool.
6. Peel skin off and cut sweet potatoes into large chunks.
7. Mash garbanzo beans with a fork or potato masher in a bowl.
8. Stir sweet potatoes and egg into mashed garbanzo beans to form a smooth consistency.
9. Stir 2 cups flour, garlic powder, cumin, and coriander into sweet potato mixture to form a cookie dough consistency.
10. Adjust with more flour if necessary.
11. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place on prepared baking sheet.
12. Slightly flatten balls into patties.
13. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 30 minutes, flipping after 15 minutes.
