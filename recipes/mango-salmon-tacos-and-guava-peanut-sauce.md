## Ingredients

- olive oil cooking spray
- 1 (6 ounce) salmon fillet
- 1  mango, peeled and cut into 4 pieces
- 3 tablespoons guava juice
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons peanut butter
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 4 (6 inch) corn tortillas
- 1  carrot, shredded
- 1 leaf kale, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Spray a large piece of aluminum foil with cooking spray; place salmon on top.
3. Lay mango pieces over salmon.
4. Wrap up salmon and mango with aluminum foil; transfer to a baking sheet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until salmon flakes easily with a fork, 30 to 40 minutes.
6. Cool until easily handled, about 5 minutes.
7. Flake salmon into pieces and chop mango.
8. Combine guava juice, soy sauce, peanut butter, and sugar in a blender; blend until smooth.
9. Place corn tortillas on a microwave-safe plate and cover with a dampened paper towel.
10. Heat in the microwave until warm, about 20 seconds.
11. Stack 2 corn tortillas on each serving plate.
12. Divide salmon, mango, carrot, and kale between each plate.
13. Drizzle peanut sauce on top.
