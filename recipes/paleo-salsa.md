## Ingredients

- 6  roma tomatoes, coarsely chopped
- ½  red onion, coarsely chopped
- 1  lime, juiced
- ½ bunch cilantro, chopped
- 1  jalapeno pepper, chopped
- 1  habanero pepper, chopped  (Optional)
- 1 clove garlic, chopped
- ground black pepper, to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine tomatoes, onion, lime juice, cilantro, jalapeno pepper, habanero pepper, garlic, and ground black pepper in a food processor.
2. Pulse several times until desired texture is reached.
