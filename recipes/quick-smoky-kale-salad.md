## Ingredients

- 1 ⅓ pounds kale, stems removed and leaves chopped
- 2 ⅓ cups frozen baby lima beans, thawed
- ⅔ cup grated Gouda cheese
- ⅔ cup pitted and chopped Kalamata olives
- ⅔ cup thinly-sliced sun-dried tomatoes
- 1 ⅓ cups olive oil
- ½ cup lemon juice
- 2 ⅔ tablespoons smoked paprika
- 2 ½ tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon ground chipotle chile pepper
- 2 ⅔ teaspoons balsamic vinegar
- 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
- 1 ¾ teaspoons kosher salt

## Instructions

1. Mix kale, lima beans, Gouda cheese, Kalamata olives, and sun-dried tomatoes together in a bowl.
2. Whisk olive oil, lemon juice, paprika, honey, chipotle pepper, balsamic vinegar, black pepper, and kosher salt together in a bowl until dressing is smooth; pour over kale mixture and toss to coat.
