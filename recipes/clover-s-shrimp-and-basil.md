## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 2 teaspoons dark sesame oil
- 2 tablespoons grated fresh ginger root
- 2 large cloves garlic, minced
- ¾ teaspoon hot red pepper flakes
- 1 pound medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- 1  yellow bell pepper, chopped
- ½ cup shredded fresh basil leaves

## Instructions

1. Heat the olive oil and sesame oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Mix in the ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes.
3. Cook and stir 5 minutes.
4. Stir the shrimp, red bell pepper, and yellow bell pepper into the skillet.
5. Over high heat, cook 3 to 4 minutes, until opaque.
6. Turn off heat, and mix in the basil leaves.
