## Ingredients

- 2 (5 ounce) cans tuna, drained
- ½ large carrot, shredded
- ⅓ cup chopped golden raisins
- ⅓ cup chopped peanuts
- ¼ cup chopped red onion
- ¼ cup chopped red bell pepper
- ¼ cup finely chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 tablespoon chopped canned jalapeno peppers, or to taste, juice reserved
- ⅓ cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup cream cheese, softened
- ½  lime, juiced
- ¼ teaspoon curry powder, or to taste
- 2 large flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. Combine tuna, carrot, raisins, peanuts, red onion, red bell pepper, cilantro, and jalapeno peppers in a bowl.
2. Stir mayonnaise, cream cheese, lime juice, curry powder, and jalapeno juice (to taste) together in a separate bowl; mix into tuna mixture until evenly combined. Spread tuna mixture onto tortillas and roll tortilla around filling. Refrigerate wraps for 1 hour before slicing.
