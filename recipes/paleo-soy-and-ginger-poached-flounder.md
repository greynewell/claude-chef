## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups water
- ½ cup soy sauce
- ½ cup chopped cilantro
- 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 ½ pounds flounder fillets
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
- ½ cup chopped scallions

## Instructions

1. Stir water, soy sauce, cilantro, and rice vinegar together in a bowl.
2. Heat olive oil in a deep skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Saute ginger in hot oil until fragrant, about 1 minute; add the water mixture and flounder to the skillet.
4. Bring the liquid to a boil and immediately remove skillet from heat.
5. Place a tight-fitting lid on the skillet and poach flounder in hot liquid until the flesh easily flakes with a fork, about 10 minutes.
6. Top fillets with chopped scallions.
