## Ingredients

- ½ teaspoon minced garlic
- 1 cup diced fresh tomatoes
- ½ cup thinly chopped apples (such as Honeycrisp)
- 2 tablespoons chopped jicama
- 2 teaspoons coarsely chopped cilantro
- 1 teaspoon chopped fresh chives
- ½ teaspoon finely chopped fresh basil
- ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 1 ½ teaspoons white vinegar

## Instructions

1. Cook and stir garlic in a skillet over medium-low heat until fragrant, about 1 minute.
2. Stir tomatoes, apples, and jicama into garlic; cook and stir until tomatoes are hot, 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Stir cilantro, chives, basil, and salt into tomato mixture; drizzle lime juice and vinegar over the top.
4. Cook and stir until apples are juicy but still crisp and tomatoes are warm but still firm, 3 to 5 minutes.
5. Drain some of the accumulated juices and transfer salsa to a bowl; refrigerate until chilled.
