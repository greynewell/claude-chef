## Ingredients

- 2  avocados, chopped
- 2 tablespoons salsa
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- ¼ teaspoon chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, mix together avocados, salsa, mayonnaise, chili powder and black pepper.
2. Mix well and salt to taste before serving.
