## Ingredients

- 1 head iceberg lettuce
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ½  onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 12 ounces raw shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2 medium zucchini, chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, diced
- 1 (8 ounce) can bamboo shoots, chopped
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon Chinese chili sauce  (Optional)
- ¼ teaspoon wasabi paste
- ½ cup Szechuan-flavored slivered almonds (such as Blue Diamond®)

## Instructions

1. Clean lettuce and peel off leaves to use as "cups." Set aside.
2. Add olive oil, onion, garlic, and shrimp to a wok or large frying pan.
3. Cook over medium heat until shrimp are pink, stirring frequently, about 5 minutes.
4. Add zucchini, water chestnuts, bamboo shoots, soy sauce, and ginger.
5. Mix and cook, stirring often, 5 to 8 minutes more.
6. Meanwhile, combine soy sauce, chili sauce, and wasabi paste in a small bowl.
7. Add almonds to shrimp mixture.
8. Spoon scoopfuls into lettuce cups, add wasabi sauce, wrap, and enjoy.
