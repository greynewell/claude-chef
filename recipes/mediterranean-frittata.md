## Ingredients

- 3  sun-dried tomato halves
- 2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil
- ¼  yellow onion, minced
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup frozen chopped spinach
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can sliced mushrooms with pieces, drained
- 3 ounces crumbled reduced-fat feta cheese
- 6  egg whites
- ¼ cup skim milk
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon dried basil
- 2 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease an 8-inch round pan.
3. Place sun-dried tomatoes into a bowl of warm water until rehydrated, about 10 minutes.
4. Drain and chop.
5. Heat olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion and garlic until onion is translucent, about 10 minutes.
6. Add spinach; cook and stir until thawed and water is evaporated, about 5 minutes.
7. Stir in mushrooms, sun-dried tomatoes, and feta cheese until well mixed.
8. Whisk egg whites, skim milk, salt, pepper, and basil together in a bowl until very frothy.
9. Carefully stir spinach mixture and 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese into egg mixture.
10. Pour into the prepared pan; top with remaining Parmesan cheese.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until frittata is set and browned on top, about 25 minutes.
