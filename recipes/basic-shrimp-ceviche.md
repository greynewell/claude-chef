## Ingredients

- 4 pounds large shrimp -- thawed, peeled, deveined, and chopped into small pieces
- 3 pounds limes, juiced
- 12  Roma tomatoes
- 3 large cucumbers, peeled
- 1  red onion
- ½ cup cilantro, or to taste
- 4 large  oranges, divided
- ½ cup ketchup, or to taste
- 1 dash hot sauce (such as Cholula®), or to taste
- 1 (14.5 ounce) package tortilla chips
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Place shrimp in a 9x13-inch pan.
2. Pour lime juice over shrimp so they are completely saturated and none are exposed.
3. Refrigerate 4 hours to overnight.
4. Chop tomatoes, cucumbers, red onion, and cilantro into small pieces.
5. Add to the pan with the shrimp.
6. Peel and chop 3 oranges and add to the shrimp.
7. Squeeze the juice of 1 orange into the mixture.
8. Add ketchup and hot sauce and mix well.
9. Taste, using a tortilla chip as a spoon.
10. Season with salt, if desired.
