## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds cubed beef stew meat
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans Italian-style diced tomatoes
- 1 (14 ounce) can beef broth
- 4  carrots, chopped
- 2  potatoes, peeled and chopped
- ¾ teaspoon dried thyme
- 2 tablespoons Dijon-style prepared mustard
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine beef and flour in a large resealable plastic bag and toss to coat evenly.
2. In a 6-quart saucepan, brown beef in hot vegetable oil. Season with salt and pepper, if desired.
3. Add diced tomatoes, beef broth, carrots, potatoes, and thyme. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer for 1 hour or until beef is tender. Blend in mustard and serve.
