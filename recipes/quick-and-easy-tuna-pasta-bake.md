## Ingredients

- 11 ounces penne pasta
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 4 tablespoons spaghetti sauce with meat
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 1 pinch dried Italian herbs, or to taste
- ⅔ cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 teaspoon dry bread crumbs, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
3. Drain and return penne to the pot.
4. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
5. Mix tuna, spaghetti sauce, salt, pepper, and Italian herbs into the drained penne.
6. Pour penne mixture into an oven-proof dish.
7. Sprinkle with Cheddar cheese and bread crumbs.
8. Broil in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and breadcrumbs are crispy, about 5 minutes.
