## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package dry penne pasta
- ¾ (4 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 ½ ounces Gorgonzola cheese
- 2 tablespoons cream
- ½ pound sugar snap peas, trimmed
- 2 tablespoons butter
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil over medium-high heat.
2. Add the pasta, and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes.
3. Drain, cover pot, and set aside.
4. Stir the cream cheese, Gorgonzola cheese, and cream together in a bowl until smooth.
5. Set aside.
6. Bring another large pot of lightly salted water to a boil over medium-high heat.
7. Add the sugar snap peas, and cook until crisp, 1 to 2 minutes.
8. Drain.
9. Toss peas with butter.
10. Stir cream cheese mixture into peas, and toss to coat evenly.
11. Serve immediately over cooked pasta.
