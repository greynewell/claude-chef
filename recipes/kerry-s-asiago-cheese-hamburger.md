## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef sirloin
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ cup buffalo wing sauce
- 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 teaspoon sea salt
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ½ cup sweet onion, sliced
- ¼ cup barbeque sauce
- 4  hamburger buns, split
- ¼ cup Additional ketchup
- ½ cup grated Asiago cheese
- 8 slices cooked bacon

## Instructions

1. Mix the beef, Worcestershire sauce, buffalo wing sauce, pepper, salt, and garlic powder in a large bowl.
2. Form mixture into four patties.
3. Place the patties on a plate; cover.
4. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
5. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
6. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
7. Cook and stir the onion in the olive oil until translucent, about 5 minutes; set aside.
8. Cook the beef patties on the preheated grill on one side for about 5 minutes; brush the top of each burger with 1 tablespoon of barbeque sauce; flip.
9. Cook burgers until well done, or the internal temperature reaches 180 degrees F (85 degrees C), about five minutes more.
10. Place one burger on the bottom half of each bun.
11. Top each burger with cooked onions, ketchup, Asiago cheese, and bacon slices; sandwich with top of bun and serve.
