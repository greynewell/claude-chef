## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- ½ cup white wine
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon dried parsley

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven's broiler, and set the oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source.
2. Heat the oil in a large, oven-proof skillet over medium heat; add the garlic, and cook until lightly browned, about 1 minute.
3. Stir in the shrimp and the tomatoes.
4. Cook just until the shrimp turn pink, 3 to 5 minutes.
5. Pour in wine; simmer for 2 additional minutes.
6. Top shrimp with mozzarella cheese, and broil until the cheese is melted and golden.
7. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and dried parsley before serving.
