## Ingredients

- ¼ cup ghee
- ½  onion, finely chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 head cauliflower, grated
- 1 cup trimmed asparagus, cut into 1-inch lengths
- ½ cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Melt ghee in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add onion and garlic; cook until tender, about 3 minutes.
3. Stir in grated cauliflower; cook for 3 minutes more.
4. Add asparagus and cook until tender, about 3 minutes.
5. Stir heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, salt, pepper, and nutmeg into the skillet; cook over medium heat until creamy, 5 to 7 minutes.
