## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package truRoots® Ancient Grains Organic Penne
- ½ cup prepared fresh basil pesto
- 1 (8 ounce) container mozzarella pearls (perline)*
- 1 pint grape or cherry tomatoes, halved
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 (2.25 ounce) can sliced black olives, drained
- 4 sprigs Fresh basil sprigs, for garnish

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta in large pot of boiling salted water, just until al dente, about 7 to 9 minutes.
2. Drain.
3. Combine pasta and pesto in large bowl.
4. Stir in mozzarella, tomatoes, Parmesan and olives.
5. Chill until ready to serve.
6. Serve garnished with basil sprigs, if desired.
