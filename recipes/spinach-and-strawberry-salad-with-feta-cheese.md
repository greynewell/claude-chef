## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package fresh strawberries, hulled and quartered
- 1 (10 ounce) package fresh spinach
- 1 (8 ounce) package feta cheese, cut into squares
- ½ cup olive oil
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup white balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
- 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
- ¼ teaspoon paprika

## Instructions

1. Toss strawberries, spinach, and feta cheese together in a large bowl.
2. Whisk olive oil, brown sugar, white balsamic vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, and paprika together in a bowl; drizzle over salad and toss to coat.
