## Ingredients

- 13 ounces spaghetti
- 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 1  onion, sliced
- 1 cup chopped kale
- 1  hot pepper, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2  tomatoes, chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, sliced
- 3 ½ ounces peeled and deveined shrimp
- 2 cups chopped Swiss chard
- 1 cup chopped green beans
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 tablespoon fresh thyme
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, about 10 minutes.
3. Drain, reserving some of the cooking liquid.
4. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
5. Add onion and kale; cook and stir until softened, about 5 minutes.
6. Add remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil, hot pepper, and garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
7. Stir in tomatoes, red bell pepper, shrimp, Swiss chard, and green beans.
8. Cook until shrimp is opaque, about 5 minutes.
9. Stir spaghetti into the skillet, pouring in some of the reserved cooking liquid if mixture is too dry.
10. Add balsamic vinegar, thyme, salt, and black pepper.
11. Cook until flavors combine, 3 to 5 minutes.
