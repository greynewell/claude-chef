## Ingredients

- 1  cucumber
- 1 head iceberg lettuce, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 1 head romaine, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 4  plum tomatoes, cut into wedges
- ½ head chicory, cut into bite-sized pieces
- ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil, or to taste
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon celery seed
- ½ teaspoon garlic salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon ground ginger
- ¼ cup pignoli (pine nuts)  (Optional)
- ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Run a fork down the length of the cucumber to create thin stripes. Cut cucumber into thin slices.
2. Mix cucumber, iceberg lettuce, romaine, tomatoes, and chicory together in a large bowl. Add olive oil and balsamic vinegar; toss to coat. Season with Italian seasoning, lemon juice, celery seed, garlic salt, pepper, and ginger.
3. Garnish salad with pignoli and Parmesan cheese.
