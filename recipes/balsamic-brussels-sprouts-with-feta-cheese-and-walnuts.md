## Ingredients

- 1 pound Brussels sprouts, trimmed and halved
- 1 large red onion, chopped
- 6 cloves garlic, chopped
- ⅓ cup balsamic vinegar
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup walnuts
- ¼ cup sweetened dried cranberries
- 2 ounces crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Combine Brussels sprouts, red onion, and garlic in a large bowl. Drizzle balsamic vinegar, olive oil, and maple syrup over the sprouts mixture and toss to coat. Season vegetables with salt and pepper; pour onto a baking sheet.
3. Roast vegetable mixture in preheated oven until the sprouts are tender and the sauce thickened, about 40 minutes.
4. Transfer vegetables and sauce to a large serving bowl; add walnuts, cranberries, and feta cheese and stir to mix.
