## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup lime juice
- 2 tablespoons tahini, or more to taste
- 1 clove garlic, peeled
- 1 tablespoon water, or as needed  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin

## Instructions

1. Place chickpeas in a blender; top with olive oil, lime juice, tahini, and garlic.
2. Blend mixture until smooth.
3. Add water 1 tablespoon at a time and blend until desired consistency is reached.
4. Add paprika, cayenne pepper, and cumin; blend until incorporated.
