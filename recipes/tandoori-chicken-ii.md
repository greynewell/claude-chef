## Ingredients

- ½ teaspoon curry powder
- ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
- 2 tablespoons water
- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill for high heat.
2. In a medium bowl, mix curry powder, red pepper flakes, salt, ginger, paprika, cinnamon, and turmeric with water to form a smooth paste.
3. Rub paste into chicken breasts, and place them on a plate.
4. Cover, and allow to marinate for 20 minutes.
5. Brush grate with oil.
6. Place chicken on the grill, and cook 6 to 8 minutes on each side, until juices run clear when pierced with a fork.
