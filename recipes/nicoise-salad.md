## Ingredients

- 1 (20 ounce) package frozen roasted red potatoes
- 1 (12 ounce) package frozen whole green beans
- 4  large eggs
- ¾ cup dry-packed sun-dried tomatoes, halved
- 3 tablespoons white balsamic or white wine vinegar
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon dried thyme
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- 2 (5 ounce) cans solid-packed tuna in oil, drained
- ½ cup pitted kalamata olives

## Instructions

1. Cook potatoes and beans according to package directions.
2. Meanwhile, put eggs in a saucepan, cover with cold water, and bring to a boil.
3. Cover, remove from heat, and let stand 12 minutes.
4. Drain, run eggs under cold water, and peel.
5. Halve each lengthwise.
6. Blanch tomatoes in a pot of boiling water to soften, 30 seconds.
7. Drain well.
8. Whisk together vinegar, mustard, salt, pepper, and thyme in a bowl.
9. Gradually add oil, whisking until combined well.
10. Arrange potatoes, beans, tomatoes, eggs, tuna, and olives on a platter and drizzle dressing over top.
