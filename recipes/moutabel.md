## Ingredients

- 4  medium eggplants
- 4 cloves garlic
- ¼ cup fresh basil
- 2  fresh green chile peppers
- ¼ cup tahini
- 4 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 sprig fresh mint
- 1 teaspoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a medium baking sheet.
3. Place eggplants on the baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven 30 minutes, or until soft and seared.
5. In a food processor, grind together garlic, basil and green chile peppers.
6. Scoop eggplant from skins and mix with garlic mixture in the food processor.
7. Transfer the mixture to a medium serving dish.
8. With a fork, mash in tahini, lemon juice and salt.
9. Garnish with mint and olive oil.
