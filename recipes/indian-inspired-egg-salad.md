## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- ½ small red onion, in small dice
- 1  jalapeno, seeded and minced
- ¼ teaspoon ginger
- ¼ teaspoon turmeric
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 6  hard-cooked eggs, peeled and cut into large dice
- 1 small tomato, cut into small dice
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Saute onion until tender, about 4 minutes.
3. Add jalapeno, ginger, turmeric and cayenne; saute until fragrant, about 1 minute.
4. Add eggs, stirring constantly to blend flavors, about 2 minutes.
5. Off heat, stir in tomato, cilantro, and salt.
6. Serve warm, at room temperature or chilled.
