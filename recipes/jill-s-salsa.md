## Ingredients

- 1 bunch fresh cilantro leaves
- 1 clove garlic
- 2  fresh jalapeno peppers
- 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
- 1 teaspoon cumin
- 4  green onions, sliced
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 teaspoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. In a food processor, finely chop the cilantro, garlic, and jalapenos.
2. Place the tomatoes and cumin in the food processor.
3. Coarsely chop the tomatoes.
4. Transfer the mixture to a bowl.
5. Mix in the green onions, lime juice, and olive oil.
