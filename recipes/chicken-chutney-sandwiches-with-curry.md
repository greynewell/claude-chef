## Ingredients

- 1  roasted chicken, bones and skin removed, meat shredded
- ¾ cup cranberry and apple chutney
- ¼ cup whipped cream cheese
- 2 teaspoons curry powder
- 6  croissants, split

## Instructions

1. Stir together the chicken, chutney, cream cheese, and curry powder.
2. Spread onto the split croissants and serve.
