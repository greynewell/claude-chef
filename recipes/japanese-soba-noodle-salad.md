## Ingredients

- 1 pound soba noodles
- ½ tablespoon olive oil, or to taste
- 1 (1 1/2 inch) piece ginger root, minced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons miso paste
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 1 tablespoon mirin
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
- 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring lightly salted water to a boil in a large pot.
2. Add soba noodles; cook, stirring occasionally until tender, about 5 minutes.
3. Drain and rinse with cold water.
4. Set aside.
5. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
6. Cook and stir ginger and garlic until golden brown, about 3 minutes.
7. Remove from heat.
8. Mix ginger, garlic, miso paste, lime juice, and mirin together in a small bowl.
9. Pour salad dressing mixture over soba noodles.
10. Top with green onions, sesame seeds, and cilantro.
11. Chill until serving.
