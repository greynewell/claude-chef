## Ingredients

- 1 cup peanut oil
- 15  eggs
- 30 cups cold cooked rice
- 6 cups frozen peas, thawed
- 3 cups cubed fully cooked ham
- 3 cups chopped green onion
- 5 ounces soy sauce, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat peanut oil in an industrial-sized skillet over medium-high heat until hot, but not smoking.
2. Add eggs to hot oil; cook and stir vigorously until eggs are cooked yet slightly wet, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir rice into the scrambled eggs, breaking into individual grains; cook and stir until hot, about 5 minutes.
4. Mix peas, ham, and green onion into the rice; cook and stir until hot, about 10 minutes.
5. Pour soy sauce over the rice mixture; stir to coat rice with sauce.
