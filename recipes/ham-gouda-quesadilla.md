## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- 1 small onion, thinly sliced
- 2 teaspoons Dijon mustard
- 1 (10 inch) flour tortilla
- 2 slices deli ham
- 8  dill pickle rounds
- ½ cup Borden® Gouda Shreds

## Instructions

1. Spread mustard on one half of tortilla.
2. Layer with ham, caramelized onion, pickles and Gouda cheese; fold tortilla in half.
3. Cook in a dry skillet over medium high heat until browned on both sides and cheese is melted.
4. Cut into four pieces.
