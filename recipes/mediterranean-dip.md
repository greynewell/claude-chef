## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 ¼ cups hummus
- 1 cup chopped, seeded Roma tomatoes
- 1 cup chopped cucumbers
- ½ cup kalamata olives, sliced
- ⅓ cup chopped red onion
- ½ cup crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese, garlic, lemon juice, and Italian seasoning together. Spread mixture onto the bottom of a round 10-inch dish. Spread hummus on top.
2. Mix tomatoes, cucumbers, olives, and red onion together in a bowl; spread over the hummus. Sprinkle feta cheese on top. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
