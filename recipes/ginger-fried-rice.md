## Ingredients

- ½ cup olive oil, divided
- 1 cup sliced green onions
- 1 cup shredded carrots
- 4  eggs, lightly beaten
- ¼ cup ginger paste
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 4 cups day-old cooked white rice
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 2 teaspoons Sriracha sauce
- ½ teaspoon sesame oil

## Instructions

1. Heat 1/4 cup olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add green onions, shredded carrots, eggs, ginger paste, minced garlic, and salt. Stir gently until eggs are cooked through, 4 to 5 minutes. Transfer to a bowl.
2. Pour remaining 1/4 cup oil into the same skillet and add rice. Fry for 2 minutes. Add soy sauce, oyster sauce, Sriracha sauce, and sesame oil; stir to combine. Add egg mixture back into the skillet and stir gently to combine. Cook for 2 minutes more.
