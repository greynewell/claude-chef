## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1  spaghetti squash, halved lengthwise and seeded
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 3 (4 ounce) Italian sausage links, casings removed
- 2  spring onions, finely chopped
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  zucchini, diced
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and diced
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
- 4 ounces crumbled feta cheese, or more to taste
- sea salt to taste
- 1 pinch lemon pepper, or to taste
- 1 small tomato, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley, or as desired  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat large baking dish with cooking spray.
3. Place spaghetti squash cut-side down in prepared baking dish.
4. Bake in preheated oven until spaghetti squash is tender when pierced with a fork, about 45 minutes.
5. Turn squash over and bake for another 5 minutes.
6. Remove spaghetti squash from oven.
7. Scrape the strands from the skin and place into a large bowl.
8. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
9. Add Italian sausage and cook, stirring occasionally, until sausage is brown and crumbly, 5 to 8 minutes.
10. Remove sausage.
11. Return skillet to heat; add 1 tablespoon olive oil, spring onions, and garlic, cook and stir until onions are softened, about 5 minutes.
12. Add zucchini, red peppers, and Italian seasoning and continue to cook and stir until the vegetables are soft, about 5 minutes.
13. Stir spaghetti squash and feta cheese into vegetable mixture; cook and stir until cheese melts, about 3 minutes.
14. Stir sausage into vegetable mixture; season with salt and lemon pepper.
15. Sprinkle tomato and parsley on top to serve.
