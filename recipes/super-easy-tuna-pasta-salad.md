## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package rotini pasta
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle creamy Italian-style salad dressing (such as Ken's Steak House® Creamy Italian Dressing)
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained and flaked

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil; cook the rotini at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes; drain.
2. Rinse pasta with cold water until chilled; drain.
3. Mix pasta and salad dressing in a large bowl; fold tuna into the pasta mixture.
4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until the salad is cold, at least 1/2 hour.
