## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ pounds skinned salmon fillet
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
- 2 teaspoons soy sauce
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 4 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
- 1 teaspoon finely grated fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon soy sauce
- 4  hamburger buns
- 4 thin slices  red onion
- 4 slices ripe tomato

## Instructions

1. Finely chop salmon fillet, discarding any bones.
2. Place salmon in a bowl with mustard, grated ginger, soy sauce, salt, and pepper.
3. Stir together until thoroughly combined.
4. Form into four 3-inch patties.
5. Heat oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
6. Add patties to the hot oil, and cook until golden brown, about 4 minutes per side.
7. While patties are cooking, whisk mayonnaise, mustard, ginger, and soy in a small bowl.
8. Let mayonnaise mixture sit until flavors meld, 1 to 2 minutes.
9. Serve cooked patties on hamburger buns with mayonnaise mixture, onions, and tomatoes.
