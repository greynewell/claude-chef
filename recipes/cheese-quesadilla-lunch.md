## Ingredients

- ¼ cup prepared salsa
- 1 ½ tablespoons shredded reduced-fat Cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon shredded Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon drained and rinsed canned black beans
- ½ tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 (6 inch) whole-wheat tortillas
- ½ teaspoon butter, melted
- 1 tablespoon prepared guacamole
- 1 tablespoon chopped red bell peppers

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Mix salsa, Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, black beans, and cilantro together in a bowl.
3. Spoon salsa mixture on the center of 1 tortilla and spread evenly.
4. Place remaining tortilla on top of filling.
5. Arrange quesadilla on a baking sheet and brush top tortilla with butter.
6. Bake in the preheated oven until top begins to brown and cheese is melted, 9 to 12 minutes.
7. Slice quesadilla with a pizza cutter; garnish with guacamole and red bell pepper.
