## Ingredients

- 1 cup red lentils
- 1  onion, chopped
- 2 ½ cups water
- 2 teaspoons curry powder
- ¾ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 cloves crushed garlic
- 1 teaspoon cumin seeds

## Instructions

1. In a medium saucepan combine the lentils, onion and water.
2. Cover and bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 25 minutes or until lentils are soft.
4. Pass lentils through a food mill or blend them in a blender.
5. Toast curry powder and cumin seeds in a small skillet over medium heat until fragrant.
6. Add cayenne, oil and garlic.
7. Saute for 1 minute.
8. Stir spice mixture into lentils and serve.
