## Ingredients

- 3 pounds chicken wings
- 3 tablespoons salt
- 2 tablespoons white sugar
- 6 tablespoons water
- 6 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon gin
- Â¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 quart vegetable oil for frying

## Instructions

1. FOR MARINADE: Combine the salt, sugar, water, soy sauce, gin and ginger.
2. Put mixture in a zipper style plastic bag, add chicken and marinate for 24 hours or as long as possible, turning bag frequently.
3. In a large skillet over medium high heat fry marinated chicken wings in hot oil until golden brown, about 8 minutes each side.
4. Ready to serve!
