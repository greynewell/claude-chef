## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground lamb
- 1  onion, grated
- 1  egg
- 3 tablespoons minced fresh dill
- ¼ cup minced fresh cilantro
- 2 teaspoons coarse salt
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 (8 ounce) package baby carrots
- 2 (15.5 ounce) cans cannellini beans
- 1 cup water
- 1 tablespoon sour cream

## Instructions

1. Mix together the ground lamb, onion, egg, dill, cilantro, salt, cumin, turmeric, cinnamon, and pepper in a bowl until evenly incorporated.
2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Crumble the lamb mixture into the skillet and cook until no longer pink, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Add the carrots to the mixture; reduce heat to medium.
5. Pour in the contents of the 2 cans of beans and the water; stir.
6. Simmer mixture 25 minutes.
7. Stir the sour cream through the mixture; cook until the liquid thickens, 5 to 10 minutes.
