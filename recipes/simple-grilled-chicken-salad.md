## Ingredients

- 5 tablespoons olive oil
- 3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon hot sauce (such as Tabasco®)
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ teaspoon dried thyme
- 2  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- 4 slices red onion, 1/3-inch thick
- 4 cups torn romaine lettuce
- ½ cup feta cheese, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Whisk oil, lemon juice, hot sauce, and thyme in a small saucepan.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Arrange chicken and onion slices on a plate.
5. Spoon 2 tablespoons of the dressing over the chicken and onions; turn to coat.
6. Let stand for 5 minutes.
7. Grill chicken and onion slices on the preheated grill, turning once, until chicken is cooked through and the juices run clear, about 8 minutes.
8. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
9. Set the saucepan with the remaining dressing at the edge of the grill to warm.
10. Transfer chicken and onion slices to a place.
11. Cut chicken crosswise into thin slices.
12. Separate onion slices into rings.
13. Place lettuce in a large bowl; add chicken and onion.
14. Add warm dressing and toss to coat.
15. Season with salt and pepper.
16. Sprinkle with feta cheese and serve.
