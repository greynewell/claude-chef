## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) (12-inch) French baguette
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 ounces finely diced, thickly sliced deli Margherita® Genoa or Hard Salami*
- ¼ cup drained, finely chopped sun-dried tomatoes (packed in oil)
- ¼ cup finely diced smoked provolone or smoked Gouda cheese
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped pitted calamata olives
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil or Italian parsley

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. Cut bread crosswise into 1/2-inch thick slices; arrange on a baking sheet.
3. Combine oil and garlic; spoon over bread slices.
4. Bake about 15 minutes or until bread is lightly toasted.
5. Meanwhile, combine salami, sun dried tomatoes, cheese, olives and basil; mix well and set aside. (If mixture seems dry, add 1 teaspoon oil from bottled sun dried tomatoes).
6. Just before serving, spoon salami mixture over toasts.
7. Makes 12 servings (about 24 pieces).
