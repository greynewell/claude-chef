## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2  limes, juiced
- 2  marinated roasted red peppers, with liquid
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- ⅛ teaspoon ground allspice
- ⅛ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon white sugar
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 ½ pounds salmon steaks
- 1 large tomato, cut into thin wedges
- 3  green onions, chopped
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 1  lime, sliced

## Instructions

1. In a medium nonreactive bowl, mix olive oil, juice of 2 limes, roasted red peppers, garlic, allspice, cinnamon, cumin, sugar, salt and pepper.
2. Place salmon steaks in bowl, and rub with the mixture.
3. Cover, and marinate in the refrigerator at least 1 hour.
4. Preheat the broiler.
5. Arrange salmon steaks on a medium broiler pan, and broil 3 to 5 minutes on each side, until flesh is easily flaked with a fork.
6. Mix tomato wedges and green onions in a small bowl.
7. Serve salmon with the tomato mixture and lettuce.
8. Garnish with lime wedges.
