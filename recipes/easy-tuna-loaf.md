## Ingredients

- 8 slices bread
- 2 cups milk
- 1 teaspoon anchovy paste, or more to taste
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 18 ounces Italian, oil-packed tuna
- 1 bunch parsley, chopped
- 1 tablespoon capers
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Arrange bread in a large baking dish.
2. Pour milk over bread; allow to soak until bread is soggy.
3. Remove bread and squeeze excess moisture from the bread.
4. Discard milk.
5. Whisk anchovy paste with eggs in a bowl until dissolved.
6. Crumble tuna and moistened bread together in a large bowl.
7. Add the egg mixture, parsley, capers, and Parmesan cheese; knead together until evenly blended and reasonably firm.
8. Wrap the mixture in a clean cotton dishtowel and roll it up tightly, securing the two ends with string or elastic bands.
9. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
10. Cook the bundled tuna loaf in boiling water for 40 minutes; drain.
11. Chill in refrigerator until cold before unwrapping and slicing to serve.
