## Ingredients

- 1 cup stemmed, chopped kale
- ⅓ cup plain Greek yogurt
- 6  grape tomatoes, halved - or more to taste
- ½  cucumber, thinly sliced
- 1 tablespoon crumbled feta cheese
- 1 tablespoon sunflower seeds

## Instructions

1. Place kale in a salad bowl and add yogurt.
2. Use your hands to rub kale and yogurt together to reduce bitterness, 2 to 3 minutes.
3. Stir grape tomatoes and cucumber into kale mixture and top salad with crumbled feta cheese and sunflower seeds.
