## Ingredients

- 1 (12 ounce) container plain nonfat Greek yogurt
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup white vinegar
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 tablespoon skim milk, or as needed
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 (12 ounce) packages coleslaw mix

## Instructions

1. Stir yogurt, mayonnaise, vinegar, and honey together in a large bowl.
2. Gradually beat milk into the yogurt mixture until you reach your desired consistency; season with salt and pepper.
3. Add coleslaw mix and toss to coat.
4. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate until chilled, about 1 hour.
