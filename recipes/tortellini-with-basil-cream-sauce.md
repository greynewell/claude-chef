## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ pounds cheese tortellini
- 1 (16 ounce) can diced tomatoes, drained
- 1 cup chopped fresh parsley, divided
- 5 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese, divided
- 1 ½ tablespoons dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 cube chicken bouillon
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 cup heavy cream

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in tortellini and return to a boil.
2. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the tortellini float to the top and the filling is hot, 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Drain and keep warm.
4. Blend tomatoes, 1/4 cup parsley, 4 tablespoons Parmesan cheese, basil, pepper, and bouillon in a food processor until smooth.
5. Set aside.
6. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium heat.
7. Cook and stir garlic until softened and fragrant, about 1 minute.
8. Pour in tomato-basil blend.
9. Add salt; reduce heat to medium-low and let simmer 5 minutes.
10. Pour in heavy cream; bring to a slow boil over medium-high heat.
11. Reduce heat and let sauce simmer until thickened, 10 to 15 minutes.
12. Pour sauce blend over cooked tortellini.
13. Top with remaining 3/4 cup parsley and 1 tablespoon Parmesan.
