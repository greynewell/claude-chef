## Ingredients

- 1 ½ pounds large shrimp, peeled
- 2 large garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- 6 tablespoons minced cilantro
- 3  Green Onions, thinly sliced
- ½ cup Thai peanut sauce
- ¼ cup dry bread crumbs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 cups slaw mix
- 4 small (4 inch) pita breads

## Instructions

1. In a food processor, blend 1/3 of the shrimp into a paste.
2. Add remaining shrimp; pulse until finely chopped.
3. Transfer to a bowl.
4. Add garlic, ginger, jalapeno, 1/4 cup cilantro, 2 green onions, 1/4 cup peanut sauce, bread crumbs and salt; combine with a fork.
5. Divide into 4 portions.
6. Pat with fingertips to flatten to about 4 inches wide.
7. Refrigerate.
8. Whisk remaining peanut sauce with lime juice and oil.
9. Mix slaw with remaining onion and cilantro; toss with dressing.
10. Refrigerate.
11. For charcoal grill: Build a hot fire on one side of grill.
12. Replace rack.
13. When coals are covered with white ash, add burgers.
14. Cover and cook, turning only once, until opaque throughout, about 3 1/2 to 4 minutes per side.
15. Grill pitas until spotty brown and warm, a minute or so per side.
16. Split crosswise.
17. Place a burger on each of 4 pita halves.
18. Top with slaw, then remaining pita halves.
