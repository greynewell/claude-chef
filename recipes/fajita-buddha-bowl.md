## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon coconut oil
- 1 cup frozen bell peppers
- 1 cup frozen corn
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups refried beans
- 2 cups cooked brown rice
- ¼ cup guacamole

## Instructions

1. Heat coconut oil in a skillet over medium heat; add frozen bell peppers, frozen corn, salt, and black pepper. Cook and stir until heated through, 5 to 7 minutes.
2. Divide refried beans, brown rice, and bell pepper-corn mixture evenly between 2 bowls. Top each with 2 tablespoons of guacamole.
