## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 1  spaghetti squash
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chopped tomatoes
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
- ¼ cup chopped fresh oregano
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- ½ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
3. Pierce spaghetti squash in several places with a knife.
4. Microwave on medium-high, rotating every 5 minutes, until tender, 10 to 15 minutes.
5. Remove from microwave.
6. Let cool until easily handled, 5 to 10 minutes.
7. Cut squash in half and scoop out seeds.
8. Scrape insides of squash into spaghetti strands with a fork.
9. Transfer to the greased baking dish.
10. Pour tomatoes, 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese, oregano, basil, garlic, and salt over spaghetti squash; mix well.
11. Sprinkle remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese on top.
12. Bake in the preheated oven until golden and bubbly, 45 to 60 minutes.
