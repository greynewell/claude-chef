## Ingredients

- 1 (32 ounce) package frozen bite-size potato nuggets (such as Tater Tots®)
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- ¾ cup water
- 1 cup spicy nacho cheese sauce
- ½ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup diced onion
- ¼ cup jalapeno pepper slices

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Arrange potato nuggets in a single layer on a baking sheet.
3. Bake potato nuggets in the preheated oven until golden and heated through, 24 to 26 minutes.
4. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes; drain and discard grease. Stir water and taco seasoning mix into beef; cook and stir until liquid is thickened, 2 to 3 minutes.
5. Pour cheese sauce into a microwave-safe bowl; heat in the microwave for 30 seconds, stir. Continue heating in 15-second intervals until hot, stirring between intervals.
6. Spread potato nuggets on a serving plate. Top with meat, cheese sauce, sour cream, onion, and jalapeno slices.
