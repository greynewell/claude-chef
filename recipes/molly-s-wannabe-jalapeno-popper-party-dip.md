## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ¼ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ cup Italian-style bread crumbs
- 4  jalapeno peppers, seeded and diced
- ½ teaspoon red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese, sour cream, and Parmesan cheese together in a saucepan over low heat until evenly blended.
2. Fold in bread crumbs, jalapeno peppers, and red pepper flakes.
3. Cook and stir dip until Parmesan cheese is melted, 2 to 5 more minutes.
