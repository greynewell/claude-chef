## Ingredients

- ¼ cup chopped cilantro
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1  lime, zested and juiced
- 1 ½ teaspoons soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 (12 ounce) package firm tofu, drained and cubed
- 1 tablespoon olive oil, or more if needed

## Instructions

1. Mix cilantro, garlic, lime zest and juice, soy sauce, cumin, brown sugar, and cayenne pepper in a bowl.
2. Add tofu and marinate in refrigerator for 1 hour to overnight.
3. Remove tofu with a slotted spoon.
4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir marinated tofu until browned on all sides, 10 to 15 minutes.
