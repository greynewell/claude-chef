## Ingredients

- 4  cooked skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 1 cup sour cream
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- 4  green onions, chopped
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- salt and pepper to taste
- 1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
- 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Shred the cooked chicken breast meat, and place in a large bowl.
3. Mix in sour cream, shredded cheese, and green onions.
4. Season with cumin, cilantro, salt, and pepper.
5. Place a heaping spoonful of the mixture in each tortilla, roll up, and place rolled tortillas seam-side down in a 9x13 inch baking dish.
6. Pour salsa over all.
7. Bake for about 20 minutes, or until heated through.
