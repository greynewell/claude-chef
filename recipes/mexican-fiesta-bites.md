## Ingredients

- ¼ cup fat-free refried beans
- 12 each Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
- 1 teaspoon taco seasoning mix, or to taste
- ¼ cup guacamole
- ¼ cup salsa
- ¼ cup reduced-fat sour cream
- 1  roma (plum) tomatoes, chopped
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
- 12 slices black olives
- 1 tablespoon shredded Cheddar cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Smooth about a teaspoon of refried beans over the top of the Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®. Sprinkle lightly with taco seasoning.
2. Add a layer of guacamole, then salsa, and a dollop of sour cream.
3. Top with some chopped tomatoes, a sprinkle of chopped cilantro, and Cheddar cheese. Place an olive slice on the top.
