## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1  green chile pepper, seeded and minced
- ¼ teaspoon ground coriander
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon garam masala
- ¼ teaspoon paprika
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 (14 ounce) can baked beans in tomato sauce (such as Heinz®)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir onion in hot oil for about 2 minutes.
3. Stir chile pepper into onion; continue to cook and stir until the onion softens slightly, 2 to 3 minutes more.
4. Season with coriander, cumin, garam masala, paprika, salt, and pepper; stir.
5. Pour beans into saucepan, mix well, and cook until hot, about 3 minutes.
