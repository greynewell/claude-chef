## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- 2 teaspoons lime juice
- 1 teaspoon lime zest
- ½ teaspoon sugar
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt and ground black pepper
- 1 (5 ounce) can solid white albacore tuna in water, drained and chunked
- 2 tablespoons chopped Roma tomatoes
- 1 ½ tablespoons chopped red onion
- 1 tablespoon peeled, seeded, and chopped cucumber
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 1 teaspoon capers, drained  (Optional)
- 8  Original Snack Factory® Pretzel Crisps®
- 8  slices pitted kalamata olives
- Crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Stir Dijon mustard, lime juice, lime zest, and sugar together in a bowl. Gradually whisk in olive oil; whisk until dressing thickens slightly. Stir in the salt and pepper.
2. Place the tuna in a bowl. Lightly fold in tomatoes, red onion, cucumber, parsley, and capers.
3. Drizzle dressing over the tuna mixture. Stir gently, trying not to break the tuna into small pieces.
4. Place a spoonful of tuna onto a pretzel crisp, top with 2 olive slices, and sprinkle with some crumbled feta cheese.
