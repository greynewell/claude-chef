## Ingredients

- 1  red onion, thinly sliced
- 2  limes, juiced
- ½  watermelon, cut into bite-sized cubes
- 1 (4 ounce) package Greek feta cheese, cubed
- 20  pitted black olives
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 teaspoon brown sugar  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix red onion and lime juice together in a bowl. Refrigerate until onion turns bright pink, about 30 minutes.
2. Add watermelon, feta cheese, olives, cilantro, parsley, and olive oil to the onion mixture; mix well. Season with salt and pepper. Add brown sugar if lime juice tastes too sharp.
