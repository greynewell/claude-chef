## Ingredients

- 8  cheese tortellinis
- 8 slices thinly sliced salami
- 8 sprigs fresh flat-leaf parsley
- 2  artichoke hearts, quartered
- 4  grape tomatoes, halved
- 8 (1 inch) cubes fresh mozzarella cheese
- 4  pitted black olives, halved
- 8  bamboo toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Bring a small pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook tortellini in the boiling water until pasta floats to the top, 1 to 2 minutes; drain.
3. Thread 1 tortellini, 1 salami slice, 1 sprig parsley, 1 artichoke heart quarter, 1 tomato half, 1 mozzarella cheese cube, and 1 olive half, respectively, onto a toothpick.
4. Repeat threading with remaining toothpicks.
