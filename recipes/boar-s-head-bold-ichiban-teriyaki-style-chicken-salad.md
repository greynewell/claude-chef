## Ingredients

- 3 (1/4 inch thick) slices Boar’s Head Bold® Ichiban Teriyaki™ Style Oven Roasted Chicken, cubed
- ¼ cup celery, diced
- ¼ cup scallions, diced
- ½ cup Mandarin oranges, diced
- 4 tablespoons soy sauce
- 4 tablespoons rice vinegar
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 1 teaspoon brown sugar

## Instructions

1. Slice Teriyaki Chicken into small 1/4-inch cubes. Finely dice celery and scallions. Drain mandarin oranges and slice into small pieces. Place the ingredients into a large mixing bowl and set aside.
2. In a separate large mixing bowl, combine the Teriyaki Sauce ingredients. Mix the sauce and pour into the large bowl with the other ingredients. Mix the salad and serve.
