## Ingredients

- PAM® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
- 2 ½ pounds boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 1 (15 ounce) can Manwich® Original Sloppy Joe Sauce
- ¼ cup hot pepper sauce
- 2 tablespoons firmly packed brown sugar
- ⅔ cup nonfat plain Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons ranch dip mix
- 2 cups thinly sliced celery
- 1 ½ cups carrots, chopped
- 12 (12 inch) flour tortillas
- 3 cups shredded romaine or Bibb lettuce
- 1 teaspoon Blue cheese crumbles

## Instructions

1. Spray inside of 4.5-quart slow cooker with cooking spray; place chicken in slow cooker.
2. Stir together Manwich, hot sauce and brown sugar in small bowl; pour over chicken.
3. Cook on LOW 4 to 5 hours or HIGH 3 to 4 hours, until chicken is tender.
4. Stir together yogurt and ranch powder in medium bowl.
5. Stir in celery and carrot until well combined; set aside.
6. Remove chicken from slow cooker.
7. Shred chicken with two forks and return to sauce in slow cooker.
8. To serve: Build wraps with about 1/2 cup chicken, 1/4 cup celery slaw, 1/4 cup shredded lettuce and blue cheese, if desired.
9. Serve immediately.
