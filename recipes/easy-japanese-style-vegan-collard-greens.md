## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons Asian (toasted) sesame oil
- 1 bunch collard greens, thinly sliced
- 3 tablespoons gomasio (such as Eden Organic Foods®)
- 1 tablespoon mirin (Japanese sweet wine)
- 2 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
- 1 pinch sea salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat sesame oil in a large skillet or wok until sizzling; add  , gomasio, and mirin.
2. Cook until collard greens are tender, 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Stir garlic into collard green mixture and cook just until garlic is fragrant, about 30 seconds; remove from heat.
4. Season with additional sea salt if desired.
