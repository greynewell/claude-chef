## Ingredients

- 1 (12 inch) flour tortilla
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon prepared yellow mustard, or to taste
- 2 ounces thinly sliced Montreal smoked meat
- ¼ cup shredded Mexican cheese blend
- ¼ cup vegetable oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Warm tortilla in microwave on high until soft, about 20 seconds.
2. Stir together mayonnaise and mustard in a small bowl.
3. Spread mayonnaise mixture in the center of the tortilla to within 1 inch of edges.
4. Place Montreal smoked meat along the center of the tortilla, 2 inches from top and bottom and 4 inches from  sides.
5. Sprinkle Mexican cheese blend over meat.
6. Fold in bottom and top of tortilla, then fold in sides.
7. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat; cook wrap, seam side down, until golden brown, about 2 minutes per side.
8. Press wrap with a spatula to flatten while cooking.
9. Remove from pan and cut in half.
