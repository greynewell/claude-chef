## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (8 ounce) container sour cream
- ¾ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¾ teaspoon garlic salt
- ½ teaspoon hot pepper sauce
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 1  onion, finely chopped
- 1 cup shredded lettuce
- 2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, mix the cream cheese, sour cream, cumin, garlic salt and hot pepper sauce.
2. Transfer the mixture to a medium serving dish.
3. Top with tomato, onion, lettuce and Cheddar cheese.
