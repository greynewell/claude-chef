## Ingredients

- 20  large shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1  lime, juiced
- 2 tablespoons fresh chopped cilantro
- 1 large clove garlic, minced
- 10  fresh jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
- 20 slices bacon
- 4  wooden skewers, soaked in water

## Instructions

1. Preheat a grill for high heat.
2. Toss shrimp together with the lime juice, cilantro, and minced garlic.
3. Place one shrimp inside each jalapeno half, and wrap with a slice of bacon.
4. Thread onto skewers, 5 shrimp per skewer.
5. Lightly oil the grill grate.
6. Place skewers on the grill and cook for 4 to 5 minutes per side or until the bacon has browned and the shrimp are pink.
