## Ingredients

- 2 small zucchini, peeled and cut into chunks
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon tahini
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 pinch ground cumin
- 1 pinch paprika

## Instructions

1. Blend zucchini, lemon juice, tahini, garlic, and salt in a blender until creamy.
2. Stream olive oil into the hummus while blending until completely incorporated.
3. Continue blending until smooth, 1 to 2 minutes.
4. Transfer to a serving bowl; sprinkle cumin and paprika over the hummus.
