## Ingredients

- 4  oranges
- 3  blood oranges
- 1 small onion, finely diced
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- salt
- 1 pinch freshly ground black pepper
- 10  dried black olives

## Instructions

1. Slice a little piece off the top and bottom of each orange so you have a stable cutting surface. Trim away all skin and pith with a small sharp knife without taking too much off the fruit. Slice oranges horizontally and arrange on a serving platter.
2. Combine onion, olive oil, lemon juice, and salt in a bowl and pour over the oranges. Grind fresh pepper over the salad and arrange black olives on top.
