## Ingredients

- 1 (11 ounce) can Mexican-style corn
- 1 (16 ounce) can chili beans, undrained
- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 1 (16 ounce) jar chunky salsa
- 2 ½ cups cooked, chopped turkey meat
- salt and pepper to taste
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- ½ cup low-fat sour cream
- 2 tablespoons guacamole

## Instructions

1. In a large pot over medium heat, combine corn, chili beans, broth and salsa.
2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and stir in cooked turkey.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Cover, and cook 5 to 10 minutes more, until heated through.
5. Stir in cilantro.
6. In a bowl, stir together sour cream and guacamole until smooth.
7. Ladle soup into bowls and top with guacamole mixture.
