## Ingredients

- 2 cups uncooked jasmine rice
- 2 ½ cups water
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 (8 ounce) package sliced mushrooms
- ½ pound frozen cooked shrimp, thawed
- 2 stalks green onions, sliced
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 large egg

## Instructions

1. Rinse rice in a strainer until the water runs clear.
2. Transfer rice to a saucepan.
3. Add water and bring to a boil.
4. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 20 minutes.
5. Check rice; the water should be completely absorbed and rice should be tender.
6. If rice is still firm, cover and continue to cook until tender, 3 to 5 more minutes.
7. Remove from the heat.
8. Measure out 2 cups cooked rice for fried rice; set aside.
9. Reserve remaining rice for another use.
10. Warm oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
11. Add mushrooms and stir-fry until golden brown and soft, about 5 minutes.
12. Add shrimp and green onions; cook, stirring frequently, for 4 minutes.
13. Stir in 2 cups cooked rice and soy sauce; cook for 1 minute.
14. Break egg over the top of the rice mixture.
15. Stir until egg has completely set up in the rice, about 3 minutes.
