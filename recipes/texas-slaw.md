## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package coleslaw mix
- 1 (7 ounce) can Mexican-style corn, drained
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 2  fresh jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 cup Ranch-style salad dressing
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon ground coriander
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1  lime, juiced

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, toss together the coleslaw mix, corn, Cheddar cheese, jalapeno and cilantro.
2. In a separate bowl, stir together the Ranch-style dressing, cumin, coriander, garlic powder and lime juice.
3. Pour over the coleslaw mixture, and toss to coat.
4. Serve right away, or refrigerate until serving.
