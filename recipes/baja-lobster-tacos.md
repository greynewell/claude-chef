## Ingredients

- 2 large Hass avocados
- 1 small Roma tomato, seeded and diced
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- salt to taste
- 2 tablespoons butter, melted
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 4  lobster tails
- 8  flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. Peel and mash avocados in a medium serving bowl.
2. Stir in tomato, jalapeno pepper, onion, cilantro, lime juice, and salt.
3. Cover guacamole with plastic wrap and set aside while preparing the tacos.
4. Preheat a cast iron grill pan over medium heat.
5. Mix melted butter, olive oil, garlic, salt and pepper together in a small bowl.
6. Split lobster tails lengthwise with a large knife and brush flesh side of tail with butter mixture.
7. Cook until grill marks show, about 6 minutes.
8. Flip lobster tails and cook for 2 minutes more.
9. In the meantime, heat a griddle over medium heat until hot.
10. Cook tortillas on the griddle for about 1 minute on each side.
11. Pull lobster meat from shells and cut into 1/2-inch pieces.
12. Spread 1 heaping tablespoon of guacamole in the center of each warm tortilla and top with the lobster meat.
