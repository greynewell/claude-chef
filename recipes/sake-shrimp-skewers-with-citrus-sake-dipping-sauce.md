## Ingredients

- 1 pound peeled and deveined large raw shrimp (21/30 size)
- 6 tablespoons Holland House® Sake Cooking Wine
- 2 teaspoons toasted sesame oil
- ¼ cup orange juice
- 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon grated or minced fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 1 tablespoon chopped cilantro
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Combine shrimp, 2 tablespoons sake cooking wine and sesame oil in a bowl.
2. Let stand 20 minutes.
3. Preheat grill.
4. Thread 5-6 shrimp on each skewer (reserve marinade).
5. Grill shrimp about 2 minutes per side, turning with a spatula to release from grill.
6. Stir reserved marinade, remaining 4 tablespoons cooking wine, juice, soy sauce, ginger and cornstarch in a small saucepan.
7. Simmer 1- 2 minutes until slightly thickened.
8. Add sesame seeds and cilantro.
9. Serve with shrimp.
