## Ingredients

- ½ (8 ounce) package PHILADELPHIA Cream Cheese, softened
- 1 cup KRAFT Mexican Style Shredded Four Cheese with a Touch of PHILADELPHIA
- 4 slices OSCAR MAYER Bacon, cooked, crumbled
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped onions
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 18  jalapeno peppers, cut lengthwise in half, seeds and membranes removed

## Instructions

1. Heat oven to 375 degrees F.
2. Combine all ingredients except peppers; spoon into peppers.
3. Place, filled-sides up, on baking sheet.
4. Bake 10 min. or until cheese is melted.
