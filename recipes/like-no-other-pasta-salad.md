## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package penne pasta
- ½ large onion, chopped
- ½ large cucumber, sliced
- ½ cup canola oil
- ½ cup white vinegar
- ½ cup white sugar
- 1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground mustard
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook penne at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
3. Drain and rinse under cold water.
4. Transfer to a large bowl.
5. Blend onion, cucumber, canola oil, vinegar, sugar, garlic powder, salt, mustard, parsley, and pepper in a food processor or blender until smooth, about 2 minutes.
6. Pour sauce over pasta; toss to coat.
7. Refrigerate 8 hours or overnight.
