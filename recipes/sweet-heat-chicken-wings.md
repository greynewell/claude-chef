## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- ⅓ cup sriracha sauce
- ¼ cup butter
- ¼ cup granular sucralose sweetener (such as Splenda®)
- 2 tablespoons Dijon mustard
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- ¼ teaspoon ground ginger
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 5 pounds cooked chicken wings

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.
2. Combine sriracha, butter, sucralose, mustard, soy sauce, ginger, onion powder, and salt in a saucepan. Cook and stir over medium heat until blended and heated through, about 5 minutes.
3. Place cooked wings in a large bowl with sauce and toss gently to coat. Spread on the prepared baking sheet.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until sauce thickens slightly, 5 to 10 minutes.
