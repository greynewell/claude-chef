## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup strawberry preserves
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- 2 tablespoons toasted sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- ½ teaspoon ground ginger
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pound skin-on salmon fillets
- 1 cup chopped fresh strawberries
- 1 cup chopped fresh pineapple
- ⅓ cup chopped sweet onion (such as Vidalia)
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Mix strawberry preserves, soy sauce, apple cider vinegar, sesame oil, garlic powder, onion powder, ginger, and pepper together in a bowl to make marinade.
2. Pour 1/3 cup into a glass measuring cup; reserve.
3. Place salmon fillets in the bowl.
4. Marinate salmon in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
5. Mix strawberries, pineapple, onion, and parsley in a bowl to make salsa.
6. Cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate.
7. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
8. Oil a stainless steel grill basket.
9. Place salmon fillets skin-side up in the basket; cook for 6 minutes.
10. Flip and continue cooking skin-side down, about 6 minutes more.
11. Divide salmon fillets among serving plates; drizzle reserved 1/3 cup marinade on top.
12. Spoon salsa on top.
