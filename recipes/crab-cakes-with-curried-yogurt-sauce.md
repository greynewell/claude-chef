## Ingredients

- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- ¼ cup plain yogurt
- 1 teaspoon curry powder
- 1 teaspoon ground ancho chile pepper
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 tablespoon mango chutney
- ½ cup chopped green onion
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- 2 cups low sodium buttery round crackers, crushed
- 1  egg, beaten
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 tablespoon spicy brown mustard
- 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
- 1 dash lemon juice
- Old Bay Seasoning TM to taste
- 1 (6 ounce) can lump crabmeat, drained
- 2 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix together 1/4 cup mayonnaise and yogurt.
2. Season with curry powder, chile pepper, and garlic powder.
3. Stir in the mango chutney.
4. Cover and chill at least 30 minutes.
5. In a separate bowl, toss together the green onion, jalapeno, garlic, bread crumbs, and 1/2 the crackers.
6. Mix in the egg, 1/2 cup mayonnaise, mustard, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, and   seasoning.
7. Fold in the crabmeat.
8. Form the mixture into 6 cakes and evenly coat with the remaining bread crumbs.
9. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
10. Cook each cake about 5 minutes on each side, until heated through and golden brown.
11. Serve with the chilled sauce mixture.
