## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 1 (9 ounce) bag fresh spinach
- 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and finely chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) can sliced water chestnuts, drained and finely chopped
- 2 cups plain non-fat Greek-style yogurt
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 pinch ground black pepper, or to taste
- 1 pinch garlic powder, or to taste
- 1 pinch onion powder, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir spinach until wilted, about 5 minutes.
2. Transfer spinach to a cutting board and finely chop.
3. Mix spinach, artichoke hearts, and water chestnuts together in a bowl.
4. Add yogurt, Parmesan cheese, black pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder to spinach mixture; stir.
