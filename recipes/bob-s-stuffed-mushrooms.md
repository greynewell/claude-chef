## Ingredients

- 20  mushrooms, stems removed
- 2 (6.5 ounce) cans minced clams, drained
- ½ cup melted butter
- 1 small onion, chopped
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ¾ cup Italian bread crumbs
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons dried parsley
- 2 tablespoons Italian seasoning
- ½ tablespoon ground black pepper
- ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 1 cup melted butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a 9x13 inch baking dish with a sheet of aluminum foil, and lightly spray with nonstick cooking spray.
3. Clean the mushrooms carefully with a damp paper towel or a soft mushroom brush, and place into the prepared pan upside down.
4. Place the clams, 1/2 cup of melted butter, onion, Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, and garlic in a large mixing bowl.
5. Season with parsley, Italian seasoning, and pepper; mix well.
6. Fill the mushroom caps with the clam mixture, and round over the tops.
7. Sprinkle with the shredded mozzarella cheese, and drizzle with the remaining 1 cup of butter.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until the mushrooms are tender, and the mozzarella cheese has lightly browned, 25-30 minutes.
