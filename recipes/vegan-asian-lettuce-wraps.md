## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons Spectrum Naturals® Canola Oil
- 1 (320 g) package Yves Veggie Cuisine® Garden Veggie Crumble
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons grated gingerroot
- 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce
- ½ teaspoon Spectrum Naturals® Sesame Oil unrefined
- 1 pinch dried red chile peppers
- 8 leaves Leaf lettuce
- ½ cup shredded carrot
- ½ cup diced English cucumber
- ½ cup diced sweet red pepper
- ⅓ cup chopped green onion
- ⅓ cup chopped fresh coriander

## Instructions

1. In a large non-stick skillet, heat oil over medium heat.
2. Add Garden Veggie Crumble, garlic and gingerroot.
3. Cook for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Stir in hoisin sauce and sesame oil.
5. Add chile peppers or pepper sauce, to taste.
6. Reduce heat to low; cook for 5 minutes.
7. Place about 3 tablespoons (45 mL) veggie filling on each lettuce leaf.
8. Add toppings.
9. Roll up lettuce.
