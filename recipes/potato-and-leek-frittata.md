## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter
- 2  leeks, white and light-green parts thinly sliced
- 2 cups shredded potato
- 8  eggs
- ½ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- ½ cup grated Parmesan-Reggiano cheese
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish.
3. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir leeks and potato in the melted butter until softened, 8 to 10 minutes.
4. Spoon leek mixture into the prepared baking dish.
5. Beat eggs, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan-Reggiano cheese, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
6. Pour egg mixture over leek mixture.
7. Bake in the preheated oven until eggs are set, 20 to 25 minutes.
