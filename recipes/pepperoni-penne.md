## Ingredients

- ¾ pound sliced pepperoni sausage
- 2 tablespoons minced shallots
- 4  medium tomato - peeled, seeded and chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 (16 ounce) package penne pasta
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook penne according to the package directions.
2. In a large skillet, sauté pepperoni until crispy, 5 to 8 minutes. Drain all but 1 tablespoon grease.
3. Add shallot and garlic to grease in the skillet. Cook until translucent. Stir in tomatoes and simmer for about 3 minutes.
4. Stir in cream and cook until reduced, about 8 minutes. Add parsley and remove from heat.
5. Add pasta and toss. Garnish with Parmesan cheese.
