## Ingredients

- 3 ounces sun-dried tomatoes
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 10 slices provolone cheese
- 8 ounces pesto

## Instructions

1. Bring a small saucepan of water to boil.
2. Turn off heat.
3. Place sun-dried tomatoes in the water and cover for 5 minutes.
4. Drain and finely chop.
5. In a medium bowl, mix cream cheese and garlic.
6. Line a medium bowl or loaf pan with a large piece of dampened cheese cloth.
7. Layer with 1/3 of the provolone cheese.
8. Spread with 1/2 the pesto.
9. Pour in 1/2 the cream cheese and garlic mixture.
10. Layer with 1/2 the sun-dried tomatoes.
11. Layer with 1/3 provolone, remaining pesto, remaining cream cheese and garlic mixture and remaining sun-dried tomatoes.
12. Top with remaining provolone cheese.
13. Pull cheese cloth around the layered mixture and gently press into a bowl to mold and remove excess oil.
14. Chill in the refrigerator 3 to 4 hours.
15. Remove cheese cloth and turn upside down onto a large dish to serve.
