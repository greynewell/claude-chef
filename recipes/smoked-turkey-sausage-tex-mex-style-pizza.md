## Ingredients

- Cooking spray
- 2 slices Butterball® Turkey bacon
- 2 ounces Butterball® Smoked Turkey Dinner Sausage
- 1  Flatout® Light Original Flatbread
- ¼ cup salsa
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese*
- 10 thin slices  fresh jalapeno pepper, seeds removed*
- 8 thin slices  ripe pitted peeled avocado
- 1 tablespoon Ranch dressing

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Spray cookie sheet with cooking spray; set aside.
3. Cook turkey bacon according to package directions.
4. Cool slightly and crumble; set aside.
5. Heat turkey sausage according to package directions.
6. Cut into 10 thin slices; set aside.
7. Place Flatout Flatbread on prepared cookie sheet.
8. Bake 2 minutes.
9. Remove from oven.
10. Spread with salsa.
11. Top evenly with cheese, peppers and sausage.
12. Return to oven and bake 4 to 5 minutes or until cheese is melted.
13. Remove from oven and top with bacon and avocado slices.
14. Drizzle with dressing and serve.
