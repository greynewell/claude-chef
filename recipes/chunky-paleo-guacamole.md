## Ingredients

- 2  ripe avocados, peeled and pitted
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1  jalapeno pepper, diced
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½  small onion, minced
- 1  Roma tomato, seeded and diced
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place avocados in a bowl.
2. Squeeze lime juice on top and roughly smash with a fork until thoroughly mashed.
3. Add jalapeno pepper, garlic, onion, tomato, cilantro, salt, and pepper; gently combine with a fork.
