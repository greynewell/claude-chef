## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2  red onions, minced
- 3  green chile peppers, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon crushed ginger
- 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon ground white pepper, or to taste
- salt to taste
- 7 ounces fresh prawns
- 1  squid, cleaned and sliced into rings, or more to taste
- 4 ounces fresh spinach, finely chopped
- 1  carrot, sliced
- 18 ounces fresh rice noodles, rinsed and drained

## Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Add onions, green chile peppers, garlic, and ginger; cook and stir until golden, about 2 minutes.
3. Stir in soy sauce, white pepper, and salt; cook for 1 minute.
4. Stir prawns and squid into the wok; cook and stir until prawns turn pink, 2 to 3 minutes.
5. Add spinach and carrot; cook until spinach is wilted, 2 to 3 minutes.
6. Add noodles; toss to fully incorporate.
