## Ingredients

- Artichoke Aioli:
- 1 (6.5 ounce) jar marinated artichoke hearts, drained
- 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon lemon zest
- ½  lemon, juiced
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- Filling:
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 ½ pounds beef tri-tip steak, thinly sliced
- 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1  onion, sliced thin
- 1  yellow bell pepper, sliced into strips
- 1  orange bell pepper, sliced into strips
- ¼ cup pickled sweet and hot pepper rings
- ¼ cup garlic basil spread (see footnote for recipe link)
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1 tablespoon Marsala wine
- 1  anchovy fillet  (Optional)
- 12 slices aged provolone cheese
- ¼ cup crumbled Gorgonzola cheese
- Assembly:
- 4  Italian-style hoagie buns, split lengthwise
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil

## Instructions

1. Combine artichoke hearts, mayonnaise, Parmesan cheese, lemon zest, lemon juice, red pepper flakes, salt, and black pepper in a food processor or blender.
2. Blend until smooth.
3. Cover and refrigerate until ready to use.
4. Heat olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium-high heat.
5. Stir tri-tip steak slices into the hot oil and cook, stirring constantly until they begin to brown, about 3 minutes.
6. Sprinkle with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
7. Add onion, yellow pepper, orange pepper, sweet hot peppers, and garlic basil spread; cook and stir until onion begins to soften, about 5 minutes.
8. Stir in mushrooms, capers, Marsala wine, and anchovy fillet and cook until mushrooms are browned and wine has evaporated, about 5 minutes.
9. Place provolone cheese and Gorgonzola cheese on top of the hot beef mixture and gently fold until cheese is melted, about 2 minutes.
10. Remove beef-cheese mixture from heat.
11. Spread each hoagie bun with about 1/4 cup artichoke aioli.
12. Spoon beef-cheese mixture into buns and garnish with basil.
