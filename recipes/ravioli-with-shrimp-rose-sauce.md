## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1  onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 cup white wine, divided
- 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
- 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese, divided
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
- 1 pound cooked shrimp
- 2 pounds frozen cheese and spinach ravioli

## Instructions

1. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion until very tender and lightly browned, 15 to 20 minutes.
2. Add garlic; cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Pour in 1/2 cup white wine; cook until reduced by half, about 5 minutes.
4. Stir remaining 1/2 cup white wine, crushed tomatoes, and Italian seasoning into onion mixture; cook for 15 minutes.
5. Mix cream, 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, and basil into tomato sauce; cook for 5 minutes.
6. Add cooked shrimp; cook until heated through, 1 to 2 minutes.
7. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil; stir in frozen ravioli and return to a boil.
8. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the ravioli float to the top and the filling is hot, 6 to 8 minutes.
9. Drain.
10. Mix ravioli into tomato sauce and top with remaining 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese.
