## Ingredients

- 2 ounces dried wakame seaweed
- ¼ cup rice vinegar
- 3  green onions, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce, or more to taste
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon Asian sweet chili sauce, or more to taste
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger, or more to taste
- 1 packet stevia powder  (Optional)
- 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Rinse wakame, place in a bowl, and cover with water.
2. Let soak until soft, 5 to 6 minutes.
3. Drain and squeeze dry; slice into thin strips.
4. Whisk rice vinegar, green onions, soy sauce, sesame oil, chili sauce, garlic, ginger, and stevia powder in a serving bowl.
5. Add wakame strips; toss until coated.
6. Sprinkle sesame seeds on top.
