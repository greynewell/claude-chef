## Ingredients

- ½ cup water
- 2 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 ½ teaspoons cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 1 teaspoon Asian (toasted) sesame oil
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ¾ pound cooked shrimp
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1  thin slice fresh ginger root
- 1 small head broccoli, broken into florets
- 1  red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 small onion, halved and sliced
- 1 small yellow squash, sliced
- 1 small zucchini, sliced
- 4  mushrooms, quartered
- 2 cups hot cooked rice

## Instructions

1. Whisk water, ketchup, soy sauce, cornstarch, honey, sesame oil, and red pepper flakes in a bowl.
2. Stir shrimp into sauce mixture until coated.
3. Heat a wok or a large skillet over medium-high heat and add vegetable oil; cook and stir garlic and ginger in hot oil until fragrant and garlic begins to soften, 1 minute.
4. Discard garlic and ginger, leaving oil in wok.
5. Cook and stir broccoli, red bell pepper, onion, yellow squash, zucchini, and mushrooms in oil until slightly softened, about 5 minutes.
6. Stir shrimp and sauce mixture into vegetables; cook and stir until shrimp are hot and sauce has thickened and coated shrimp and vegetables, 3 to 5 more minutes.
7. Serve over hot cooked rice.
