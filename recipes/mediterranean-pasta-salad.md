## Ingredients

- 1 cup macaroni
- 2 ounces roasted red bell peppers, diced
- ¼ cup sliced black olives
- ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl or cup, combine olive oil and chopped garlic.
2. Set aside.
3. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water until al dente.
4. Drain.
5. Transfer pasta to a medium mixing bowl, and add roasted red peppers, olives, and feta cheese.
6. Toss with olive oil mixture and lemon juice.
7. Season with salt and pepper.
8. Serve immediately.
