## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, seeded and cut into squares
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and cut into squares
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 cup chicken broth
- 3 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 3 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
- 3 (14.5 ounce) cans Mexican-style stewed tomatoes
- 1  rotisserie chicken, meat removed and chopped
- water, as needed

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in Dutch oven or deep skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Saute chopped onion, green bell pepper, red bell pepper, and garlic in hot oil until tender but not yet browned, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Pour chicken broth over the vegetable mixture; season with chili powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper.
4. Place a lid on the pot, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer for 30 minutes.
5. Stir black beans, stewed tomatoes, and chicken meat together in a separate pot over medium-low heat; bring to a low simmer.
6. Stir the vegetable-broth mixture into the black bean mixture.
7. Bring again to a simmer and cook, adding water as needed to keep the mixture from becoming too dry, until the flavors blend, at least 30 minutes.
