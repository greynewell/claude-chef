## Ingredients

- 4  plum (roma) tomatoes, seeded and cubed
- 1  cucumber, peeled and diced
- ½  red onion, thinly sliced
- ½ cup cubed fontina cheese
- ½ cup pitted and chopped kalamata olives
- 2 tablespoons coarsely chopped fresh basil
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ½ teaspoon dried parsley
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ cup extra virgin olive oil
- 3 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- 1 (1 pound) loaf day-old Italian bread, cubed

## Instructions

1. Toss tomatoes, cucumber, onion, fontina cheese, olives, basil, oregano, parsley, salt, and black pepper with olive oil and balsamic vinegar in a large bowl.
2. Allow mixture to rest for 10 minutes.
3. Place bread cubes in a large dish.
4. Pour tomato mixture over bread and toss to coat.
5. Bread salad should be served within 2 hours.
