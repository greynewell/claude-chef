## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ large onion, diced
- ½ pound shaved rib-eye, chopped
- 1 (18 ounce) package restaurant-style tortilla chips
- 1 (8 fl oz) jar processed cheese spread (such as Kraft® Cheez Whiz®)
- 2  fresh jalapeno peppers, sliced

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
2. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Transfer to a bowl.
4. Cook rib-eye in the same skillet, stirring occasionally, until browned and juices have evaporated, 5 to 10 minutes.
5. Stir onion back into the skillet.
6. Place a handful of tortilla chips on each plate.
7. Spoon rib-eye and onion mixture over chips; top with cheese spread and jalapeno slices.
