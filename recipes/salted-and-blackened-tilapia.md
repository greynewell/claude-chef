## Ingredients

- 2 (4 ounce) tilapia fillets
- 1 teaspoon Diamond Crystal® Kosher Salt
- 2 teaspoons Cajun seasoning

## Instructions

1. Lightly salt one side of each tilapia filets with Diamond Crystal® Kosher Salt.
2. Rub Cajun seasoning on both sides of the filets.
3. Heat oil over medium-high heat in a skillet.
4. When the oil is hot, carefully place filets in the skillet.
5. Fry each side until richly browned and fish flakes easily, 3 to 4 minutes.
6. Place tilapia on a warmed plate; cover with foil for a few minutes to let rest.
