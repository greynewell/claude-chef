## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1  red onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoon red chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon garam masala
- 1 (16 ounce) package frankfurters, sliced
- 1 tablespoon ketchup  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion in hot oil until slightly pink, about 5 minutes.
2. Add red chili powder and garam masala; stir to coat the onion.
3. Stir the frankfurter slices into the onion mixture; cook and stir until hot, about 2 minutes.
4. Pour the ketchup over the frankfurters; stir to coat.
