## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans
- 1 (15 ounce) can kidney beans
- 1 (15 ounce) can green beans
- 1 (15 ounce) can wax beans
- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- ½ medium red onion, chopped
- ½ cup chopped green bell pepper
- ½ cup chopped red bell pepper
- ¼ cup sliced green olives
- ¼ cup sliced black olives
- ¼ cup dried parsley
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary
- ⅔ cup Greek salad dressing
- 1 tablespoon honey, or more to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Drain all beans and transfer to a large bowl. Add celery, onion, bell peppers, olives, parsley, and rosemary.
2. Mix Greek salad dressing and 1 tablespoon honey together. Taste and continue to add more honey as desired. Pour on salad and mix until evenly distributed. Season with salt and pepper.
3. Cover and refrigerate for 2 to 3 hours before serving.
