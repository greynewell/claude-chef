## Ingredients

- 6 tablespoons rice wine vinegar
- 6 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 5 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons minced fresh ginger root
- 1 ½ tablespoons minced garlic
- 5 cups thinly sliced green cabbage
- 2 cups thinly sliced red cabbage
- 2 cups shredded napa cabbage
- 2  red bell peppers, thinly sliced
- 2  carrots, julienned
- 6  green onions, chopped
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro

## Instructions

1. Prepare the peanut dressing: Whisk together rice vinegar, oil, peanut butter, soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, and garlic in a medium bowl. Set aside.
2. Make the cabbage salad: Mix green cabbage, red cabbage, napa cabbage, red bell peppers, carrots, green onions, and cilantro in a large bowl.
3. Toss salad together with peanut dressing just before serving.
