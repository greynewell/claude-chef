## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package farfalle (bow tie) pasta
- 2 (6.5 ounce) jars marinated artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- 3 tablespoons shredded Parmesan cheese
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon dried basil
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook the farfalle pasta at a boil, stirring occasionally, until cooked through yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes; drain and set aside.
3. Mix artichoke hearts, walnuts, Parmesan cheese, olive oil, lemon juice, basil, and oregano in a large bowl.
4. Season with salt and black pepper.
5. Add farfalle and stir to coat.
