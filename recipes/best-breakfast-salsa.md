## Ingredients

- 9 slices bacon
- ½  onion, chopped
- 1  fresh jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1  fresh tomato, finely chopped
- ½ cup canned tomato sauce

## Instructions

1. Place bacon in a large, deep skillet.
2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
3. Remove from pan; drain on paper towels.
4. Pour grease from pan, leaving a thin coating.
5. Saute the onions, tomato and pepper in the pan until the onion is soft, about 3 minutes.
6. Crumble the cooked bacon into the pan and then stir in the tomato sauce.
7. Cook, stirring constantly, until heated through.
