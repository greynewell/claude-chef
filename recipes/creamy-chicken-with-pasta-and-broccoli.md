## Ingredients

- 1 pound uncooked spaghetti
- 1 pound broccoli florets
- 1 tablespoon margarine
- 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - cut into strips
- ½ cup chopped onions
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- ⅔ cup milk
- ⅔ cup water
- 1 (3 ounce) package cream cheese, cubed and softened
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Place spaghetti in the pot, and cook 4 minutes.
3. Mix in the broccoli florets.
4. Continue cooking 4 to 6 minutes, until spaghetti is al dente.
5. Drain, and transfer to a large bowl.
6. Melt the margarine in a skillet over medium heat, and cook the chicken and onion 5 minutes, until chicken juices run clear and onions are tender.
7. In a bowl, whisk together the soup, milk, water, and cream cheese until smooth.
8. Stir into the skillet with the chicken and onion, and bring to a boil.
9. Reduce heat to low, and simmer 5 minutes, or until slightly thickened.
10. Toss in the bowl with the spaghetti and broccoli to serve.
