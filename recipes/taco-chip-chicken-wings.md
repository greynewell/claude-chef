## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package chicken drumettes
- 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- 2 cups crushed tortilla chip crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Rinse chicken pieces and pat dry.
3. In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the   mix and tortilla chip crumbs.
4. Roll chicken pieces in chip mixture and place coated chicken in a lightly greased 9x13 inch baking dish.
5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
6. Turn chicken pieces and bake for another 15 to 20 minutes.
