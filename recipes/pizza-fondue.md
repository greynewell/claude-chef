## Ingredients

- 1 (26 ounce) jar meatless spaghetti sauce
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon dried minced onion
- ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
- 1 (16 ounce) loaf French bread, cubed

## Instructions

1. Stir spaghetti sauce, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, oregano, dried onion, and garlic salt together in a fondue pot or heavy pan.
2. Light the fondue pot or place over medium heat, stirring sauce to melt cheeses.
3. Serve with cubes of French bread for dipping.
