## Ingredients

- 1 cup chicken broth
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 ¼ pounds thin catfish fillets
- 6 large white mushrooms, sliced
- ¼ cup sliced green onion
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Put chicken broth, ginger, and garlic in a wide pot with a lid.
2. Bring the broth to a boil, reduce heat to medium-low, and simmer 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Stir soy sauce into the broth mixture; add catfish fillets.
4. Spoon broth over the fillets.
5. Place cover on the pot, bring broth to a boil, and let cook for 3 minutes more.
6. Add mushrooms, cover, and cook until the fish loses pinkness and begins to flake, about 3 minutes more.
7. Sprinkle green onion over the fillets, cover, and cook for 30 seconds.
8. Garnish with cilantro to serve.
