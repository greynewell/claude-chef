## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped tomatoes
- ¼ cup chopped red onion
- ¼ cup chopped yellow onion
- 2 tablespoons canned green chilies
- 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Combine tomatoes, red onion, yellow onion, green chilies, lime juice, cilantro, garlic, cumin, and salt in a food processor. Pulse until mixture is combined but still chunky.
2. Transfer salsa to a bowl, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
