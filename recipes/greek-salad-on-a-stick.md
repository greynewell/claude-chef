## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons dried oregano, or to taste
- 8 (1/2 inch) cubes feta cheese
- 8  1/2-inch cucumber slices
- 8  grape tomatoes
- 8  Kalamata olives, pitted
- 8  bamboo toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Pour oregano into a shallow bowl.
2. Roll each feta cheese cube in oregano to coat.
3. Thread 1 cucumber, 1 tomato, 1 olive, and 1 feta cube onto a toothpick.
4. Repeat with remaining toothpicks.
