## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) jar Classico® Fresh Alfredo Sauce
- ½ cup canned chipotle sauce
- 2 pounds rotisserie chicken, shredded
- 8 slices bacon, cooked and drained
- 8  flour tortillas
- ½  onion, thinly sliced
- Toothpicks
- 1 tablespoon grapeseed oil
- 4 tablespoons Guacamole
- Pico de gallo

## Instructions

1. Mix together   with chipotle sauce.
2. Add the chicken mix until well blended.
3. Place a slice of bacon on a tortilla.
4. Spoon some of the chicken mixture on the tortilla and top with onion slices.
5. Roll up the tortilla and secure with a toothpick.
6. Fill and secure the other tortillas with the rest of the chicken and onions.
7. Heat oil in a heavy skillet over medium-high heat.
8. Place rolled tortillas in the skillet in a single layer.
9. Fry on each side until golden brown.
10. Brown in batches if necessary.
11. Serve with guacamole and pico de gallo.
