## Ingredients

- 3 cups fresh basil leaves
- 1 ½ cups chopped walnuts
- 4 cloves garlic, peeled
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 cup olive oil
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Blend together basil leaves, nuts, garlic, and cheese in a food processor until finely chopped.
2. Pour in oil slowly with the processor running. Season with salt and pepper.
