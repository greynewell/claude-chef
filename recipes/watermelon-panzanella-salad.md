## Ingredients

- 3 cups cubed French bread
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ½ cup walnut halves
- ¼ cup fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- ½ cup olive oil
- 3 cups cubed seeded watermelon
- 3 cups baby spinach-arugula mix
- ½ cup cubed feta cheese
- 6 leaves fresh basil, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Toss bread cubes, 1 tablespoon plus 2 teaspoons olive oil, and 1/2 teaspoon salt in a bowl.
3. Spread onto a baking sheet in a single layer.
4. Bake in the preheated oven, tossing once halfway through baking, until golden, about 8 minutes.
5. Let cool.
6. Reduce oven temperature to 350 degrees (175 degrees C).
7. Spread walnuts onto a baking sheet.
8. Toast walnuts in the preheated oven until golden brown and fragrant, 10 to 15 minutes.
9. Whisk lime juice, honey, Dijon mustard, 1/2 teaspoon salt, cumin, and pepper together in a bowl.
10. Pour in 1/2 cup olive oil, whisking continuously, until dressing is blended.
11. Combine bread cubes, watermelon, spinach-arugula mix, and feta cheese in a large bowl.
12. Add a few tablespoons of dressing; toss to mix.
13. Sprinkle toasted walnuts and basil on top.
14. Drizzle more dressing over the top.
