## Ingredients

- 2 (14 ounce) cans artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 cup shredded Asiago cheese
- 1 cup shredded fontina cheese
- 1 cup Greek yogurt (such as Fage®)
- 2 teaspoons red pepper flakes
- 2 tablespoons roasted minced garlic
- 2 tablespoons ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Mix artichoke hearts, spinach, cream cheese, Asiago cheese, fontina cheese, and Greek yogurt together in a bowl.
3. Stir red pepper flakes, minced garlic, and black pepper through the mixture; spread into a casserole dish.
4. Bake in preheated oven until warm, about 8 minutes.
