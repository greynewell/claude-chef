## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 1 small red onion, chopped
- 1 large garlic clove, minced
- 1 (16 ounce) can garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained and rinsed
- 1 (7.75 ounce) can spinach, well drained
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup dry breadcrumbs
- Hamburger buns
- Lettuce leaves
- Sliced tomato
- Sliced red onion
- Tzatziki sauce

## Instructions

1. In 12-inch skillet over medium heat, in 1 tablespoon hot olive oil, cook red onion and garlic about 5 minutes until tender-crisp.
2. To food processor, add one-fourth of garbanzo beans, lemon juice and salt; pulse canned spinach until mixture is a smooth paste.
3. Add remaining garbanzo beans, breadcrumbs and onion mixture; pulse until coarsely chopped.
4. Shape mixture into four 4-inch patties. (If desired, you can refrigerate until ready to cook.) In 12-inch skillet over medium heat, in remaining tablespoon hot olive oil, cook falafel patties until golden and crisp, turning once.
5. Serve with any of the above serving suggestions.
