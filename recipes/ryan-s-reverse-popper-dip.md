## Ingredients

- 8 slices bacon
- 1 teaspoon canola oil
- 1 teaspoon minced sweet onion
- 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
- 3 tablespoons dry bread crumbs
- 2 (8 ounce) packages Neufchatel cheese, at room temperature
- 2 cups shredded reduced-fat sharp Cheddar cheese
- 10  jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil; place a baking rack atop the aluminum foil.
3. Lay bacon strips in a single layer on the baking rack.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until crisp, 25 to 35 minutes.
5. Drain the bacon slices on a paper towel-lined plate.
6. Increase oven temperature to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
7. Heat canola oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion in the hot oil until slightly softened, about 2 minutes.
8. Add garlic; cook until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
9. Remove from heat and cool.
10. Coarsely chop 4 slices bacon; mix with bread crumbs in a bowl.
11. Coarsely chop the remaining 4 slices bacon to mix with dip.
12. Mix Neufchatel cheese, Cheddar cheese, onion-garlic mixture, chopped bacon, jalapeno peppers, cumin, and oregano in the bowl of a stand mixer fitted with paddle.
13. Spoon mixture using a rubber spatula into an 8-inch pie pan.
14. Sprinkle bread crumb-bacon mixture over dip.
15. Bake in the preheated oven until dip is bubbling and topping is lightly browned, 12 to 15 minutes.
