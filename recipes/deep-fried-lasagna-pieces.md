## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 pound ground Italian sausage
- 1 (8 ounce) package manicotti shells
- 1 (24 ounce) carton cottage cheese, drained
- 2 (16 ounce) jars pizza sauce, divided
- 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
- vegetable oil for frying
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 3  eggs
- 2 cups bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add beef and Italian sausage; cook and stir until browned, about 8 minutes.
3. Drain excess grease.
4. Let cool slightly.
5. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
6. Cook manicotti shells in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, about 7 minutes.
7. Drain.
8. Mix cooled beef and sausage, cottage cheese, 1 jar pizza sauce, and mozzarella cheese together in a large bowl.
9. Stuff shells generously with mixture.
10. Arrange stuffed shells on a baking sheet and freeze until firm, 15 to 20 minutes.
11. Cut each shell into 2 or 3 lasagna pieces.
12. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
13. Place flour in a bowl.
14. Beat eggs in another bowl.
15. Pour bread crumbs onto a shallow plate.
16. Dredge stuffed lasagna pieces in flour.
17. Dip in beaten eggs.
18. Roll in bread crumbs until coated.
19. Fry 4 breaded lasagna pieces at a time in the hot oil until golden brown, 3 to 5 minutes.
20. Repeat with remaining pieces.
21. Pour remaining jar of pizza sauce into a bowl for dipping.
