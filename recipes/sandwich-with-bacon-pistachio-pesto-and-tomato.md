## Ingredients

- ½ cup shelled, salted whole pistachios
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 bunch fresh cilantro
- 3 cloves garlic
- ½ cup olive oil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 slices turkey bacon
- 2 slices whole wheat sandwich bread
- olive oil, or as needed, divided
- 4 slices Roma tomato
- salt and pepper to taste
- ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Combine pistachio nuts, lime juice, cilantro, and garlic in the bowl of a food processor; pulse until blended.
2. Pour olive oil in slowly, with the processor running, until pesto is smooth.
3. Season with salt and pepper; set aside.
4. Place bacon in a large skillet and cook over medium-high heat, turning occasionally, until crisp, about 10 minutes.
5. Remove bacon from pan and drain on paper towels.
6. When cool enough to handle, tear each strip of bacon in 1/2 so you have 4 pieces.
7. Brush both sides of bread slices with 1 tablespoon olive oil.
8. Place remaining olive oil in the skillet.
9. Cook bread in the skillet over medium heat, turning as necessary, until both sides are browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
10. Remove bread from pan.
11. Meanwhile, season tomato slices liberally with salt and pepper.
12. To assemble sandwich, place 4 pieces of bacon on 1 slice of bread.
13. Top with Parmesan cheese, tomato slices, 2 tablespoons pistachio pesto, and the remaining slice of bread.
