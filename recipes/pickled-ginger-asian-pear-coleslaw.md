## Ingredients

- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ⅓ cup seasoned rice vinegar
- ½ teaspoon yellow miso paste, or to taste
- 1 teaspoon chile-garlic sauce (such as Sriracha®), or to taste
- ½ small head green cabbage, thinly sliced
- 1 large Asian pear, thinly sliced
- ⅓ cup finely sliced pickled ginger
- ¼ cup sliced green onions
- 1 teaspoon toasted sesame seeds
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Whisk together mayonnaise, rice vinegar, miso, and chile-garlic sauce in a large bowl.
2. Stir cabbage, pear, and ginger into the mayonnaise mixture until well coated.
3. Stir in green onions and sesame seeds.
4. Season with salt to taste.
