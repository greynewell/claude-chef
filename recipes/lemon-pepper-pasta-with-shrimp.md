## Ingredients

- 3 cups uncooked dried cavatappi or fusilli pasta
- 2 squares Lemon Pepper Saute Express® Saute Starter
- 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled, deveined
- 1 (6 ounce) package fresh baby spinach leaves
- 1 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
2. Drain.
3. Melt Saute Express® squares in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-low heat just until bubbles begin to form.
4. Add shrimp.
5. Saute 5-7 minutes or until shrimp turn pink.
6. Add spinach and heat until wilted.
7. Toss in pasta and cheese until coated.
