## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground chicken
- ½ cup chopped cilantro
- ¼ cup dry bread crumbs
- ¼ cup grated onion
- 2 cloves garlic, grated
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- ¼ cup peanut butter
- 4 tablespoons sesame oil
- 3 tablespoons white sugar
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons chopped peanuts, or to taste
- toothpicks

## Instructions

1. Mix chicken, cilantro, bread crumbs, onion, garlic, salt, and pepper; roll tablespoonfuls of the mixture into balls.
2. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add meatballs and cook until no longer pink in the center, 6 to 8 minutes.
3. Mix peanut butter, sesame oil, sugar, and soy sauce together in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave until sauce is heated through, about 1 minute.
4. Toss meatballs with 1/2 of the sauce. Sprinkle with peanuts. Serve with toothpicks and remaining sauce for dipping.
