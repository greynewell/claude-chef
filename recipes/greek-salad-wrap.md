## Ingredients

- 16  grape tomatoes, quartered
- ½ large cucumber, diced
- ½  avocado, diced
- ⅓ cup shredded lettuce
- 10  black olives, quartered
- 8  green olives, quartered
- ¼ cup feta cheese crumbles
- ¼ cup shredded carrots
- ¼ cup zesty Italian dressing
- 2  spinach-flavored wraps
- ½ small spaghetti squash, seeded
- 3 slices deli turkey  (Optional)
- ½ cup grilled chicken  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place tomatoes, cucumber, avocado, lettuce, black olives, green olives, feta cheese, and carrots in a large resealable plastic bag.
2. Pour in Italian dressing.
3. Seal bag and toss to coat ingredients with dressing.
4. Microwave spaghetti squash on high for 5 minutes.
5. Cool until easily handled.
6. Scrape spaghetti squash with a fork and spread onto wraps.
7. Lay turkey and chicken on top.
8. Use a spoon to scoop out ingredients from the dressing onto the wraps.
9. Roll up wraps and slice each one in half.
