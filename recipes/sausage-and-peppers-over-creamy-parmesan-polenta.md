## Ingredients

- 4 cups chicken stock
- 2 cups half-and-half
- 2 ½ teaspoons sea salt, divided
- 1 ¼ cups coarsely ground cornmeal (polenta)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 (4 ounce) links hot Italian sausage
- 1  red bell pepper, sliced
- 1  yellow bell pepper, sliced
- 1  red onion, sliced
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup dry white wine
- 1 cup basil marinara sauce
- ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
- ¼ cup butter
- ground black pepper to taste
- 5 large basil leaves, rolled and thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken stock, half-and-half, and 1 1/2 teaspoon salt in a large saucepan; cover and bring to a boil.
2. Whisk in polenta.
3. Reduce heat to low; cook, whisking often, until polenta is soft, about 15 minutes.
4. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over high heat.
5. Add sausage links; cook, turning often, until browned on all sides, about 6 minutes.
6. Transfer to a plate.
7. Stir remaining 1 teaspoon salt, red bell pepper, yellow bell pepper, red onion, and oregano into the skillet.
8. Cook over medium heat, stirring often, until onion is translucent, about 6 minutes.
9. Add garlic; cook and stir for 1 minute.
10. Reduce heat to low; pour in white wine.
11. Stir in marinara sauce.
12. Slice sausages and return to the skillet.
13. Cook, covered, until no longer pink in the center, about 3 minutes.
14. Stir 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese and butter into polenta until melted.
15. Season with black pepper.
16. Divide polenta among 4 serving plates.
17. Top with sausage and pepper mixture.
18. Garnish with remaining 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese and basil.
