## Ingredients

- 9  lasagna noodles
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of chicken soup
- 2 cups milk
- 1 pound kielbasa
- 1 (20 ounce) can sauerkraut, drained
- 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
3. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
4. In a blender or with an electric mixer, blend mushroom soup, cream of chicken soup and milk until smooth.
5. Cut sausage in half lengthwise and slice thinly.
6. In a 9x13 inch dish, layer 1 cup soup mixture, 3 noodles, half the sauerkraut, half the sausage and a third of the cheese.
7. Repeat.
8. Top with remaining 3 noodles and remaining soup mixture.
9. Cover with foil.
10. Bake in preheated oven 25 minutes, then uncover and bake 15 minutes more.
11. Sprinkle with remaining cheese when still hot.
