## Ingredients

- 16  asparagus spears, trimmed
- 4 slices mozzarella cheese, halved
- 4 thin slices  prosciutto
- 3 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Fill a large bowl with cold water.
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
3. Add asparagus and cook uncovered until blanched, about 2 minutes.
4. Drain in a colander and immediately refresh in cold water for several minutes to stop the cooking process.
5. Drain.
6. Place 2 asparagus spears side by side and top with 2 half-slices of mozzarella cheese.
7. Top cheese with 2 more asparagus spears.
8. Wrap bundle in a slice of prosciutto so mozzarella cheese is tightly encased.
9. Repeat with remaining asparagus, mozzarella cheese, and prosciutto to create 4 parcels.
10. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat.
11. Carefully fry parcels in the hot oil until prosciutto has crisped and mozzarella cheese oozes, about 5 minutes per side.
12. Season with salt and pepper.
13. Serve warm.
