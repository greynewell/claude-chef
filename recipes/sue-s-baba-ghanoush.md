## Ingredients

- 1  large eggplant
- 2 tablespoons tahini
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Pierce the eggplant 4 or 5 times with the tip of a paring knife.
2. Microwave on High for 6 to 8 minutes until softened, then place on a plate, and allow to cool to room temperature.
3. Peel off the skin and stem, discard.
4. Roughly chop the eggplant and place into the bowl of a blender along with the tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and olive oil.
5. Puree until smooth, adding water if needed to make a thick paste; season to taste with salt.
