## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (1/2 inch) piece fresh ginger root, minced
- 6 ounces small shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 quart chicken stock
- 1 (8 ounce) container tofu, diced
- ⅓ cup chopped asparagus
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 tablespoon cornstarch  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon water, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large saucepan over high heat.
2. Add garlic and ginger.
3. Cook until fragrant and lightly browned, about 2 minutes.
4. Stir in shrimp.
5. Saute until opaque, about 3 minutes.
6. Transfer shrimp to a bowl.
7. Pour chicken stock into the saucepan and bring to a boil.
8. Reduce heat to medium; add tofu, asparagus, salt and cayenne pepper.
9. Reduce heat to a simmer.
10. Stir cornstarch into a bowl with enough water to form a thin paste.
11. Stir into the soup and continue to simmer until clear and thickened, about 1 minute more.
12. Stir shrimp back into soup and serve.
