## Ingredients

- 2 (11.5 ounce) cans condensed bean with bacon soup
- 1 (1 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- 8 ounces sour cream
- ¼ cup salsa
- ½ cup shredded Cheddar cheese

## Instructions

1. Place the soup, seasoning mix, sour cream, and salsa in a slow cooker and mix together.
2. Top with cheese and heat on low until cheese melts, about 1 hour.
