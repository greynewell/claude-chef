## Ingredients

- 1 (64 ounce) bottle tomato and clam juice cocktail
- ¼ cup ketchup
- lemons, juiced
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 large cucumber, seeded and diced
- ½ pound cooked small shrimp
- 4 ounces imitation crabmeat, chopped
- 1  green bell pepper, chopped  (Optional)
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
- 1  tomato, diced

## Instructions

1. Mix tomato and clam juice cocktail, ketchup, lemon juice, cilantro, cucumber, shrimp, imitation crabmeat, and green bell pepper in a 2-quart container.
2. Chill 8 hours or overnight and serve in bowls topped with diced avocado and tomato.
