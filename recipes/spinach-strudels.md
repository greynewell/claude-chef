## Ingredients

- ½ cup olive oil
- 1 bunch green onions, chopped
- 2 (10 ounce) packages frozen chopped spinach, thawed, well drained
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- 3 extra large eggs, lightly beaten
- 7 ounces feta cheese, crumbled
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 40 sheets frozen phyllo pastry, thawed in refrigerator
- 1 cup unsalted butter, melted
- ½ cup plain bread crumbs

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. In a medium skillet, heat olive oil over a medium heat.
3. Stir in green onions and cook until soft, about 5 minutes.
4. Set aside.
5. Meanwhile in a large bowl, combine spinach, dill, eggs, feta, salt and pepper.
6. Add reserved green onions, and mix well.
7. Unfold 1 sheet of the phyllo pastry, brush with melted butter, and sprinkle lightly with breadcrumbs.
8. Lay another sheet directly on top of the first sheet; repeat butter and breadcrumb steps.
9. Continue to do this until you have 10 layers.
10. Spoon 3/4 cup of spinach mixture (in a sausage-like shape) along longer edge of the top sheet.
11. Roll it up, place on baking sheet, and brush lightly with butter.
12. Score off 1-inch rounds by marking with a knife for easier cutting later.
13. Repeat process with the remaining pastry sheets.
14. You will have 4 rolls total when finished.
15. Bake in a preheated oven for 12 minutes or until edges are lightly brown.
16. Cut into individual servings where you have marked.
17. Serve immediately.
