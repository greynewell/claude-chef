## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
- 2  carrots, diced
- 1  zucchini, diced
- 3 small yellow squash, diced
- 4 ½ cups chicken broth or vegetable broth
- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
- 1 small onion, diced
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 cup pearl barley
- 2 tablespoons butter  (Optional)
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat 1 teaspoon vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir carrots 2 to 3 minutes.
2. Add zucchini and yellow squash; cook until tender, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Heat chicken broth in a large saucepan over medium heat; reduce heat to low and keep warm.
4. Heat 1 teaspoon vegetable oil in a separate large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir onion until lightly browned and tender, about 10 minutes.
5. Add garlic and thyme; cook until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
6. Cook and stir barley into onion mixture until lightly toasted, 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Pour about 2 cups warm broth into barley mixture; reduce heat to low, and simmer, stirring occasionally, until broth is absorbed, 5 to 10 minutes.
8. Add remaining broth, 1/2 cup at a time, allowing broth to absorb before each addition, until barley is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
9. Stir in carrot mixture, butter, and Parmesan cheese until cheese melts, 1 to 2 minutes.
10. Season with salt and black pepper.
