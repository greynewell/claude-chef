## Ingredients

- 1 cup cold cooked sushi rice
- 1 sheet nori (dry seaweed)
- 1 tablespoon masago
- ½  deep-fried soft-shell crab
- 3 slices red bell pepper
- 2 leaves leaf lettuce
- ½ tablespoon okonomiyaki sauce
- ½  avocado, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Spread rice on 1/2 of the sheet of nori.
2. Sprinkle masago over the rice.
3. Flip nori over and place on a sushi rolling mat.
4. Add crab, bell pepper, lettuce, and okonomiyaki sauce.
5. Roll nori over ingredients into a roll.
6. Place avocado slices on top of the sushi roll, then cover with plastic wrap and press on by rolling in the sushi mat again.
7. Slice the roll into about 6 pieces and remove the plastic wrap.
8. Place sushi on a plate and drizzle with additional okonomiyaki sauce.
