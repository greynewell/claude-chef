## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons shichimi togarashi (Japanese seven spice), or to taste
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 pinch salt, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 (1 pound) package frozen cooked edamame, thawed

## Instructions

1. Whisk shichimi togarashi, garlic powder, and salt together in a small bowl.
2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Cook and stir edamame in hot oil until crunchy and hot throughout, 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Sprinkle shichimi mixture over the top and toss to coat.
