## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 10  boneless, skinless chicken thighs
- ¾ cup honey
- ¾ cup lite soy sauce
- 3 tablespoons ketchup
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
- 1 (20 ounce) can pineapple tidbits, drained with juice reserved
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- ¼ cup water

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat, and cook chicken thighs just until evenly browned on all sides.
2. Place thighs in a slow cooker.
3. In a bowl, mix honey, soy sauce, ketchup, garlic, ginger, and reserved pineapple juice.
4. Pour into the slow cooker.
5. Cover, and cook 4 hours on High.
6. Stir in pineapple tidbits just before serving.
7. Mix the cornstarch and water in a small bowl.
8. Remove thighs from slow cooker.
9. Blend the cornstarch mixture into remaining sauce in the slow cooker to thicken.
10. Serve sauce over the chicken.
