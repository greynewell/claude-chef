## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup diced tomatoes
- ¼ cup chopped fresh mint
- 2 ounces sliced black olives
- 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon sea salt
- 6 (6 inch) corn tortillas
- 3 cups mixed greens
- 1 large avocado, sliced
- ½ cup crumbled queso fresco

## Instructions

1. Stir garbanzo beans, tomatoes, mint, olives, balsamic vinegar, and sea salt together in a bowl.
2. Heat a skillet over medium heat.
3. Cook tortillas, one at a time, in hot skillet until warmed through and pliable, 1 to 2 minutes.
4. Spoon garbanzo bean mixture into warm tortillas.
5. Top with greens, avocado slices, and queso fresco.
