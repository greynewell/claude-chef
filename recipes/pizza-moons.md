## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
- 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- 16 slices pepperoni
- ½ cup Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Unroll crescents onto work surface.
3. Top each piece of dough with shredded cheese and a few slices of pepperoni.
4. Sprinkle on a bit more cheese, and roll into crescent shape.
5. Place pizza "moons" on a baking sheet.
6. Bake until crescents are golden brown, 12 to 15 minutes.
7. Remove from oven, and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
