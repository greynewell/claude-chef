## Ingredients

- ½ large pink grapefruit, peeled and membranes removed
- 1 cup blueberries
- 2 tablespoons minced red onion
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 1  jalapeno pepper, chopped
- 1 teaspoon honey

## Instructions

1. Dice grapefruit into blueberry-size pieces and transfer to a large bowl.
2. Add blueberries, onion, cilantro, lime juice, jalapeno pepper, and honey; mix well.
