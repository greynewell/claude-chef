## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1  egg
- ¼ cup dry bread crumbs
- 1  onion, diced
- 1 cup packed brown sugar
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ cups water
- ¼ cup distilled white vinegar
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce

## Instructions

1. Mix beef, onion, egg, and bread crumbs together in a medium bowl until well combined; shape into balls, about 1 1/2-inches in size.
2. Cook meatballs in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat until evenly browned, about 10 minutes; set aside.
3. Stir water, sugar, vinegar, soy sauce, and flour together in a large saucepan until well combined. Add meatballs and bring to a boil; reduce heat to low and simmer, stirring often, for 30 minutes.
