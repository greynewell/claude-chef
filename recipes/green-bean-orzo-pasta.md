## Ingredients

- 1 ½ cups orzo pasta
- 1 (12 ounce) bag fresh green beans, trimmed
- 3 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- ½ cup pine nuts, or more to taste
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can petite diced tomatoes, drained and rinsed
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook orzo in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer.
5. Bring water to a boil.
6. Add green beans, cover, and steam until tender, 2 to 4 minutes.
7. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat; cook and stir pine nuts until toasted and fragrant, 2 to 4 minutes.
8. Combine orzo, green beans, pine nuts, tomatoes, remaining olive oil, Parmesan cheese, basil, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
