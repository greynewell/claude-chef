## Ingredients

- ½ pound fresh tilapia fillets
- 3 tablespoons lime juice, divided
- 1 tablespoon TACO BELL® HOME ORIGINALS® Taco Seasoning Mix
- ⅓ cup KRAFT Mayo Real Mayonnaise
- 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro, divided
- 3 cups coleslaw blend (cabbage slaw mix)
- 8 (6 inch) corn tortillas, warmed
- ½ cup KRAFT Mexican Style Shredded Four Cheese

## Instructions

1. Brush fish with 1 Tbsp. lime juice; sprinkle with seasoning mix.
2. Cooking in skillet sprayed with cooking spray on medium heat 3 to 3-1/2 min. on each side or until fish flakes easily with fork, drizzling fish with 1 Tbsp. of the remaining lime juice for the last minute.
3. Flake with fork into bite-size pieces.
4. Mix mayo, remaining lime juice and 1 Tbsp. cilantro.
5. Add to coleslaw blend; mix lightly.
6. Top tortillas with coleslaw, fish, cheese and remaining cilantro.
