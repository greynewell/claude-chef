## Ingredients

- 1 (13.25 ounce) package dried whole wheat rotini pasta
- 2 (5 ounce) cans skinless, boneless salmon, drained
- 1  cucumber, thinly sliced
- 2 (5.3 ounce) containers Greek yogurt
- ⅔ cup mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons dried dill weed
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 2  Roma tomatoes, thinly sliced

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
2. Stir in rotini, and return to a boil.
3. Cook uncovered, stirring occasionally, until rotini has cooked through, but is still firm to the bite, 10 to 12 minutes.
4. Drain and rinse with cold water.
5. Transfer pasta to a large bowl.
6. Toss salmon and cucumber in with rotini, flaking the salmon as you stir.
7. Combine Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, dill, and onion powder in a small bowl.
8. Spoon yogurt mixture over rotini and gently combine.
9. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
10. Arrange slices of Roma tomatoes on the salad before serving.
