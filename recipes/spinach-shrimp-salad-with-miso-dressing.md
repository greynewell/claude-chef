## Ingredients

- ¼ cup rice wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 tablespoons white miso paste
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup canola oil
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- ½ cup chopped green onions (scallions)
- 3 cups fresh spinach
- 1 (16 ounce) package cooked shrimp
- 1 tablespoon sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Combine vinegar, honey, white miso paste, soy sauce, sugar, salt, and pepper in a blender; blend until smooth.
2. Add canola oil and sesame oil; blend until dressing is emulsified and thick.
3. Pour dressing into a large bowl; stir in green onions.
4. Add spinach and toss to coat.
5. Transfer spinach to a serving plate.
6. Arrange shrimp on top.
7. Sprinkle sesame seeds over shrimp.
