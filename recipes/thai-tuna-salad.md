## Ingredients

- ¼ cup Asian fish sauce
- ½ cup fresh lime juice
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 ¼ pounds fresh tuna steaks, cubed
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 4  fresh tomatoes, chopped
- 1 bunch green onion, finely chopped
- 1 small fresh red chile pepper, seeded and chopped
- ½ cup fresh cilantro leaves
- ½ cup fresh basil

## Instructions

1. In a small bowl, mix together fish sauce, lime juice, and honey.
2. Place tuna in a large, plastic, resealable bag, and pour fish sauce mixture over the tuna.
3. Refrigerate for 1 hour.
4. Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a wok over high heat.
5. When the oil is very hot, almost smoking, place 1/2 tuna in pan; cook and stir for 1 minute, or until desired doneness.
6. Transfer cooked fish to a large bowl.
7. Repeat with remaining oil and tuna.
8. Toss warm fish with tomatoes, green onion, red chile pepper, cilantro, and basil.
9. Serve.
