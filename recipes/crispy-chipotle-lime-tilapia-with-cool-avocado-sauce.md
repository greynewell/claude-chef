## Ingredients

- 1  canned chipotle pepper in adobo sauce
- 2 tablespoons adobo sauce from the can
- ¼ cup salsa
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 4  tilapia fillets
- ½ (8 ounce) package tortilla chips, crushed
- 1 small avocado - peeled, pitted, and cut into large chunks
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 1 tablespoon lime juice
- 3 tablespoons milk
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Grease a baking sheet with cooking spray.
3. Combine the chipotle pepper, adobo sauce, salsa, and 1 tablespoon lime juice in a blender and blend until smooth; brush the mixture over both sides of each tilapia fillet.
4. Spread the crushed tortilla chips into the bottom of a deep dish; dredge the coated fillets in the chips to coat.
5. Arrange the fillets on the prepared baking pan.
6. Bake the fillets 15 minutes; turn and lightly spray with cooking spray.
7. Continue to bake until golden brown, another 10 to 15 minutes.
8. While the fish bakes, place the avocado, sour cream, and 1 tablespoon lime juice and blend until smooth.
9. Stir in the milk 1 tablespoon at a time until the consistency is similar to ranch dressing.
10. Season with salt and pepper.
11. Spoon the sauce over the baked fillets to serve.
