## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon butter, or more as needed
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar, or more to taste
- 12  eggs
- 2 tablespoons milk, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons pesto
- 1 pinch salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 cups cooked salmon, chopped
- 1 ½ cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat and cook onion until soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
2. Add rice vinegar.
3. Beat eggs in a large bowl and mix in milk and pesto.
4. Season with salt and pepper.
5. Pour into skillet; cook and stir for 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Add salmon and keep stirring until eggs start to set on the edges of the skillet, 5 to 8 minutes.
7. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese; cook and stir until eggs are scrambled to your desired consistency.
