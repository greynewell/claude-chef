## Ingredients

- 3  garlic scapes
- 1 (15 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
- ⅓ cup tahini
- ¼ cup freshly squeezed lime juice, or more to taste
- ⅛ teaspoon chili powder
- 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 teaspoon snipped garlic chives  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Remove the blossom end and the opposite fibrous end of each garlic scape.
2. Remove any fibrous skin by breaking the scapes into 1-inch pieces and peeling it off.
3. Keep the skin on young and pliable scapes; chop coarsely.
4. Place drained garbanzo beans in the bowl of a food processor.
5. Add chopped scapes, tahini, lime juice, and chili powder.
6. Process until smooth, scraping down the sides of the bowl.
7. Add water, 1 tablespoon at a time, if hummus is too stiff.
8. Pulse until hummus has the desired consistency.
9. Season with salt.
10. Transfer to a bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with snipped garlic chives.
