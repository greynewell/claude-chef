## Ingredients

- 1 pound jumbo prawns
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 2 tablespoons minced garlic
- 2 tablespoons thinly sliced lemon grass
- 5  fresh Thai chile peppers, sliced thin
- 1  shallot, sliced thin
- 2  kaffir lime leaves
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce, or to taste
- 1  lime, juiced
- 1 tablespoon Thai roasted chilli paste (nam prik pao)
- 1 tablespoon torn fresh mint leaves

## Instructions

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat; lightly oil the grate.
2. Cook the shrimp on the hot grill until they are bright pink on the outside and the meat is no longer transparent in the center, 5 to 10 minutes.
3. Arrange the prawns on a serving platter.
4. Heat the oil in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Fry the garlic in the hot oil until brown, 7 to 10 minutes.
6. Remove from heat and stir the lemon grass, chile peppers, shallot, lime leaves, fish sauce, lime juice, and chilli paste into the garlic; toss to combine.
7. Spoon the sauce over the prawns.
8. Garnish with the mint to serve.
