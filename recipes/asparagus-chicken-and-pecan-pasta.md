## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package penne pasta
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic
- 1  red bell pepper, chopped
- 2 bunches asparagus, trimmed and cut into 1 inch pieces
- 1 cup chicken broth
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil
- salt to taste
- ½ teaspoon pepper
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 1 pound grilled chicken breast strips
- 1 ½ cups grated Parmesan cheese
- ½ cup pecan halves  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add penne pasta and cook until al dente, 8 to 10 minutes; drain.
3. Heat the olive oil in a Dutch oven or large pot over medium heat.
4. Stir in the garlic, red pepper, and asparagus; cook and stir 5 minutes until the garlic softens and mellows.
5. Pour in the chicken broth, and bring to a boil over medium-high heat.
6. Simmer until the vegetables have softened, then add the basil, salt, pepper, butter, and chicken.
7. Cook and stir a few minutes until the chicken is hot.
8. Stir in the cooked pasta, then fold in the Parmesan cheese and pecan halves to serve.
