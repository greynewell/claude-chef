## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 2 tablespoons sour cream
- 2 teaspoons curry powder
- 1 (8 ounce) jar mango chutney (such as Major Grey®), chunks removed
- 1 cup shredded coconut, or to taste
- ½ cup chopped cashews, or to taste
- 4  green onions, chopped, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix cream cheese, sour cream, and curry powder together in a bowl; spread mixture onto the middle of a plate.
2. Top cream cheese mixture with a layer of chutney, a layer of coconut, a layer of cashews, and a layer of green onions.
