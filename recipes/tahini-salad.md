## Ingredients

- 2  pickle-size Persian cucumbers, diced
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 3  radishes, diced  (Optional)
- 1  green onion, sliced
- 10  mint leaves, sliced
- ⅓ bunch parsley, chopped
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ¾ cup tahini
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- Salt, to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine the cucumber, tomato, and radish in a bowl with the green onion, mint, and parsley.
2. In a small bowl, stir together the garlic, tahini, lemon juice, and olive oil until smooth; season to taste with salt.
3. Toss the vegetables with the dressing until combined.
4. Cover, and refrigerate for 30 minutes before serving.
