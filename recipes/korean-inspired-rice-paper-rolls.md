## Ingredients

- 6 ounces firm tofu, cut into rectangles
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or to taste
- 4  rice paper wrappers
- ½ cup fresh spinach, or to taste, stems removed
- ½ cup kimchi
- ½ (15.5 ounce) can black beans
- ¼ cup peanuts, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Saute tofu with oil and soy sauce in a pan over medium heat until golden brown, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Wet a rice paper wrapper in a large bowl of water then lay flat on a plate.
3. Add 5 to 6 leaves spinach to the center.
4. Place 1 to 2 pieces tofu on top.
5. Add enough kimchi to cover the tofu, 1 to 2 tablespoons black beans, and several peanuts.
6. While the rice paper is still soft, fold in sides and roll up around the filling.
7. Repeat to make 3 remaining rolls.
