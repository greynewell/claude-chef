## Ingredients

- 2 (5 ounce) red snapper fillets
- 1 teaspoon ground paprika
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 3 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 4 thin slices lemon, or more to taste
- 1 large tomato, seeded and finely chopped
- 3  shallots, chopped
- 3 cloves garlic cloves, finely chopped
- 1  lemon, zested
- 1 teaspoon grated ginger
- ½ cup dry white wine
- 1 tablespoon butter

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Coat red snapper fillets with paprika, salt, and pepper.
3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
4. Add red snapper; cook until browned, 1 to 2 minutes per side.
5. Transfer to a large baking dish; top with lemon slices.
6. Combine tomato, shallots, garlic, lemon zest, and ginger in the skillet; cook and stir until fragrant, about 1 minute.
7. Stir in white wine.
8. Season with salt and pepper.
9. Add butter; reduce heat and simmer for 2 to 3 minutes.
10. Pour mixture over red snapper.
11. Bake in the preheated oven until red snapper flakes easily with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes.
