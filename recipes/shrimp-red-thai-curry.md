## Ingredients

- 2 (14 ounce) cans coconut milk
- 2 tablespoons red Thai curry paste
- 1 tablespoon fish sauce
- 1  fresh red chili pepper, seeded and minced
- 24 large shrimp, peeled and deveined

## Instructions

1. In a wok, combine coconut milk, curry paste, fish sauce, and minced pepper.
2. Slowly bring to a simmer over medium low heat, stirring frequently.
3. Reduce heat to low, and add shrimp.
4. Cook uncovered, stirring frequently, until prawns are cooked and flavors mellow, about 15 minutes.
