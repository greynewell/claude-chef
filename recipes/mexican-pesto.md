## Ingredients

- ¼ cup hulled pumpkin seeds (pepitas)
- 1 bunch cilantro
- ¼ cup grated cotija cheese
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1  serrano chile pepper, seeded
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 6 tablespoons olive oil

## Instructions

1. Place the pumpkin seeds in a food processor or blender; pulse until coarsely chopped.
2. Add cilantro, cheese, garlic, chile pepper, salt, and olive oil; cover and process until smooth, scraping the sides of the bowl with a spatula as necessary.
