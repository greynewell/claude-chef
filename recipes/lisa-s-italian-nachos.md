## Ingredients

- 2 pounds bulk Italian sausage
- 1 pound pepperoni, sliced
- 1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
- 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa
- 1 (18 ounce) package tortilla chips
- 4 cups crumbled feta cheese
- 1  onion, chopped
- 1 cup sliced jalapeno peppers
- 2 cups black olives, drained and sliced
- 1 ½ cups sour cream

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat, and add the Italian sausage, sliced pepperoni and crushed red pepper flakes.
2. Cook and stir until the sausage is well browned and cooked through, about 8 minutes.
3. Drain fat from skillet.
4. Stir in the spaghetti sauce and salsa.
5. Cook until meat and sauce are hot, about 5 minutes.
6. Spread the tortilla chips on a very large platter.
7. Spoon the hot meat sauce evenly over the chips and top with the feta cheese, chopped onions, jalapenos, black olives, and sour cream.
