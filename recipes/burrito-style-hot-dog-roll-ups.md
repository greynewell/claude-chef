## Ingredients

- 4  hot dogs
- 4 slices American cheese
- 4 (6 inch) flour tortillas
- 2 tablespoons ketchup, or to taste
- 2 tablespoons mustard, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place hot dogs in a medium frying pan over medium heat.
2. Cook, turning occasionally, until cooked on all sides, about 5 minutes.
3. Place 1 slice of American cheese in the middle of each tortilla.
4. Place cooked hot dogs over the cheese slices.
5. Roll up tortillas around the filling.
6. Place all 4 roll-ups on a microwave-safe plate.
7. Cook in the microwave for 10 to 12 seconds to fully melt the cheese and warm the tortillas.
8. Drizzle ketchup and mustard over the roll-ups.
