## Ingredients

- 1 ¼ cups frozen shelled edamame (green soybeans), thawed
- 2 teaspoons olive oil
- ½ teaspoon chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon dried basil
- ¼ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ⅛ teaspoon paprika
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Place the thawed edamame into a mixing bowl, drizzle with the olive oil, then sprinkle with chili powder, basil, onion powder, cumin, paprika, and pepper.
3. Toss until the edamame are evenly coated with the oil and spices.
4. Spread into a 9x13 inch glass baking dish in a single layer.
5. Bake uncovered in the preheated oven until the beans begin to brown, 12 to 15 minutes.
6. Stir once halfway through cooking.
