## Ingredients

- 3 cups water
- 1 cup wheat berries
- 1 teaspoon olive oil, or as needed
- 5 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 cup pitted and finely chopped kalamata olives
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Bring water to a boil in a saucepan; add wheat berries, lower heat to medium-low, and cook until wheat berries are tender and water is absorbed, 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
2. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat; saute garlic until fragrant, 1 to 2 minutes.
3. Add olives; cook and stir for 10 minutes.
4. Add wheat berries to olive mixture.
5. Cook and stir wheat berries-olive mixture until heated through, about 5 minutes.
6. Season with salt.
