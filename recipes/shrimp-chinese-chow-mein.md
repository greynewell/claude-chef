## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped celery
- 1  onion, diced
- 1  red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 pound fresh shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 (15 ounce) can mixed vegetables, drained
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ½ cup green bell pepper, chopped
- 4 cups chow mein noodles

## Instructions

1. In a large saucepan, combine celery, onions, red pepper, and shrimp.
2. Cook over medium heat until shrimp are pink.
3. Mix in canned vegetables and mushroom soup, soy sauce, and green pepper.
4. Heat thoroughly.
5. Serve over chow mein noodles.
