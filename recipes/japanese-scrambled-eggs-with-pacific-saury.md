## Ingredients

- ½ cup water
- 1 (5 ounce) can grilled Pacific saury
- 1 tablespoon mirin (sweet cooking rice wine)
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce, or more to taste
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 2 tablespoons chopped scallion

## Instructions

1. Bring water to the boil in a skillet.
2. Add saury, mirin, and soy sauce.
3. Return to a boil and slowly pour in beaten eggs, mixing well.
4. Cook eggs until firm and set, 2 to 3 minutes; garnish with scallion.
