## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
- 1 (1.25 ounce) package taco seasoning mix
- 10 (10 inch) flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. In a medium-size mixing bowl, blend together cream cheese, and sour cream.
2. Mix in taco seasonings.
3. Layer mixture onto tortillas; stack the tortillas 5 high, with the mixture layered between them.
4. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
5. Cut into 1 inch squares and serve.
