## Ingredients

- 2 large avocados, diced
- 1 large tomato, diced
- 1 medium onion, diced
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 ears  fresh corn
- 1  lime
- ½ cup olive oil
- 3 tablespoons white vinegar
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- ½ tablespoon salt
- ¼ tablespoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Combine avocados, tomato, onion, and cilantro in a large bowl.
2. Cut corn kernels from the cobs and add to the same bowl.
3. Cut the lime and squeeze juice into the salsa mixture; add olive oil, vinegar, cumin, salt, and pepper.
4. Mix well.
