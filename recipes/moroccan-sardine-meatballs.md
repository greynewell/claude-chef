## Ingredients

- 1 (4 ounce) can sardines, drained
- 2  eggs
- 2 tablespoons dry bread crumbs
- ½ teaspoon ground paprika
- ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
- ½ teaspoon ground ginger
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- ¼  onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can canned diced tomatoes
- 2 tablespoons ground coriander
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin

## Instructions

1. Place sardines in a food processor; puree until smooth.
2. Add eggs, bread crumbs, paprika, turmeric, ginger, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and black pepper.
3. Pulse briefly until well-combined.
4. Shape mixture into small balls.
5. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
6. Add onion and garlic; cook and stir until fragrant, about 5 minutes.
7. Stir in tomatoes, coriander, and cumin.
8. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer sauce until flavors combine, about 15 minutes.
9. Stir in meatballs and simmer until heated through, about 5 minutes.
