## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 medium red onion, sliced
- 3 large fresh portabella mushrooms, sliced, cut in half, stems removed
- 2 tablespoons Old El Paso® taco seasoning mix
- 2 tablespoons water
- 8 each Old El Paso® flour tortillas for soft tacos & fajitas
- 1 cup guacamole
- ½ cup sour cream
- ½ cup crumbled queso fresco cheese

## Instructions

1. In 12-inch nonstick skillet, heat oil over medium-high heat.
2. Cook onion in oil, stirring occasionally, until brown.
3. Add mushrooms.
4. Cook 6 to 8 minutes or until tender.
5. Reduce heat to low.
6. Add   mix and water.
7. Cook about 5 minutes or until water is absorbed.
8. Remove from heat.
9. Spoon filling on tortillas.
10. Add toppings.
11. Roll up tortillas.
