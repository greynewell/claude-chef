## Ingredients

- 2  jumbo hot dogs
- 1 teaspoon taco seasoning mix
- 2  hot dog buns
- ⅓ cup guacamole
- 4 tablespoons salsa
- 6  pickled jalapeno slices

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 390 degrees F (200 degrees C). Make sure the air fryer is at temperature for at least 4 minutes.
2. Cut 5 slits into each hot dog. Evenly rub 1/2 teaspoon taco seasoning over each hot dog.
3. Cook hot dogs in the air fryer basket for 5 minutes. Place hot dogs in buns and return to the basket. Cook until buns are toasted and hot dogs are crisp, about 4 minutes more.
4. Top hot dogs with equal amounts of guacamole, salsa, and jalapenos.
