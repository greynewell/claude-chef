## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 3 cups baby spinach leaves
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon garlic powder
- ⅛ teaspoon onion powder
- 6  sun-dried tomato halves packed in olive oil with herbs, cut into strips
- 1 ounce crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir baby spinach in hot oil until very tender, about 5 minutes.
3. Season spinach evenly with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder, turning with tongs while sprinkling.
4. Add tomatoes; cook and stir until tomatoes begin to soften, about 90 seconds.
5. Sprinkle with feta cheese off heat and toss to serve.
