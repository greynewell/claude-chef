## Ingredients

- nonstick cooking spray
- 2  eggs
- ½ cup milk
- ¾ cup seasoned bread crumbs
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 medium head cauliflower, broken into small florets
- ¼ cup light mayonnaise
- ¼ cup plain fat-free Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons prepared horseradish, or to taste
- ¼  lemon, juiced
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Coat a rimmed baking sheet with nonstick spray.
3. Whisk eggs and milk together in a medium bowl.
4. Pour bread crumbs and flour into separate resealable plastic bags.
5. Shake cauliflower florets in flour, then pour them into the milk mixture.
6. Use a slotted spoon to transfer florets to the bread crumb bag and shake to coat.
7. Pour florets onto the prepared baking sheet and spray the tops with nonstick spray.
8. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 minutes.
9. Meanwhile, combine mayonnaise, yogurt, horseradish, and lemon juice in a bowl.
10. Season with salt and pepper.
11. Refrigerate dip until ready to serve.
12. Turn florets over and continue baking until golden, about 15 minutes more.
13. Serve with dipping sauce.
