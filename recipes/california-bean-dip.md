## Ingredients

- 2 (11 ounce) cans white corn, drained
- 2 (15 ounce) cans black beans, rinsed and drained
- ½ cup Italian-style salad dressing
- 1 cup Ranch-style salad dressing
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 teaspoon hot pepper sauce
- 2 teaspoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a medium bowl, thoroughly mix white corn, black beans, Italian-style salad dressing, ranch-style salad dressing, onion, hot pepper sauce, cilantro, chili powder and ground black pepper.
2. Chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight, before serving.
