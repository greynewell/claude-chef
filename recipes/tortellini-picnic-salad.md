## Ingredients

- 2 (9 ounce) packages cheese tortellini
- 1 (8 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
- 3  roma tomatoes, diced
- 1 (6 ounce) can black olives
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- 1 bunch green onions, sliced
- 5 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 cup white vinegar
- ½ cup olive oil, or as needed
- 1 teaspoon dried basil
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook tortellini in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until pasta floats to the surface, about 5 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Mix tortellini, feta cheese, tomatoes, olives, walnuts, green onions, and garlic together in a large bowl.
5. Whisk vinegar, olive oil, and basil together in a separate small bowl.
6. Pour dressing over tortellini mixture; toss to coat.
7. Sprinkle with salt.
8. Chill in refrigerator for 1 hour before serving.
