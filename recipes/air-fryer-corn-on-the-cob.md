## Ingredients

- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons crumbled cotija cheese
- 1 teaspoon lime juice
- ¼ teaspoon chili powder
- 2 ears corn, shucked and halved
- 4 sprigs fresh cilantro, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Mix mayonnaise, cotija cheese, lime juice, and chili powder in a shallow dish.
3. Roll each piece of corn in the mayonnaise mixture until all sides are covered.
4. Place all 4 pieces of the corn in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 8 minutes. Garnish with cilantro.
