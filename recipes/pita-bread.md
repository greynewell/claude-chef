## Ingredients

- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup pastry flour
- 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
- 1 tablespoon applesauce
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
- 1 tablespoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Dissolve the yeast and sugar in the warm water.
2. Combine the all-purpose flour, pastry flour and salt in a bowl.
3. Stir in the yeast mixture and applesauce and knead.
4. Dough shouldn't be sticky, but it shouldn't be dry either.
5. If too sticky add 1 tablespoon of all-purpose flour till you get the right consistency.
6. If too dry, add 1 tablespoons of water at a time till you get the right consistency.
7. Roll out into a rope and cut into 8 pieces.
8. Shape each piece into a ball and roll out till it's anywhere from a 6 to 8 inch circle There are two ways to cook pita bread.
9. The flavor is a bit different with both.
10. To Bake Pita:  Preheat oven to 500 degrees F (260 degrees C) and put a pita on a wire cake rack.
11. Toss in the rack and cook for 3 minutes or until the bread stops puffing up.
12. When you take it out, smash down the pita (Careful, its hot!) and quickly put it in a plastic freezer bag.
13. To Fry Pita:  Heat olive oil in a skillet over high heat.
14. When almost smoking, place a pita in the pan and cook for a few minutes on each side, till brown spots begin to appear.
15. It should look something like a tortilla when you're done.
16. Put in a plastic bag once it has cooled a bit.
