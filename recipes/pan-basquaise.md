## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 4 large red bell peppers - roasted, peeled and sliced
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 tablespoons white wine vinegar
- 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 (8 ounce) loaves French baguette
- 4  hard-cooked eggs, sliced
- 8  kalamata olives, pitted and halved

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375  degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Heat 3 tablespoons olive oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
3. Saute red peppers for 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Stir in tuna, and season with salt and pepper.
5. Add vinegar, 2 tablespoons parsley and garlic.
6. Cook for 2 to 3 minutes, or until vinegar has evaporated.
7. Remove from heat, and set aside.
8. Slice the bread in half lengthwise, then crosswise into 4 inch pieces.
9. Toast bread in the oven until warm and crisp, but not brown.
10. Place the bread on a serving platter.
11. Top each piece with red pepper tuna mixture.
12. Garnish with sliced egg and a halved olive.
13. Drizzle with remaining olive oil and sprinkle with remaining parsley.
