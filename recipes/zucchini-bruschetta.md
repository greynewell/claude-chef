## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1  zucchini, cubed
- 1  tomato, cubed and drained
- 1 dash balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ground black pepper to taste
- 2 leaves fresh basil, chopped, or more to taste
- 1 small French baguette, thinly sliced
- ½ cup feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
2. Add garlic; cook and stir until golden, about 2 minutes.
3. Add zucchini; cook, stirring occasionally, until starting to soften, 5 to 7 minutes.
4. Remove skillet from heat and stir in tomato, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper.
5. Let cool, about 5 minutes.
6. Stir in basil.
7. Let zucchini mixture marinate in the refrigerator, 20 minutes to overnight.
8. Spread zucchini mixture over baguette slices.
9. Sprinkle feta cheese on top.
