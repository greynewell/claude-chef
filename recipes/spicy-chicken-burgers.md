## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 ½ teaspoons minced garlic
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- 1 pound ground chicken
- 1  egg
- 1 large avocado - halved, pitted, and peeled
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 (8 ounce) container light sour cream
- 1 ½ cups shredded cabbage
- 4  whole wheat Kaiser rolls
- 1 large tomato, sliced  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Mix chili powder, 1 1/2 teaspoon salt, cumin, minced garlic, and black pepper together in a small bowl.
2. Sprinkle 3/4 of the chili powder mixture over ground chicken in a large bowl.
3. Add egg; mix well and shape into 4 patties.
4. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
5. Grill patties until browned and juices run clear, about 5 minutes per side.
6. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
7. Mash avocado in a small bowl; season with garlic powder and 1/4 teaspoon salt.
8. Mix remaining chili powder mixture, sour cream, and shredded cabbage together in a bowl to make coleslaw.
9. Toast Kaiser rolls lightly on the grill.
10. Place chicken patties on the buns.
11. Top each patty with mashed avocado, a large scoop of coleslaw, and sliced tomato.
