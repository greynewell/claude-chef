## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup pizza sauce
- 1 (13 ounce) can refrigerated pizza crust dough
- 1 (7 ounce) package HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Uncured Hard Salami
- 1 (9 ounce) package HORMEL® NATURAL CHOICE® Smoked Deli Ham, cut into strips
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- ¼ teaspoon Italian seasoning
- 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese
- 1  egg, for egg wash

## Instructions

1. Heat oven 400 degrees F.
2. Spray baking sheet with nonstick cooking spray.
3. Unroll dough; place on sprayed baking sheet.
4. Starting at center, press out dough with hands to form a 12x8-inch rectangle.
5. Spread sauce over dough within 1 to 2 inches of the edge.
6. Evenly distribute salami and ham lengthwise down center, within 1/2 inch of short sides.
7. Top with cheese, bell pepper, onion and Italian seasoning.
8. Fold long sides of dough over filling; press edges to seal.
9. Spread beaten egg wash on top of sealed dough and sprinkle with 1 tablespoon of grated Parmesan cheese.
10. Wrap in aluminum foil.
11. Bake 20 to 25 minutes or until crust is golden brown.
