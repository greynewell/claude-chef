## Ingredients

- 3 (5.25 ounce) packages natto
- 3  eggs
- 1 ½ cups cooked rice
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½  cabbage, sliced into thin strips
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ⅛ cup crumbled laver, or to taste
- 1 pinch dried bonito flakes, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine natto and eggs in a bowl; mix well.
2. Add cooked rice and mix using a rice paddle.
3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
4. Spread egg mixture in the skillet and cook without stirring until browned, about 2 minutes.
5. Add cabbage and soy sauce.
6. Stir until mixed in and heated through, about 5 minutes.
7. Season fried rice with salt and pepper.
8. Place portions onto 3 individual plates; sprinkle laver and bonito flakes on top.
