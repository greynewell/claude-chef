## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
- 3  eggs
- 2  egg yolks
- ¾ cup butter, softened
- 3 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ½ teaspoon active dry yeast
- 1  egg white
- 2 tablespoons water

## Instructions

1. Place the first seven ingredients in bread machine in order suggested by your manufacturer.
2. Select dough cycle.
3. Turn finished dough out onto a lightly floured board and knead 5 to 10 times.
4. Separate into 2 or 3 pieces.
5. Roll with hands into strips.
6. Braid or twist strips together.
7. Place onto a parchment lined baking sheet.
8. Set aside to rise in a warm place until doubled in size.
9. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
10. Whisk together 1 egg white and the water.
11. Brush onto the top of the loaf.
12. Bake in the preheated oven until deep golden brown, about 20 minutes.
