## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
- ½ cup sour cream
- ¼ cup picante sauce
- 2 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
- 1 pinch garlic powder
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can chopped black olives, drained
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chilies
- 1 cup finely shredded Cheddar cheese
- 8  flour tortillas

## Instructions

1. Beat cream cheese, sour cream, picante sauce, taco seasoning, and garlic powder together in a small bowl until smooth. Stir olives, chilies, and Cheddar cheese into the cream cheese mixture.
2. Spread about 1/2 cup of cream cheese mixture onto each tortilla. Roll each tortilla like a jelly roll; wrap in plastic wrap. Refrigerate for 2 hours to overnight. Cut into 1-inch slices.
