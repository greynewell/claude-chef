## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 4 cloves garlic, sliced lengthwise
- 1 small onion, sliced lengthwise
- 1  jalapeno pepper, sliced lengthwise
- 1 (1 inch) piece fresh ginger, sliced lengthwise
- 4 tablespoons ground coriander
- 4 tablespoons ground turmeric
- 3 tablespoons ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon cayenne pepper
- 1 teaspoon mustard seeds
- 3  fresh curry leaves
- 3 tablespoons white vinegar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 2 (14 ounce) cans light coconut milk
- 4  tilapia fillets, cut into 1-inch strips
- 1 pinch ground cumin
- 1 pinch salt
- 3 pinches saffron
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil over medium-low heat in a large, deep, non-stick pan.
2. Add garlic, onion, jalapeno, and ginger; cook and stir until onions are softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.
3. Stir in coriander, turmeric, cumin, cayenne, mustard seeds, and curry leaves to the mixture.
4. Cook and stir until fragrant, about 3 minutes.
5. Pour in vinegar and salt; cook and stir for 2 minutes more.
6. Pour coconut milk into the mixture.
7. Bring to a simmer and heat until warm.
8. Season tilapia with cumin and 1 pinch salt.
9. Add tilapia to the simmering coconut milk mixture.
10. Continue to let simmer for 10 minutes.
11. Stir in saffron; let simmer 3 to 5 minutes more.
12. Season with salt and pepper.
13. Serve.
