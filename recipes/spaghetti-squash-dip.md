## Ingredients

- 1  spaghetti squash, halved and seeded
- ¼ cup butter
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cups grated Monterey Jack cheese
- 3 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese for topping

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Lightly grease a baking dish.
3. Place the spaghetti squash cut side down on a lightly greased baking sheet.
4. Place in preheated oven, and bake until soft when a fork is inserted., 30 to 40 minutes.
5. Scoop out the cooked squash, and place in a mixing bowl.
6. Add butter to the squash and stir until butter melts.
7. Stir the mayonnaise, 1 cup Parmesan cheese, and Monterey Jack cheese into the squash mixture until well blended.
8. Pour into the prepared baking dish.
9. Sprinkle with 3 tablespoons Parmesan cheese.
10. Bake in preheated oven until cheese melts, about 20 minutes.
