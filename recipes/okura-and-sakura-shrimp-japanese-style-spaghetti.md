## Ingredients

- 1 (7 ounce) package spaghetti
- 2 tablespoons olive oil, divided
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and chopped
- 8 pods okra, cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon sake
- 1 tablespoon mirin (Japanese sweet rice wine)
- 2 ounces sakura (cherry) shrimp

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
3. Drain, reserving 3 tablespoons of the cooking water.
4. Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a skillet over medium-low heat.
5. Cook and stir red bell pepper in the hot oil until softened, about 5 minutes.
6. Stir in okra, soy sauce, sake, and mirin; cook for 1 minute.
7. Stir cooked spaghetti, reserved cooking water, and remaining 1 tablespoon olive oil into the skillet.
8. Mix in half of the shrimp; cook for 1 minute.
9. Scoop spaghetti mixture into a bowl.
10. Garnish with remaining shrimp.
