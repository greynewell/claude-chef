## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup low-fat sour cream
- 2 tablespoons mango chutney
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon honey
- ¼ teaspoon curry powder
- 4 cups chopped cooked turkey
- 1 cup diced red bell pepper
- 1 cup diced celery
- 1 cup pineapple chunks
- 1 cup chopped orange segments
- ½ cup chopped green onion

## Instructions

1. For the dressing, blend sour cream, chutney, lemon juice, honey, and curry powder in a small bowl.
2. Mix well, and refrigerate until ready to use.
3. In a large bowl, combine turkey, red pepper, celery, pineapple, orange segments, and green onion.
4. Add dressing, and toss well to coat.
5. Refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
