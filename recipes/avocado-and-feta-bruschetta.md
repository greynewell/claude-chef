## Ingredients

- 1 loaf ciabatta bread
- ⅓ cup olive oil
- 2 cloves garlic, finely minced, or more to taste
- 2  avocados - peeled, pitted, and diced
- ⅓ cup crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Cut bread into two 3-inch sections and cut each section in half.
3. Place bread pieces on a baking sheet and drizzle olive oil over each piece.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until toasted, 5 to 7 minutes.
5. Sprinkle garlic over toasted bread pieces.
6. Spoon avocado pieces onto each toast and sprinkle feta cheese over avocado layer.
