## Ingredients

- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ½ cup rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons bonito shavings (dry fish flakes)
- 1 (1 inch) square konbu (kelp)
- 1  orange, quartered

## Instructions

1. In a saucepan, combine the soy sauce, rice vinegar, bonito shavings, konbu and the orange quarters.
2. Let stand for 30 minutes.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. Just as it starts to boil, remove from the heat.
5. Allow to cool, then strain through a cheesecloth lined sieve.
