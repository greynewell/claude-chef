## Ingredients

- ½ (16 ounce) package orecchiette pasta
- 1 cup cream
- 2 slices red onion, chopped
- 6 ounces smoked salmon, cut into strips
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 tablespoon capers
- 1 tablespoon salted butter
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add orecchiette and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 9 to 12 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, combine cream and onion in a heavy saucepan over medium-high heat. Simmer for 2 minutes and remove from heat.
3. Gently stir salmon, Parmesan cheese, capers, butter, and parsley into the cream mixture and serve over pasta.
