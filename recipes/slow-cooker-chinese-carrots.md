## Ingredients

- 2 pounds baby carrots
- ½ cup orange juice concentrate
- ¼ cup tamari sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon grated orange zest
- 1 tablespoon Asian (toasted) sesame oil
- 1 tablespoon honey

## Instructions

1. Place the baby carrots, orange juice concentrate, tamari sauce, garlic, ginger, orange zest, toasted sesame oil, and honey into a slow cooker, and mix well.
2. Set the cooker to Low, cover, and cook for 8 hours.
3. Turn setting to High, and cook for 30 minutes.
