## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 pound ground turkey
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 2 cups chopped kale
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 tablespoon ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- 1 tablespoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
- 1 teaspoon ground paprika
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 (32 ounce) carton chicken broth
- 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
- ½ cup uncooked brown rice
- ½ cup water

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a stockpot over medium-high heat.
2. Cook and stir turkey, onion, and garlic in the hot pot until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Add kale and cook until wilted, about 2 minutes.
4. Stir in brown sugar, chili powder, cumin, oregano, onion powder, smoked and regular paprika, thyme, salt, pepper, and cayenne.
5. Add chicken broth, crushed tomatoes, brown rice, and water; bring to a simmer.
6. Reduce heat to medium-low and cook until rice is tender, about 40 minutes.
