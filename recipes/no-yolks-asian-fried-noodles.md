## Ingredients

- 1 ½ (12 ounce) packages NO YOLKS® Fine Noodles
- 1 tablespoon canola oil
- 4 cups frozen Asian stir-fry vegetables, unthawed
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh garlic
- 3 tablespoons sodium-reduced soy sauce
- 2  Sliced green onion

## Instructions

1. Prepare the noodles according to package directions.
2. Rinse under cold running water and drain well.
3. Set aside.
4. Heat the oil in a large, nonstick wok (or skillet) set over medium-high heat.
5. Add the vegetable blend.
6. Stir-fry for 6 minutes.
7. Add ginger and garlic.
8. Stir-fry for 1 minute.
9. Toss in cooked noodles and soy sauce.
10. Stir-fry for 2 to 3 additional minutes or until well combined and heated through.
11. Garnish with green onions (if using).
