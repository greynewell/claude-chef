## Ingredients

- 4  serrano chile peppers, or to taste
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 cup packed fresh cilantro leaves
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1  egg
- 1 cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Place the chile peppers, garlic, cilantro, salt, and egg into the bowl of a blender.
2. Puree until smooth, then, with motor running, slowly pour in the olive oil.
3. Continue blending for a minute or two until the sauce acquires a mayonnaise-like texture.
