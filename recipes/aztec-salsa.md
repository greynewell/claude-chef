## Ingredients

- 2  avocados - peeled, pitted, and chopped
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 (11 ounce) can Mexican-style corn
- 3  tomatoes, seeded and chopped
- 3  green onions, sliced
- 1 small red onion, chopped
- ¼ cup olive oil, or as needed
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian-style salad dressing mix

## Instructions

1. Lightly combine the avocados with red wine vinegar in a salad bowl.
2. Stir in the black beans, corn, tomatoes, green onions, and red onion.
3. Whisk together the olive oil and Italian dressing mix in a bowl, pour over the salad, and toss lightly.
