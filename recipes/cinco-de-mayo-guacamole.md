## Ingredients

- 6  ripe avocados, halved and pitted
- ¼  red onion, finely diced
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- 5  chopped leaves cilantro, or to taste
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon salt, or to taste
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ cup reduced-fat sour cream, or as needed
- ½  tomato, chopped

## Instructions

1. Scoop 5 avocados into a large bowl.
2. Add red onion, lime juice, cilantro, garlic, salt, pepper, and 2 dollops of sour cream.
3. Mash with a fork until desired texture is reached.
4. Cut remaining avocado into chunks and stir into guacamole with tomato.
5. Cover and refrigerate for 1 hour before serving.
