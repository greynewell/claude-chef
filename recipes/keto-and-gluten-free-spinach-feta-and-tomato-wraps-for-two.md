## Ingredients

- 3  eggs
- ½ cup almond milk
- ¼ cup coconut flour
- 1 pinch sea salt
- cooking spray
- 2 cups baby spinach
- ½ cup feta cheese, crumbled
- 3  chopped sun-dried tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon olive oil

## Instructions

1. Whisk eggs and almond milk together in a bowl until well combined.
2. Combine coconut flour and salt in a separate bowl.
3. Stir in egg mixture; whisk until a smooth and soft batter forms, about 2 minutes.
4. Let stand for 5 minutes.
5. Heat an 8-inch skillet over medium-high heat; grease with cooking spray.
6. Pour 1/2 of the batter in and immediately rotate the skillet to spread batter out in a thin layer.
7. Cook until the top of the wrap is no longer wet and the bottom has turned light brown, about 3 minutes.
8. Run a spatula around the edge of the skillet to loosen wrap; flip wrap and cook until the other side has turned light brown, about 1 minute more.
9. Repeat with remaining batter.
10. Place spinach, feta cheese, and sun-dried tomatoes in the center of the wraps; drizzle with olive oil.
11. Roll from one end to the other to make the wraps.
