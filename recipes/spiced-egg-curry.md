## Ingredients

- 3 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1 (1/2 inch) piece ginger, grated
- 4  curry leaves, or to taste
- 2  onions, finely sliced
- 1 teaspoon garlic paste
- 2 tablespoons chili powder
- 1 tablespoon coriander powder
- ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
- salt to taste
- 2  ripe tomatoes, chopped
- 1 teaspoon tomato paste
- 4  hard-boiled eggs, chopped

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat.
2. Add ginger and curry leaves; cook and stir until flavor is released, 3 to 5 minutes.
3. Place onions into the pan containing the ginger mixture; cook until the onions are translucent, 3 to 4 minutes.
4. Add garlic paste; cook until garlic releases aroma, 3 to 4 minutes.
5. Pour chili powder, coriander powder, turmeric powder, and salt into the onion mixture.
6. Cook and stir, being careful not to burn the spices, until spices are incorporated, about 2 minutes.
7. Stir tomatoes and tomato paste into the pan; cook and stir until the tomatoes are soft, about 5 minutes.
8. Add boiled eggs to the pan; stir until eggs are coated, about 1 minute.
