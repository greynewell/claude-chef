## Ingredients

- 3 cups chopped garlic scapes
- 1 cup freshly grated Romano cheese
- 1 cup flat-leaf parsley
- ½ cup chopped walnuts
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 1 (16 ounce) box fettuccine pasta
- 1 pound cooked shrimp
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  lemon, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Place garlic scapes, Romano cheese, parsley, and walnuts in the bowl of a food processor; pulse to combine.
2. Scrape down sides.
3. Turn on food processor; pour in olive oil and lemon juice while processing pesto until smooth.
4. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
5. Stir in pasta, bring back to a boil, and cook over medium heat until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes.
6. Drain and transfer to a large bowl.
7. Stir pesto and shrimp into the pasta.
8. Season with salt and black pepper.
9. Squeeze a lemon wedge over pasta and serve remaining wedges alongside.
