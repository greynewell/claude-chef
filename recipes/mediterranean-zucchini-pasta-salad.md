## Ingredients

- 1 (10 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
- ½ cup pitted Kalamata olives, halved
- 3  zucchini, spiralized
- 2  lemons, juiced
- ¼ cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- 1 tablespoon white vinegar
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1  lemon, zested
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ cup crumbled goat milk feta cheese, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine artichoke hearts, tomatoes, olives, and zucchini in a large bowl.
2. Pour lemon juice, olive oil, parsley, vinegar, garlic, oregano, kosher salt, lemon zest, and pepper into a small bowl; stir to combine.
3. Pour over the zucchini mixture.
4. Top zucchini pasta with goat milk feta cheese and serve on a large platter.
