## Ingredients

- 1 pound white American cheese, cubed
- ½ cup milk, or as needed
- 1 tablespoon butter or margarine
- 2 (4 ounce) cans chopped green chilies
- 2 teaspoons cumin
- 2 teaspoons garlic powder
- 2 teaspoons onion powder
- cayenne pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place cheese, milk, and butter in a medium saucepan over low heat. Stir until cheese is melted.
2. Add green chilies, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, and cayenne pepper; stir until smooth. Thin with more milk if sauce is too thick.
3. Cook until heated through, about 2 minutes. Serve warm.
