## Ingredients

- 8 ounces rotini pasta
- ½ cup olive oil
- ½ cup red wine vinegar
- 1 ½ teaspoons garlic powder
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil leaves
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried oregano
- 3 cups sliced mushrooms
- 15  halved cherry tomatoes
- ¾ cup crumbled feta cheese
- ½ cup chopped green onions
- 1 (4 ounce) can chopped black olives

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add rotini pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. Mix together cooked pasta, olive oil, vinegar, garlic powder, basil, oregano, mushrooms, tomatoes, Feta cheese, green onions and olives.
4. Cover and chill for at least 2 hours, serve cold.
