## Ingredients

- 2 heads fresh broccoli
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon salt
- 2 tablespoons light soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons distilled white vinegar
- 2 tablespoons sesame oil
- Â¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon white sugar

## Instructions

1. Separate broccoli into bite-sized florets.
2. Peel tough skin off stem and quarter it into 2 inch pieces cut slantwise.
3. Bring 2 1/2 quarts water, 2 tablespoons oil and 1 tablespoon salt to boil.
4. Add broccoli and boil quickly for 1 minute; plunge into cold water to set color; drain and place on platter or in a bowl.
5. In a small bowl combine soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, 1/4 teaspoon salt and sugar.
6. Pour mixture over broccoli, toss.
7. This salad can be served hot or cold, your choice!
