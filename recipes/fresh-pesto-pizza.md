## Ingredients

- 1 (12 inch) pre-baked whole-wheat pizza crust
- 1 cup prepared pesto
- 1 ½ cups chopped fresh spinach
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- ½ cup crumbled feta cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Remove crust from wrapper and spread pesto over crust; top with a layer of spinach leaves, chopped tomato, shredded mozzarella cheese and feta cheese.
3. Place pizza on a baking sheet.
4. Bake pizza in the preheated oven until the spinach leaves are wilted and the cheese is melted, bubbling, and beginning to brown, 10 to 12 minutes.
