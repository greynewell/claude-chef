## Ingredients

- 4  fresh tomatoes
- 10  fresh jalapeno peppers, halved lengthwise and seeded
- 1 (12 fluid ounce) bottle Mexican beer
- ½ cup water, or as needed
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 slice onion
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Place tomatoes and jalapeños in a saucepan; pour in beer and enough water to cover tomatoes and jalapeños. Simmer over medium heat until soft; remove from heat and let cool slightly.
2. Transfer tomatoes and jalapeños to a blender; add garlic and onion. Pulse until well blended, adding some of the cooking liquid to help with blending if necessary. Taste and season with salt.
