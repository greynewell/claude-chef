## Ingredients

- ½  red onion, sliced
- 2  scallions, chopped
- 1 ½  tomatoes, chopped
- 2  cucumbers, peeled and chopped
- 2  fresh jalapeno peppers, chopped
- Chopped fresh cilantro
- 4 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained, flaked
- 6 ounces Clamato® Original
- Fresh lime juice
- Maggi® salsa liquid seasoning
- Worcestershire sauce to taste
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 10  tostada shells

## Instructions

1. Place the red onion into a mixing bowl.
2. Add the chopped scallions, tomatoes, cucumbers, jalapenos and cilantro to the same bowl.
3. In a separate serving bowl, combine the tuna, Clamato®, lime, salsa Maggi®, Worcestershire sauce, salt and pepper.
4. Mix in the chopped veggies and enjoy over some freshly-baked tostadas.
