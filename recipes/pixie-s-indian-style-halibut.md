## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon kosher salt
- 2 (4 ounce) fillets halibut
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground turmeric
- 2 tablespoons ground coriander
- 1 ½ teaspoons paprika
- ½ teaspoon kosher salt
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 sprig cilantro

## Instructions

1. Combine the water and 1 tablespoon salt in a shallow container.
2. Soak the fish in the salt water for 10 minutes.
3. Mix the turmeric, coriander, paprika, and 1/2 teaspoon salt in a small bowl.
4. Remove fish from the salt water and pat dry with paper towels.
5. Coat fish on both sides with spice mixture.
6. Heat the olive oil in a small skillet over medium heat.
7. Cook fish in hot oil until it flakes easily with a fork, about 3 minutes per side.
8. Serve garnished with cilantro.
