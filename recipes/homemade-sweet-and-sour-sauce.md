## Ingredients

- 2 cups packed dark brown sugar
- ¼ cup cornstarch
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 ⅓ cups red wine vinegar
- 1 cup pineapple juice
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 1 (8 ounce) can crushed pineapple with juice

## Instructions

1. Mix dark brown sugar, cornstarch, garlic powder, and ground ginger together in a saucepan until well-combined. Add red wine vinegar, pineapple juice, and soy sauce.
2. Cook and stir over medium heat until bubbling, about 8 minutes. Add crushed pineapple; cook until thickened, 2 to 3 minutes more.
