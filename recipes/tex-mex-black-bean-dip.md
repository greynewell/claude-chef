## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ cup fresh corn kernels
- ¾ cup chopped tomatoes
- ½ cup mild picante sauce
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- ½ cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese
- ¼ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice

## Instructions

1. Place black beans in a medium size mixing bowl, partially mash beans -- beans should remain a little chunky.
2. In a medium size frying pan, heat oil over a medium heat.
3. Stir in onion and garlic and saute for 4 minutes.
4. Mix beans, corn, tomato, picante sauce, cumin, and chili powder into the frying pan; cook for 5 minutes or until thickened.
5. Remove the pan from the heat, mix in cheese, cilantro and lime juice; stir until cheese is melted.
