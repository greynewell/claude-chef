## Ingredients

- ¾ cup white sugar
- ½ cup butter, melted
- 2  eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ cup banana-flavored Greek yogurt
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 medium bananas, mashed
- 1 cup white chocolate chips
- ½ cup chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
2. Stir sugar and melted butter together in a large bowl. Add eggs and vanilla extract; mix well. Mix in banana yogurt.
3. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl. Stir into the butter mixture until smooth. Fold in bananas, white chocolate chips, and walnuts. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the loaf comes out clean, about 1 hour. Cool loaf in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
