## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons reduced-fat mayonnaise
- 1 ½ teaspoons wasabi paste
- 1 teaspoon Chinese five-spice powder
- 1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
- 4 (6 ounce) yellowfin tuna fillets
- 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
- 1 tablespoon low-sodium soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds

## Instructions

1. Mix together the mayonnaise, wasabi paste, five-spice powder, and 1 tablespoon soy sauce in a small bowl.
2. Lay the tuna fillets into a glass baking dish.
3. Pour the rice vinegar and 1 tablespoon soy sauce over the tuna.
4. Spread the mayonnaise mixture evenly over both sides of each piece of fish.
5. Cover the dish and refrigerate 15 to 30 minutes.
6. Prepare a skillet with cooking spray and place over medium-high heat.
7. Sprinkle the sesame seeds evenly over both sides of the fillets.
8. Lay the tuna gently into the skillet.
9. Grill to desired level of doneness, about 2 minutes per side for medium-rare.
10. Serve immediately.
