## Ingredients

- 2 pounds extra lean ground beef
- 1  onion, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 medium (blank)s jalapeno chile peppers, seeded and minced
- 1  red bell pepper, diced
- 1 (12 ounce) jar Crosse & Blackwell® Seafood Cocktail Sauce
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- 2 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon chipotle seasoning
- 1 pinch Salt and pepper to taste
- 8 each whole wheat buns

## Instructions

1. Brown meat and onions in large skillet over medium-high heat 5 minutes or until browned.
2. Drain drippings from pan and discard.
3. Add garlic, jalapeno peppers and red pepper to beef in pan and cook an additional 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
4. Stir in cocktail sauce, molasses, sugar, seasoning, salt and pepper.
5. Reduce heat to low, and simmer 5 minutes.
6. Serve on buns.
