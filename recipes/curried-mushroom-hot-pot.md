## Ingredients

- ½  onion, diced
- 2 ½ tablespoons curry powder
- 1 (32 fluid ounce) container chicken broth
- ½  lemon, sliced
- 1  1/4-inch-thick slices fresh ginger, peeled
- 1 ½ teaspoons white sugar
- salt to taste
- 1 pound assorted mushrooms
- 1 (13.5 ounce) can coconut milk
- 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
- salt to taste
- 8  kaffir lime leaves

## Instructions

1. Prepare a saucepan with cooking spray and place it over high heat.
2. Place the onion in the saucepan; cook and stir for 2 minutes.
3. Sprinkle the curry powder over the onion; stir to coat evenly.
4. Add the chicken broth, lemon, ginger, sugar, and salt to the onions.
5. Reduce heat to medium; cook and stir for 2 to 3 minutes.
6. Stir in the mushrooms and cook another 3 minutes.
7. Mix in the coconut milk and lemon juice; stir and remove from heat.
8. Drop the lemon leaves into the soup; allow to sit for 5 minutes.
9. Remove leaves before serving.
