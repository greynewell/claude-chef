## Ingredients

- ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon ground coriander
- ¼ teaspoon chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 2 cups cubed seeded watermelon
- ½  lime, juiced

## Instructions

1. Mix cumin, coriander, chili powder, salt, and cayenne pepper together in a bowl.
2. Place watermelon into serving bowls; sprinkle with spice mixture. Squeeze lime juice over spiced watermelon.
