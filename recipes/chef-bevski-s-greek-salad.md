## Ingredients

- ¾ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar
- ¼ cup chopped fresh dill
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1  cucumber, peeled and diced
- 1 cup chopped broccoli
- 1 cup chopped cauliflower
- 2  plum tomatoes, diced
- ¼ head red cabbage, shredded
- ¼ large red onion, diced
- ½  red bell pepper, chopped
- ½  green bell pepper, chopped
- 1 (5 ounce) jar pimento-stuffed green olives, sliced
- 1 (4 ounce) package feta cheese, crumbled

## Instructions

1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, dill, salt, and black pepper together in a bowl.
2. Mix cucumber, broccoli, cauliflower, plum tomatoes, red cabbage, red onion, red bell pepper, green bell pepper, olives, and feta cheese together in a large bowl.
3. Drizzle dressing over vegetable mixture; toss to coat.
4. Refrigerate at least 1 hour to allow flavors to marinate.
