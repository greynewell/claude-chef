## Ingredients

- 1 cup plain yogurt
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- ½ cup feta cheese, crumbled
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Mix yogurt, garlic, oregano, and black pepper together in a bowl.
2. Add chicken to yogurt marinade, coating all sides.
3. Cover and refrigerate for 4 hours.
4. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
5. Remove chicken from yogurt marinade; place on preheated grill.
6. Cook for 6 minutes; flip and sprinkle with feta cheese.
7. Cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, 4 to 6 more minutes.
8. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
9. Garnish with fresh parsley.
