## Ingredients

- 2 cups chopped kimchi
- ½  yellow onion, sliced
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 cups water
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, or as needed
- ¼ cup chopped green onions
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- ½ teaspoon sesame seeds
- ½ teaspoon Korean red pepper powder
- ½ teaspoon sesame oil

## Instructions

1. Mix kimchi and onion together in a large bowl; add flour and mix well for 1 minute.
2. Pour water over kimchi mixture and stir until batter is evenly combined.
3. Heat a skillet over medium heat; add about 1 teaspoon vegetable oil.
4. Scoop about 1/4 cup batter into the hot skillet, creating a pancake that is 1/4-inch thick; flatten and smooth the edges using a spatula.
5. Cook pancake for 3 minutes; flip, pressing lightly, and cook for 2 minutes more.
6. Repeat with remaining vegetable oil and batter.
7. Whisk green onion, soy sauce, sesame seeds, red pepper powder, and sesame oil together in a bowl; serve alongside pancake.
