## Ingredients

- vegetable oil for frying
- 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
- ½ pound cooked bacon, crumbled
- 8  jalapeno peppers, diced, or more to taste
- ¼ cup shredded processed cheese (such as Velveeta®)
- 4  green onions, chopped
- 3 tablespoons hot sauce (such as Tabasco® Chipotle Pepper Sauce)  (Optional)
- 1  egg
- 2 tablespoons water
- 1 (16 ounce) package wonton wrappers

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine cream cheese, bacon, jalapenos, shredded cheese, green onions, and hot sauce in a bowl.
3. Mix well.
4. Whisk egg and water together in a small bowl.
5. Place 1 tablespoon cream cheese mixture on a wonton wrapper.
6. Brush the edges of the wonton wrappers with the egg mixture using a small pastry brush.
7. Fold wonton into a triangle; pinch shut.
8. Repeat with the rest of the wonton wrappers.
9. Place wontons carefully in the oil, working in batches.
10. Fry until golden brown and crispy, about 6 minutes.
