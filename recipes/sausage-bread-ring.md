## Ingredients

- 1 pound bulk breakfast sausage
- 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1  egg
- 1 (16 ounce) package refrigerated pizza dough (such as Pillsbury®)
- 1  egg yolk, beaten

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir sausage, breaking meat into chunks, until crumbly and no longer pink, about 10 minutes.
3. Transfer sausage to a paper towel-lined plate to drain.
4. Mix cooked sausage, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, and egg together in a bowl.
5. Rough dough into a 10x14-inch rectangle on a baking sheet.
6. Spread sausage-cheese mixture onto dough, leaving a 1-inch border.
7. Roll dough around filling, starting from the long edge, creating a long log.
8. Form the log into a circle and pinch edges together to seal.
9. Brush top of roll with egg yolk.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until ring is dark golden brown, 1 hour.
11. Cool for 15 minutes before slicing into pieces to serve.
