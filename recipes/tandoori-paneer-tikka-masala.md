## Ingredients

- 5 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 (2 inch) piece fresh ginger root, chopped
- ½ cup plain yogurt
- 2 tablespoons mustard oil
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 ½ teaspoons dried fenugreek leaves
- 1 teaspoon garam masala
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- 16 ounces paneer, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 1  green bell pepper, cut in 1-inch pieces
- 1  red bell pepper, cut in 1-inch pieces
- ½ large onion, cut into 1-inch pieces
- ½ cup cashews
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
- ½  onion, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon dried fenugreek leaves
- ½ teaspoon garam masala
- 2 large tomatoes, finely chopped
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 tablespoon ground coriander
- 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper, or to taste
- ½ teaspoon ground turmeric
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Soak 8 to 10 wooden skewers in water while you prepare the kebabs.
2. Process garlic and ginger in a food processor until a smooth paste forms.
3. Combine yogurt, mustard oil, lime juice of 1/2 lime, 1 1/2 teaspoons fenugreek leaves, 1 teaspoon garam masala, 1/2 teaspoon cayenne, and 1/2 of the ginger-garlic paste in a bowl.
4. Whisk until smooth.
5. Add paneer, green bell pepper, red bell pepper, and onion.
6. Stir until coated.
7. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
8. Thread the coated paneer, peppers, and onion onto the soaked skewers.
9. Place kebabs in a baking dish.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until kebabs are golden brown, 25 to 30 minutes.
11. Soak cashews in a bowl of hot water for 10 minutes; blend as finely or coarsely as desired.
12. Heat olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-high heat.
13. Add cumin seeds; cook until sputtering, about 1 minute.
14. Add remaining onion; cook and stir until translucent, about 5 minutes.
15. Add the remaining ginger-garlic paste, 1 tablespoon fenugreek leaves, and 1/2 teaspoon garam masala.
16. Cook and stir until flavors blend, about 2 minutes.
17. Stir in tomatoes; cover and cook until tomatoes break down, about 5 minutes.
18. Stir the ground cashews into the saucepan with the tomato mixture.
19. Add juice of 1/2 lime, coriander, 1 teaspoon cayenne, turmeric, and salt.
20. Let simmer until sauce is thickened, adding some water if sauce gets too dry and sticks to the pan, about 5 minutes more.
21. Add the baked kebabs to the sauce.
22. Stir to coat evenly.
