## Ingredients

- 8 slices thinly sliced pancetta
- 2  ciabatta rolls
- 8 ounces apricot preserves
- 8 slices Galban®i Fresh Mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. In a frying pan cook pancetta for about one minute, turning once.
2. Cut ciabatta rolls open and spread a thick layer of apricot preserves.
3. Add 4 slices of fresh mozzarella and pancetta to each roll and close sandwich.
4. In a panini grill pan or cast iron skillet, cook sandwiches on both sides to melt cheese and crisp bread.
