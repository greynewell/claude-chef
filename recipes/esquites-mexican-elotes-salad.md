## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 4 ears corn, kernels cut from cob
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- 1 small shallot, minced
- 1 tablespoon mayonnaise, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon chile-lime seasoning (such as Tajin®), or more to taste
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced
- ½ cup fresh cilantro, chopped
- 2 ounces Cotija cheese, crumbled
- 1  lime, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Heat oil in a large cast iron skillet over high heat until shimmering.
2. Add corn kernels, in batches if necessary.
3. Stir briefly; sprinkle lightly with salt and pepper.
4. Cook, without stirring, until corn is slightly charred, about 2 minutes.
5. Stir once and cook for 1 minute more.
6. Remove from heat and set aside.
7. Mix shallot, mayonnaise, and chile-lime seasoning together in a large bowl.
8. Stir in the corn and jalapeno until well coated in dressing.
9. Stir in 1/2 of the cilantro and Cotija cheese.
10. Taste and season as desired.
11. Top with the remaining cilantro.
12. Serve each portion with a lime wedge.
