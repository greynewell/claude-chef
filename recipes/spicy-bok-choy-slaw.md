## Ingredients

- 1 head bok choy, finely shredded
- 1  cucumber, seeded and finely shredded
- 3  carrots, peeled and finely shredded
- 5  hot cherry peppers, seeded and finely shredded
- 5  jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
- ¾ cup apple cider vinegar
- ¼ cup coarse-grain brown mustard
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 2 tablespoons agave syrup
- ⅛ teaspoon roasted ground ginger
- freshly cracked black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place the shredded bok choy, cucumber, carrots, and cherry peppers into a large salad bowl.
2. Place the jalapeno peppers into the work bowl of a food processor, then pour in the apple cider vinegar, brown mustard, soy sauce, and agave syrup.
3. Pulse several times, then process for a few seconds to combine.
4. Pour the dressing over the vegetables and toss.
5. Refrigerate from 1 hour to overnight.
6. Before serving, sprinkle with roasted ginger and black pepper; toss again to serve.
