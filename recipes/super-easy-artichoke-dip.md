## Ingredients

- 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 ¼ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- ⅓ cup chopped sun-dried tomatoes  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Stir together the artichoke hearts, mayonnaise, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, garlic, and sun-dried tomatoes.
3. Place the dip in a small baking dish.
4. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly and heated through, about 15 minutes.
5. Serve hot.
