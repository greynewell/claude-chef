## Ingredients

- 3 pounds pork shoulder
- 2 (1 ounce) packages taco seasoning mix
- chili powder to taste
- crushed red pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Place pork shoulder in a slow cooker with  .
2. If desired, add chili powder and/or red pepper flakes.
3. Add water until meat is covered.
4. Place lid on pot and cook on low for 8 hours.
5. Remove pork shoulder from pot and shred.
