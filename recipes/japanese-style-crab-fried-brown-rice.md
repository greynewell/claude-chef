## Ingredients

- 2 ½  water
- 2 cups uncooked brown rice
- 2  green onions, diced, white parts only
- 1 clove garlic, diced
- 3  mushrooms, diced
- 1 large carrot, shredded
- ½ pound cooked Dungeness crab
- 1 ½ tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil

## Instructions

1. Bring water and brown rice to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed, 45 to 50 minutes.
3. While rice is cooking, heat a skillet over medium heat.
4. Add green onions and garlic and cook for 1 minute.
5. Add mushrooms and cook until softened, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Add carrots and cook until softened, about 2 minutes more.
7. Add crab and cook until heated through, about 3 minutes more.
8. Mix soy sauce, maple syrup, and sesame oil to the vegetable mixture in the skillet.
9. Add cooked rice and stir to combine.
