## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
- 1 (16 ounce) package rotini pasta
- 2 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
4. In a skillet, cook beef until brown over medium heat.
5. Drain excess fat from meat.
6. Add spaghetti sauce and rotini pasta to browned beef.
7. In a three quart casserole dish layer meat mixture, followed with the mozzarella cheese, repeat.
8. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes.
