## Ingredients

- 2 pounds top sirloin steak, cut into thin strips
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 18 (6 inch) corn tortillas
- 1  onion, diced
- 4  fresh jalapeno peppers, seeded and chopped
- 1 bunch fresh cilantro, chopped
- 4  limes, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat.
2. Saute steak in hot skillet until browned on the outside and cooked through, about 5 minutes.
3. Season with salt and pepper.
4. Remove to a plate and keep warm.
5. Heat oil in the same skillet.
6. Place a tortilla in the hot oil and cook, turning once, until tortilla is lightly browned and flexible.
7. Repeat with remaining tortillas.
8. Place tortillas on a plate and top each with steak, onion, jalapeno, and cilantro.
9. Squeeze lime juice over the top.
