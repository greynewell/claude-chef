## Ingredients

- 8  frozen breaded fish fillets
- 1 cup light sour cream
- 2 tablespoons finely chopped fresh cilantro
- 1  lime, juiced
- 8  flour tortillas
- 1 cup salsa
- 8 large Romaine lettuce leaves, shredded

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Place fish fillets in a single layer on a baking sheet.
3. Bake in the preheated oven, flipping halfway, until fish fillets are crispy and browned, 22 to 24 minutes.
4. Meanwhile, mix sour cream, cilantro, and lime juice together in a bowl.
5. Cut baked fillets in 1/2 lengthwise.
6. Place halves end-to-end on tortillas, 1 whole fillet per tortilla.
7. Spoon 1 rounded tablespoon each of sour cream mixture and salsa onto taco and top evenly with lettuce.
