## Ingredients

- 8 ounces capellini pasta
- ½ pound shiitake mushrooms
- 1  red bell pepper, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup rice vinegar
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 teaspoon grated fresh ginger
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta in a large pot of boiling water.
2. Meanwhile, clean, stem, and slice mushrooms.
3. Add mushrooms and red bell pepper during last 2 minutes of cooking.
4. Drain.
5. In a small bowl, mix together vinegar, soy sauce, oil, and ginger.
6. Transfer pasta, mushrooms, and pepper to a serving bowl; toss with ginger dressing.
7. Sprinkle with parsley before serving.
