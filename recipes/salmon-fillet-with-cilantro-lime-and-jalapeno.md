## Ingredients

- 1 cup fresh cilantro leaves
- 5 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, divided
- lime, juiced
- 1 small garlic clove
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
- 2 pounds salmon fillets, or more to taste
- 1 large vine tomato, seeded and chopped
- ½  jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Combine cilantro, 2 tablespoons olive oil, lime juice, and garlic in a blender.
3. Season with salt and black pepper.Blend until creamy smooth.
4. Brush a baking pan with remaining oil and place salmon in the pan.
5. Spread cilantro mixture on the salmon; arrange tomato and jalapeno on top.
6. Season with salt and black pepper.
7. Bake in the preheated oven, uncovered, until fish flakes easily with a fork, 15 to 20 minutes.
