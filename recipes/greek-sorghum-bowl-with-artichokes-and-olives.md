## Ingredients

- 1 cup whole grain sorghum, uncooked
- 4 cups water
- 1 cup marinated artichoke hearts, quartered
- 1 cup pitted Kalamata olives, halved
- 1 cup cucumbers, halved and sliced
- 1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
- ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
- ½ cup red onion, thinly sliced
- ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. In a small saucepan, boil 4 cups of water and add 1 cup of whole grain sorghum to make 3 cups of cooked sorghum.
2. Cover with a tight-fitting lid, reduce heat to medium and simmer for 45 minutes or until tender.
3. Fluff with a fork.
4. Combine all salad ingredients, including cooked sorghum, in a large bowl.
5. Whisk together all dressing ingredients.
6. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss to coat.
7. Serve with lemon wedges.
