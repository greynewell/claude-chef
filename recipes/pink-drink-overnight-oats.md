## Ingredients

- ¼ cup oats, or more to taste
- 7 large strawberries, diced
- 3 tablespoons low-fat milk
- 2 tablespoons plain Greek yogurt, or more to taste
- 1 tablespoon vanilla protein powder
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- ½ teaspoon honey

## Instructions

1. Combine oats, strawberries, milk, Greek yogurt, protein powder, vanilla extract, and honey in a mason jar.
2. Stir to combine.
3. Cover and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight.
