## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
- 1 pound lean ground beef
- 1 ½ (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) container ricotta cheese
- 2  eggs
- 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.
2. Once the water is boiling, stir in the lasagna noodles, and return to a boil.
3. Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is slightly tender but not cooked through, about 7 minutes.
4. Drain well in a colander set in the sink.
5. Cook and stir ground beef in a large skillet over medium-high heat until beef is browned; drain, then stir in sauce.
6. Set aside.
7. Combine 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, ricotta cheese, and eggs in a separate bowl.
8. Pour about 1/2 cup of the sauce mixture in the bottom of a slow cooker and cover with a layer of noodles.
9. Sprinkle about 1/4 of the cheese mixture over the noodles, then ladle about 1/4 of the remaining sauce over the cheese.
10. Repeat layering, ending with a layer of sauce and topping with the remaining 2 cups of mozzarella cheese.
11. Cook on High setting for 2 to 3 hours, or on Low setting for 8 to 9 hours.
