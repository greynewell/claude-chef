## Ingredients

- 2  kiwis, peeled and diced
- 2  Golden Delicious apples - peeled, cored and diced
- 8 ounces raspberries
- 1 pound strawberries, divided
- 3 tablespoons fruit preserves, any flavor
- 10 (10 inch) whole wheat flour tortillas
- butter flavored cooking spray
- ⅓ cup cinnamon sugar

## Instructions

1. Thoroughly mix kiwis, Golden Delicious apples, raspberries, strawberries, and fruit preserves in a large bowl.
2. Cover and chill in the refrigerator at least 15 minutes.
3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
4. Coat one side of each flour tortilla with butter-flavored cooking spray.
5. Cut into wedges and arrange in a single layer on a large baking sheet.
6. Sprinkle wedges with cinnamon sugar.
7. Spray again with cooking spray.
8. Bake in the preheated oven until light brown and crispy, about 8 to 10 minutes.
9. Repeat with any remaining tortilla wedges.
10. Allow to cool approximately 15 minutes.
11. Serve with chilled fruit mixture.
