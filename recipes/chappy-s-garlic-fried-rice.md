## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1 cup uncooked white rice
- 1 teaspoon butter
- 1 small onion, minced
- 1 large clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon diced chile pepper
- 1  egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon sesame oil  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Bring water and rice to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until the rice is tender and liquid has been absorbed, 20 to 25 minutes.
3. Cool.
4. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
5. Saute onion, garlic, and chile pepper in hot butter until fragrant and lightly browned, 5 to 7 minutes.
6. Stir cooked rice, egg, soy sauce, and sesame oil into onion mixture.
7. Cook and stir until egg is cooked through and rice is coated and heated through, 3 to 5 minutes.
