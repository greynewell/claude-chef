## Ingredients

- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 (16 ounce) jar salsa
- 1 (10.75 ounce) can  condensed cream of mushroom soup
- 2 pounds processed cheese food, cubed

## Instructions

1. Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet.
2. Cook over medium high heat until evenly brown.
3. Drain beef and place in a slow cooker or crock pot with salsa, condensed cream of mushroom soup and processed cheese food.
4. Cook on high until cheese is melted.
5. Lower heat and simmer until serving.
