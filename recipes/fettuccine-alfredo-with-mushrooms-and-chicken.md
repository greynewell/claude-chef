## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 bunch green onions, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced, or more to taste
- 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 2 cups heavy whipping cream
- 2 cups milk
- 2 cups freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) package sliced fresh mushrooms
- 1 (16 ounce) box dry fettuccine pasta
- 2  grilled chicken breasts, cubed, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat butter in a large pot over medium-high heat and add green onions and garlic.
2. Cook until butter is simmering and onions and garlic are softened, 3 to 4 minutes.
3. Add flour and stir for 1 minute.
4. Stir in heavy cream, milk, Parmesan cheese, and mushrooms.
5. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-low and let simmer until sauce looks thick and creamy, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes.
6. Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil.
7. Cook fettuccine at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
8. Drain.
9. Stir chicken into sauce mixture and heat through, 3 to 5 minutes, to avoid overcooking.
10. Serve chicken   over cooked pasta.
