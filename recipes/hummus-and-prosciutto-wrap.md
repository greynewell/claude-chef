## Ingredients

- 12 thin slices  prosciutto
- 4  10-inch low carb whole wheat tortillas
- ½ cup hummus spread, divided
- 4 slices mozzarella cheese
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced - divided
- 4 small tomatoes, sliced
- 1 cup torn lettuce leaves, divided

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Arrange the prosciutto slices on the baking sheet so they don't touch, and bake them in the preheated oven until crisp, 6 to 8 minutes.
4. Remove from the oven, and let cool.
5. Stack the tortillas on a plate and microwave them on High until warmed and pliable, 20 to 30 seconds.
6. Spread each tortilla with 2 tablespoons of hummus spread, and top each with 3 slices of crisped prosciutto per wrap.
7. Place a slice of mozzarella cheese on top of the prosciutto, and spread 1/4 of the avocado slices per wrap on the cheese.
8. Top each wrap with 1 sliced tomato and about 1/4 cup of torn lettuce leaves.
9. Fold the bottom of each tortilla up about 2 inches to enclose the filling, and roll the wrap tightly into a compact cylinder.
