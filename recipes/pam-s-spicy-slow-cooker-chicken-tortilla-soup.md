## Ingredients

- PAM® Original No-Stick Cooking Spray
- 1 ½ pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
- 2 cups frozen Southwest mixed vegetables (corn, black beans, red peppers)
- 1 (10 ounce) can Ro*Tel® Original Diced Tomatoes & Green Chilies, undrained
- 1 tablespoon ground chipotle chili pepper
- 1 ½ teaspoons ground cumin
- 4 cups chicken broth
- ¼ cup fresh lime juice
- 1 tablespoon Tortilla strips
- 1 tablespoon Diced avocado
- 1 teaspoon Chopped cilantro

## Instructions

1. Spray inside of 4-quart slow cooker with cooking spray.
2. Place chicken,  , undrained tomatoes, chili pepper, cumin and broth in slow cooker.
3. Cover; cook on LOW 6 to 8 hours or until chicken is tender.
4. Remove chicken from slow cooker; pull into shreds with 2 forks.
5. Return chicken to slow cooker.
6. Stir in lime juice.
7. Serve and top with tortilla strips, avocado and cilantro, if desired.
