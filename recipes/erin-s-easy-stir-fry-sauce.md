## Ingredients

- 1 cup butter
- ¾ cup soy sauce
- ¾ cup white vinegar
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons minced garlic
- ½ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ½ teaspoon crushed red pepper

## Instructions

1. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Stir soy sauce, vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, black pepper, and red pepper together in a bowl. Pour into the pan with the butter; bring to a boil. Remove from heat.
