## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) box Barilla® Spaghetti
- 1 (24 ounce) jar Barilla® Traditional Sauce
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½  onion, chopped
- 2 (6 ounce) cans tuna, drained and flaked
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 2 tablespoons capers, drained and chopped
- ¼ cup sliced black olives
- ½ cup canned diced tomatoes, drained
- 1 teaspoon herbes de Provence
- 1 teaspoon anchovy paste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
3. Drain, reserving about 1/2 cup cooking water.
4. Heat olive oil in medium-sized pot over medium-high heat.
5. Cook and stir onion until it starts turning translucent, 3 to 5 minutes.
6. Add tuna, garlic, cayenne pepper, capers, and olive slices.
7. Cook 3 to 4 minutes longer.
8. Stir in tomatoes, herbes de Provence, and anchovy paste.
9. Simmer for 10 minutes.
10. Add Barilla® Traditional pasta sauce.
11. Bring to a boil; reduce heat to medium-low and simmer 20 minutes.
12. If sauce seems too thick, add a few tablespoons of reserved pasta cooking water.
13. Serve over pasta.
