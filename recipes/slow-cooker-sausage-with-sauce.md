## Ingredients

- 8 (4 ounce) links fresh Italian sausage
- 1 (26 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
- 1  green bell pepper, seeded and sliced into strips
- 1  onion, sliced
- 6  hoagie rolls, split lengthwise  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place Italian sausage links, spaghetti sauce, green pepper, and onion into a slow cooker; mix until well combined.
2. Cover and cook on Low for 6 hours. Serve on hoagie rolls.
