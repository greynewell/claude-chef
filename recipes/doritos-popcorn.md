## Ingredients

- ¼ cup nutritional yeast
- 2 teaspoons salt (such as Real Salt®)
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon onion powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon chili powder
- ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper, or more to taste  (Optional)
- 8 cups hot popcorn, or as desired

## Instructions

1. Combine nutritional yeast, salt, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, chili powder, and cayenne pepper in a small bowl.
2. Place popcorn in a bowl; add nutritional yeast mixture and toss to coat.
