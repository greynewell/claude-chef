## Ingredients

- 1 pound ricotta cheese
- 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
- ¼ cup all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 teaspoons grated lemon zest
- 2  eggs, beaten
- 4  egg whites
- ¾ cup white sugar

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 300 degrees F (150 degrees C).
2. In a medium bowl, mix together the ricotta cheese, cream cheese, flour, salt, vanilla, lemon zest and eggs.
3. In a separate bowl, whisk together the egg whites and sugar.
4. Pour into the cheese mixture, and mix until well blended.
5. Roll into balls about the size of a golf ball, and place in a glass baking dish.
6. Bake for 45 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown on top.
