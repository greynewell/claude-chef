## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup loosely packed fresh basil leaves
- 3 tablespoons honey
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon chopped shallot
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce
- ½ teaspoon grated fresh ginger
- 3 pounds salmon fillets

## Instructions

1. Make a shallow pan from a double layer of aluminum foil.
2. Place foil pan in a large baking sheet.
3. Arrange salmon fillets in the prepared foil pan, skin-side down.
4. Stir basil, honey, lemon, oil, soy sauce, shallot, Worcestershire sauce, and ginger together in a bowl; pour over salmon.
5. Let salmon marinate for 30 minutes to 2 hours.
6. Preheat grill for medium-low heat.
7. Slide salmon in the foil pan off the baking sheet onto the preheated grill.
8. Cover grill.
9. Cook until salmon is easily flaked with a fork, about 20 minutes.
