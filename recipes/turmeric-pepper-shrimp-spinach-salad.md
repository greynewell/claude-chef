## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon ghee (clarified butter)
- 7  prawns, peeled and deveined
- ¼ teaspoon ground turmeric
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- 1 cup fresh baby spinach, or to taste
- ½  avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
- ½  apple - peeled, cored, and thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons crumbled feta cheese
- 1 tablespoon sliced almonds, or to taste
- 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 pinch Himalayan pink salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat a small skillet over medium heat; add ghee.
2. Place prawns in the melted ghee and season with turmeric and pepper; cook for 30 seconds.
3. Flip prawns and cook other side until pink and cooked through, 30 to 60 seconds.
4. Remove skillet from heat.
5. Place spinach in a bowl and top with avocado, apple slices, and shrimp.
6. Sprinkle feta cheese and almonds over salad.
7. Drizzle olive oil over salad and top with salt.
