## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
- ½ teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 3 cups vegetable or chicken broth or stock
- 1 (15.5 ounce) can cannellini (white kidney) beans, rinsed and drained
- 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 (5.3 ounce) container VOSKOS® Nonfat Honey Greek Yogurt
- Toasted pepitas (pumpkin seeds)*

## Instructions

1. Cook ginger and cumin in hot vegetable oil in a medium saucepan over medium heat for 1 to 2 minutes or until fragrant.
2. Add the vegetable broth, cannellini beans, pumpkin, and salt.
3. Heat through.
4. Remove from heat and puree until smooth with an immersion blender.
5. Set aside 1/4 cup of VOSKOS® Nonfat Honey Greek Yogurt to use as topper.
6. Whisk remaining yogurt into soup.
7. Ladle into bowls; top each with a tablespoon of the reserved yogurt, swirling with a knife.
8. Sprinkle with some toasted pepitas.
