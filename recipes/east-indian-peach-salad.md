## Ingredients

- ½ cup low-fat vanilla yogurt
- 1 (16 ounce) can sliced peaches in juice, drained and juice reserved
- ½ teaspoon curry powder, or to taste
- 2 cups 1/2-inch pieces cooked ham
- 1 cup halved seedless grapes
- 8  lettuce leaves
- 2 tablespoons sliced almonds

## Instructions

1. Mix yogurt, 1 tablespoon reserved peach juice, and curry powder together in a bowl.
2. Combine peaches, ham, and grapes in a separate bowl; add yogurt mixture and toss gently.
3. Line a serving platter with lettuce leaves.
4. Scoop peach mixture into each leaf; sprinkle with almonds.
