## Ingredients

- 1 large egg
- 1 teaspoon honey
- 1 cup panko bread crumbs
- ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 12  asparagus spears, trimmed
- ¼ cup stone-ground mustard
- ¼ cup Greek yogurt
- 1 pinch cayenne pepper  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
2. Combine egg and honey in a long, narrow dish and beat together. Combine panko and Parmesan cheese in a separate plate. Coat each asparagus stalk in egg mixture and roll in panko mix to coat.
3. Place 6 spears in the air fryer and cook to desired brownness, 4 to 6 minutes. Repeat with remaining spears.
4. Combine mustard, yogurt, and cayenne pepper in a small bowl. Serve dipping sauce alongside asparagus spears.
