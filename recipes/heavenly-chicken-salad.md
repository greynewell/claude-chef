## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained and halved
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 2 pounds cooked and chopped chicken breast
- ½ bunch celery, chopped
- 1 pound red seedless grapes, halved
- 1 cup toasted slivered almonds
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- ½ (8 ounce) jar Major Grey's chutney
- ½ teaspoon curry powder
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Toss the water chestnuts with the lemon juice in a large bowl.
2. Add the chicken, celery, grapes, and almonds to the water chestnuts.
3. Stir the mayonnaise, chutney, curry powder, and salt together in a separate bowl.
4. Fold the chicken mixture into the mayonnaise mixture; stir to coat evenly.
5. Chill in refrigerator at least 30 minutes before serving.
