## Ingredients

- 2  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 1 ¼ cups Italian salad dressing, divided
- 4 cups fresh spinach, torn
- ⅓ cup crumbled feta cheese
- 8  sun-dried tomatoes, packed without oil, chopped
- 1 (1 pound) loaf focaccia bread, cut into 1/2-inch thick slices
- ¼ cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Place the chicken and 1 cup salad dressing in a bowl.
2. Cover, and marinate at least 3 hours in the refrigerator.
3. Preheat the grill for high heat.
4. Lightly oil the grill grate.
5. Discard dressing used for marinating, and grill chicken 7 minutes per side, or until juices run clear.
6. Cool and shred.
7. In a large bowl, mix the cooked chicken, spinach, feta cheese, sun-dried tomatoes, and remaining dressing.
8. Brush the focaccia bread with olive oil, and cook 1 minute per side on the prepared grill, or until lightly toasted.
9. Place portions of the chicken mixture on the toasted focaccia to serve.
