## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 teaspoon toasted sesame oil
- 3  green onions, chopped, white and green parts separated
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 pound fresh medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 tablespoon gochujang (Korean hot pepper paste)
- 1 teaspoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon honey
- ½ cup julienned cucumber
- ¼ cup julienned carrot
- 1 tablespoon toasted sesame seeds
- 8 leaves butterhead lettuce
- 1  lemon, cut into wedges

## Instructions

1. Heat butter and sesame oil over medium heat in a large skillet.
2. Stir in white parts of green onion and garlic; cook until softened and fragrant, about 1 minute.
3. Add shrimp; cook, stirring, until shrimp are opaque, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Stir in gochujang, soy sauce, and honey; toss to coat.
5. Stir cucumber, carrot, green parts of green onion, and sesame seeds together in a small bowl.
6. Top lettuce leaves with shrimp mixture and cucumber mixture.
7. Serve with lemon wedges.
