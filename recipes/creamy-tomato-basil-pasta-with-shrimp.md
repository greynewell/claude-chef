## Ingredients

- 3 cups farfalle (bow tie) pasta, uncooked
- ¼ cup KRAFT Sun-Dried Tomato Vinaigrette Dressing, divided
- 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled and deveined
- 1 cup fat-free reduced-sodium chicken broth
- ½ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon black pepper
- 4 ounces PHILADELPHIA Neufchatel Cheese, 1/3 Less Fat than Cream Cheese
- 2 cups grape tomatoes
- ½ cup KRAFT Shredded Parmesan Cheese
- 8  fresh basil leaves, cut into strips

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta as directed on package.
2. Meanwhile, heat 2 Tbsp. dressing in large skillet on medium heat.
3. Add shrimp; cook and stir 2 to 3 min. or until shrimp turn pink.
4. Use slotted spoon to remove shrimp from skillet; cover to keep warm.
5. Discard any drippings in skillet.
6. Add remaining dressing, broth and seasonings to skillet; cook 2 min. or until heated through.
7. Add Neufchatel; cook and stir 2 to 3 min. or until melted.
8. Stir in tomatoes; cook 1 min.
9. Drain pasta.
10. Add to ingredients in skillet.
11. Stir in Parmesan and half the basil; top with shrimp and remaining basil.
