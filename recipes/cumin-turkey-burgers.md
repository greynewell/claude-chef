## Ingredients

- 1  egg
- 2  jalapeno peppers, seeded and minced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- ¼ cup low-sodium soy sauce
- ¼ cup Worcestershire sauce
- 2 teaspoons ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon mustard powder
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- ½ teaspoon chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
- ¼ cup dry bread crumbs
- 1 ½ pounds ground turkey
- 6  hamburger buns, split and toasted

## Instructions

1. Mix the egg, jalapeno peppers, and garlic in a large mixing bowl until the egg is well blended.
2. Add the soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, cumin, mustard, paprika, chili powder, salt, bread crumbs, and turkey; mix well and form into 6 patties.
3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
4. Cook the turkey burgers on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 4 minutes per side.
5. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
6. Serve on the toasted hamburger buns.
