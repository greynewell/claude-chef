## Ingredients

- 1 (15.25 ounce) can sweet corn, drained
- 1  jalapeno pepper, chopped
- ½ large red onion, chopped
- ½  lime, juiced
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine corn and jalapeno pepper in a skillet over high heat.
2. Cook and stir until edges start to blacken, about 10 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and let cool, 5 to 10 minutes.
4. Combine onion, lime juice, cilantro, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
5. Fold in cooled corn and jalapeno.
