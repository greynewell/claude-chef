## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 8 thin slices  Monterey Jack cheese
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and sliced
- ¼ cup olive oil
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 tablespoon spicy mesquite-flavored seasoning
- 1 pinch sea salt
- 1 cup chipotle barbeque sauce
- 4  Kaiser rolls, split

## Instructions

1. Slice a small pocket into each chicken breast with a sharp knife.
2. Layer cheese slices, avocado slices, and sliced jalapeno inside the chicken pockets.
3. Pinch chicken breasts closed and secure with toothpicks or skewers; place chicken into a casserole dish.
4. Whisk olive oil, lime juice, mesquite seasoning, and salt together in a bowl; drizzle half of the mixture over chicken breasts.
5. Turn chicken breasts over and drizzle with remaining olive oil mixture; cover and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
6. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat, and lightly oil the grate.
7. Place chicken breasts on preheated grill and cook for 7 minutes; turn chicken over and brush with BBQ sauce on the cooked side.
8. Continue grilling for about 5 minutes; turn chicken over and apply BBQ sauce to the second cooked side.
9. Cook chicken until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 2 minutes more.
10. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
11. Serve each chicken breast on a Kaiser roll.
