## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti
- 2  apples - peeled, cored, and chopped
- 1 large green bell pepper, chopped
- ½ pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
- ¾ cup mango chutney
- ¾ cup mayonnaise
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup dry-roasted peanuts

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes; drain.
3. Rinse spaghetti with cold water to cool; drain.
4. Stir spaghetti, apples, bell pepper, shrimp, mango chutney, mayonnaise, and raisins together in a large bowl.
5. Top with peanuts.
