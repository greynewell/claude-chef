## Ingredients

- 1 large sweet onion, sliced
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 1 (1 ounce) package Old El Paso® taco seasoning mix
- 1 (3 pound) boneless pork shoulder
- 1 (10 ounce) can Old El Paso® red enchilada sauce
- 1 (4.5 ounce) can Old El Paso® chopped green chiles, drained
- 16 (6 inch) Old El Paso® flour tortillas for soft tacos & fajitas
- ½ cup fresh cilantro leaves
- 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
- 1 cup sour cream  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Spray 5-quart slow cooker with cooking spray; add onion slices.
2. In 10-inch nonstick skillet, heat oil over high heat.
3. Sprinkle   mix over pork shoulder, pressing to coat.
4. Brown pork in oil on all sides; remove from skillet to slow cooker.
5. Pour enchilada sauce on top of pork.
6. Cover; cook on High heat setting 4 to 5 hours or until tender.
7. Remove pork from slow cooker; shred meat with fork.
8. Place shredded pork in large bowl.
9. Add chiles, 2 cups of the sauce from slow cooker and the onions; stir well.
10. Spoon pork mixture onto tortillas.
11. Add toppings.
12. Roll up tortillas.
