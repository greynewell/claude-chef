## Ingredients

- 4 large jalapenos, seeded and chopped
- ½ cup chopped cilantro
- 5 cloves garlic, chopped
- 1 (8 ounce) container light sour cream
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 (1 ounce) package dry ranch dressing mix
- 2 tablespoons milk, or more as needed

## Instructions

1. Combine jalapenos, cilantro, and garlic in the bowl of a food processor; pulse until chopped, scraping down the sides a few times.
2. Add sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, and ranch dressing mix and pulse until creamy.
3. Add 2 tablespoons of milk slowly and keep pulsing until you have reached the desired consistency.
4. Spoon dip into a bowl and refrigerate until flavors are combined, at least 30 minutes
