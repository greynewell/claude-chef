## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons ranch dressing
- 1 teaspoon seafood seasoning (such as
- ®)
- 1 pound rockfish
- 1 (5.8 ounce) package hard taco shells
- 2 cups coleslaw mix
- ⅓ cup sour cream
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
- 1 tablespoon lime juice, or more to taste
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
2. Mix ranch dressing and seafood seasoning together. Place rockfish on a rimmed baking sheet and brush fish with the ranch mixture.
3. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 25 minutes.
4. Meanwhile, mix coleslaw mix, sour cream, cilantro, lime juice, cumin, salt, and pepper together in a bowl. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
5. Reduce oven temperature to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Arrange taco shells on a baking sheet and bake until crisp, 6 to 7 minutes.
6. Separate cooked rockfish into 1- to 2-inch pieces. Place slaw inside taco shells and top with rockfish pieces. Serve immediately.
