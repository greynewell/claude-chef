## Ingredients

- 2 (12 ounce) packages spaghetti
- 1 small red onion, finely chopped
- 1  red bell pepper, seeded and finely chopped
- 1  cucumber, chopped
- 1  tomato, chopped
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle fat-free Italian salad dressing
- 1 (.7 ounce) package dry Italian salad dressing mix (such as Good Seasons®)
- 1 teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
3. Drain and rinse in cold water.
4. Place spaghetti into a large salad bowl and toss with red onion, red bell pepper, cucumber, tomato, Italian salad dressing, salad dressing mix, and salt.
5. Chill before serving; salad is better the longer it chills.
