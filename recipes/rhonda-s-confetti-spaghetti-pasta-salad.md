## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package spaghetti, broken into 1-inch pieces
- 2  tomatoes, diced
- 1  red onion, diced
- 1 large cucumber, diced
- 4 stalks celery, diced
- 2 cups imitation crabmeat, diced
- 1 (5 ounce) can tuna, drained
- 2 cups creamy salad dressing
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic, or to taste
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, 9 to 11 minutes.
3. Drain and run under cold water.
4. Mix tomatoes, onion, cucumber, celery, crabmeat, and tuna in a bowl; add spaghetti.
5. Stir in salad dressing and garlic; season with salt and black pepper.
6. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for flavors to blend, at least 30 minutes.
