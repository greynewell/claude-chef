## Ingredients

- 3 cloves garlic
- 2 teaspoons coarse salt
- 1 (19 ounce) can garbanzo beans, drained
- 3 tablespoons tahini (sesame-seed paste)
- 3 tablespoons lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 2 tablespoons water, or as needed
- ¼ cup olive oil, divided
- 6  pita bread rounds
- salt to taste
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh rosemary

## Instructions

1. Mash garlic with the salt in a small bowl.
2. Place into a blender or food processor along with the garbanzo beans, tahini, lemon juice, honey, and enough water to cover the beans.
3. Process until smooth.
4. Spoon into a serving dish, and drizzle 2 tablespoons of olive oil over the top.
5. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
6. Brush pita breads with remaining olive oil, and cut into wedges.
7. Season with salt and fresh rosemary.
8. Bake for 5 minutes in the preheated oven.
9. Cool, and serve with hummus.
