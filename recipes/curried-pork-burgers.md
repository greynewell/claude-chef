## Ingredients

- 1 pound lean ground pork
- ½ cup crumbled feta cheese
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro
- 2 tablespoons curry paste
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper

## Instructions

1. Preheat a grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
2. Mix pork, feta cheese, cilantro, curry paste, garlic, ginger, cumin, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.
3. Divide mixture into fourths and form a burger from each portion.
4. Refrigerate for 10 minutes (up to 4 hours).
5. Place burgers on preheated grill, close lid, and grill until an instant-read meat thermometer inserted sideways into the center of a burger reads 160 degrees F (70 degrees C), about 5 minutes per side.
