## Ingredients

- 2 cups water
- 1 cup quinoa
- 1 ½ cups water
- 1 cup couscous
- 1 (15 ounce) can chickpeas, rinsed and drained
- 2 cups chopped tomatoes
- 2  red bell peppers, chopped
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- 1 cup dried cranberries
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- 2  shallots, chopped
- ¼ cup freshly chopped parsley  (Optional)
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
- 3  lemons, juiced
- ¼ cup curry paste (such as Patak's®)
- 4 teaspoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons white sugar
- 2 teaspoons sesame oil
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup olive oil

## Instructions

1. Bring 2 cups water and quinoa to a boil in a saucepan.
2. Reduce heat to medium-low, cover, and simmer until quinoa is tender, 15 to 20 minutes.
3. Bring 1 1/2 cups water to a boil in a saucepan; remove from heat and stir couscous into the water.
4. Cover saucepan and let stand until water is absorbed completely, about 10 minutes.
5. Fluff couscous with a fork.
6. Combine quinoa, couscous, chickpeas, tomatoes, red bell peppers, celery, cranberries, pecans, shallots, parsley, and cilantro.
7. Combine lemon juice, curry paste, soy sauce, sugar, sesame oil, cumin, and salt in a blender; blend until smooth.
8. Slowly pour olive oil into the blender while it is still running until dressing is emulsified.
9. Pour dressing over quinoa-couscous mixture and toss to coat.
