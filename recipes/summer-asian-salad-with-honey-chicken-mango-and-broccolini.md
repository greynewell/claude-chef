## Ingredients

- 2 teaspoons vegetable oil
- 2  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cubed
- 1 tablespoon honey
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 3 tablespoons Indonesian sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
- 2 tablespoons honey
- 2 heads   bok choy, ends trimmed
- 1 bunch broccolini, trimmed and cut into bite-size pieces
- 3 ounces snow peas, ends and strings removed
- 2 large tomatoes, sliced
- 1 large mango - peeled, seeded, and sliced
- 1 large Hass avocado - peeled, pitted, and sliced
- 1  bell pepper, sliced
- 1 bunch mint

## Instructions

1. Heat vegetable oil in a skillet over medium heat until shimmering, about 1 minute.
2. Add chicken and 1 tablespoon honey.
3. Cook and stir until chicken is no longer pink in the center and juices run clear, 3 to 5 minutes.
4. Cover and remove from heat.
5. Place lemon juice, soy sauce, and 2 tablespoons honey in a small bowl; whisk dressing until well combined.
6. Rinse bok choy, broccolini, and snow peas together in a strainer.
7. Transfer to a microwave-safe bowl; cook in the microwave until lightly steamed, about 3 minutes.
8. Transfer to a large salad bowl; add tomatoes, mango, avocado, bell pepper, and chicken.
9. Pour in dressing and toss well to coat.
10. Serve with mint.
