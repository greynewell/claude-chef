## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package linguine pasta
- ½ cup butter
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, crushed
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- 1 pound crawfish tails
- ½ cup chopped green onions
- 1 cup half-and-half cream
- ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Cook pasta for 8 to 10 minutes, or until al dente; drain.
3. Melt butter with olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
4. Saute garlic and mushrooms until tender.
5. Stir in crawfish and green onions; reduce heat to low, and cook 5 minutes.
6. Stir in half and half, Parmesan cheese, and parsley.
7. Simmer for 5 minutes.
8. Season with salt and pepper, and serve over hot linguine.
