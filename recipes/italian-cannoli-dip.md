## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) container whole milk ricotta cheese
- 1 ½ cups confectioners' sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ¼ cup mini chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Combine ricotta cheese, confectioners' sugar, vanilla extract, and nutmeg in a bowl; whip at medium speed until smooth and blended.
2. Fold in chocolate chips with a spatula.
