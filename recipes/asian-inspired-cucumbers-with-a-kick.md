## Ingredients

- ⅓ cup rice vinegar
- ½  lime, juiced
- 1 ½ teaspoons honey
- 1 ½ teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
- 1 teaspoon peanut butter
- ½ teaspoon minced garlic
- ½ teaspoon sesame oil
- ¼ teaspoon red pepper flakes
- ½ teaspoon sriracha sauce
- ½ large red onion
- 2  cucumbers, unpeeled
- 2 tablespoons chopped peanuts, or to taste
- ½  jalapeno pepper, seeded and sliced, or to taste  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Whisk rice vinegar, lime juice, honey, ginger, peanut butter, garlic, sesame oil, red pepper flakes, and sriracha sauce together in a small bowl to make dressing.
2. Slice red onion into spirals with a spiralizer fitted with a straight-flat blade.
3. Spiralize cucumbers into thin, curly ribbons; snip into serving-size lengths with kitchen shears or a knife.
4. Transfer to a shallow bowl.
5. Drizzle dressing over onion and cucumbers; mix gently to combine.
6. Garnish with peanuts and jalapeno pepper.
