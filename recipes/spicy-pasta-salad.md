## Ingredients

- 1 pound penne pasta
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
- 2  green onions, chopped
- ½ (6 ounce) can black olives, chopped
- ¼ cup sun-dried tomatoes, softened in water and sliced
- ¼ cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing

## Instructions

1. In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook pasta until al dente, rinse under cold water and drain.
2. In a large bowl, combine the pasta, beans, green onions, olives, sun dried tomatoes and feta cheese.
3. Add enough salad dressing to coat; toss and chill before serving.
