## Ingredients

- 8 ounces uncooked dried linguine
- 2 squares Italian Herb Saute Express® Saute Starter
- 1 pound uncooked medium shrimp, peeled, deveined
- 1 cup grape tomatoes, halved
- 1 cup fresh mozzarella pearls or cubed mozzarella
- ¼ cup chopped fresh basil leaves
- ¼ cup shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions; drain.
2. Melt Saute Express® squares in 12-inch nonstick skillet over medium-low heat just until bubbles begin to form.
3. Add shrimp.
4. Saute 5-7 minutes or until shrimp turn pink.
5. Add pasta, tomatoes, mozzarella and basil; stir until coated and heated through.
6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.
