## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- steak seasoning to taste
- 8 ounces rotini pasta
- 8 ounces mozzarella cheese, cubed
- 1  red onion, chopped
- 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
- 6  cherry tomatoes, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat the grill for high heat.
2. Season both sides of chicken breast halves with steak seasoning.
3. Lightly oil the grill grate.
4. Grill chicken 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until juices run clear.
5. Remove from heat, cool, and cut into strips.
6. Meanwhile, place the rotini pasta in a large pot of lightly salted boiling water.
7. Cook 8 to 10 minutes, until al dente.
8. Drain, and rinse with cold water to cool.
9. In a large bowl, mix together the cheese, onion, lettuce, and tomatoes.
10. Toss with the cooled chicken and pasta to serve.
