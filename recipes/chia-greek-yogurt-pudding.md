## Ingredients

- 1 cup unsweetened soy milk
- 1 cup Greek yogurt
- 2 tablespoons hulled hemp seeds
- 2 tablespoons ground flax seeds
- 1 tablespoon honey, or more to taste  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- ⅔ cup chia seeds

## Instructions

1. Whisk soy milk and Greek yogurt together in a large sealable container.
2. Stir hemp seeds, flax seeds, honey, cinnamon, and vanilla extract into yogurt mixture.
3. Stir chia seeds into yogurt mixture until seeds are evenly distributed.
4. Cover the container and refrigerate for 15 minutes.
5. Stir mixture until chia seeds are evenly distributed again.
6. Refrigerate until chilled and set, at least 1 hour.
