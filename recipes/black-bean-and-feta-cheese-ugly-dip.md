## Ingredients

- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- ¼ cup white sugar
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 (11 ounce) can Mexican-style corn, drained
- 1 (4 ounce) package crumbled feta cheese
- 5  green onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. Whisk vinegar, sugar, and olive oil together in a bowl.
2. Mix black beans, corn, feta cheese, and green onions together in a separate bowl. Drizzle dressing over black bean mixture; toss to coat. Refrigerate dip 1 hour before serving.
