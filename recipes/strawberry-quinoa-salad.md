## Ingredients

- 1 cup quinoa
- 2 cups water
- 4 teaspoons lemon juice
- 1 teaspoon olive oil
- 2 cups baby spinach, or to taste
- 2 cups walnuts
- 1 (4 ounce) container feta cheese, crumbled
- ½ cup thinly sliced strawberries, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Place quinoa in a saucepan.
2. Pour 2 cups water over quinoa; bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat; cover.
4. Simmer until quinoa is tender, 12 to 15 minutes.
5. Drain; cool.
6. Combine lemon juice and olive oil in a small bowl; stir until dressing is combined.
7. Pour half of the quinoa into a large salad bowl.
8. Add 1 handful of baby spinach; stir together.
9. Add 1 handful of walnuts, feta cheese, and strawberries; stir until mixed.
10. Repeat layering and stirring quinoa, spinach, feta cheese, and strawberries until all ingredients are mixed together.
11. Top with dressing.
