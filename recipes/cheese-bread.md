## Ingredients

- 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, sliced horizontally
- 4 tablespoons butter
- ½ cup mayonnaise
- 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 4 ounces grated mozzarella cheese
- 4 ounces shredded Cheddar cheese
- ¼ teaspoon garlic salt

## Instructions

1. Preheat the broiler.
2. Place the bread open faced on a cookie sheet.
3. Spread the butter on the bread, then the mayonnaise.
4. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese, mozzarella cheese, Cheddar cheese and garlic salt.
5. Broil 6 to 8 minutes, until the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
