## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package ziti pasta
- 1 (15 ounce) jar Alfredo sauce
- 8 ounces sour cream
- 1 (15 ounce) container ricotta cheese
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- 2  eggs
- 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2. Add ziti and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
3. Drain.
4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
5. Mix   and sour cream together in a bowl.
6. Add cooked pasta and stir until evenly coated.
7. Mix ricotta cheese, Parmesan cheese, and eggs together in another bowl.
8. Pour 1/2 of the pasta mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
9. Spread ricotta-egg mixture over the top.
10. Cover with remaining pasta mixture.
11. Sprinkle, or completely cover, with mozzarella cheese.
12. Bake in the preheated oven until bubbly, about 30 minutes.
