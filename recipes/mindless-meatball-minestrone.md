## Ingredients

- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can beef broth
- 1 (18 ounce) package frozen meatballs
- 1 (16 ounce) package frozen mixed vegetables
- 1 (14.5 ounce) can stewed tomatoes
- ½ cup elbow macaroni
- 1 (15 ounce) can light red kidney beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 ½ teaspoons Italian seasoning
- 6 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat chicken broth and beef broth in a large Dutch oven or stock pot; add meatballs, vegetables, tomatoes, and macaroni.
2. Cover Dutch oven and bring to a boil, about 10 minutes.
3. Remove cover and stir.
4. Stir kidney beans and Italian seasoning into soup; reduce heat to medium.
5. Simmer, stirring frequently, until macaroni is tender, 6 to 7 minutes.
6. Ladle soup into 6 bowls; garnish each with 1 tablespoon Parmesan cheese.
