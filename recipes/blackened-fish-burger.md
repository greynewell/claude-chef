## Ingredients

- 1 (6 ounce) tilapia fillet
- 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning
- 1 tablespoon Greek yogurt
- 1 teaspoon Sriracha sauce
- 1  whole wheat hamburger bun (such as Wonder® Bread)
- 1 leaf red cabbage
- 3 slices avocado

## Instructions

1. Heat a cast iron skillet or griddle over medium-high heat until hot, about 10 minutes.
2. Meanwhile, rub tilapia with Cajun seasoning and set aside.
3. Combine Greek yogurt and Sriracha in a bowl; mix well. Place mixture onto one side of the bun; add red cabbage and avocado.
4. Cook fish in the hot skillet or griddle until almost black, but not burned, about 2 minutes per side; it will should be crunchy on the outside but flaky on the inside. Place fish on the prepared bun.
