## Ingredients

- 1 (5 ounce) can chunk white tuna, drained
- 1 rib celery, diced
- ½  red onion, chopped
- ⅓ cup raisins
- 1 small carrot, chopped
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts  (Optional)
- 3 tablespoons mayonnaise
- 2 tablespoons tamarind chutney
- 1 teaspoon red chile chutney
- 1 teaspoon green chutney
- ¼ teaspoon garam masala
- ¼ teaspoon salt

## Instructions

1. Mix tuna, celery, red onion, raisins, carrot, and walnuts together in a bowl.
2. Add mayonnaise, tamarind chutney, red chile chutney, green chutney, garam masala, and salt; mix with a fork until uniform in consistency.
3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
