## Ingredients

- 4  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ½  onion, diced
- 1 rib celery, diced
- 1 teaspoon Cajon seasoning, divided, or to taste
- ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
- 4 ounces sliced mushrooms
- 1  egg, beaten
- 2 tablespoons fine dry bread crumbs
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
- 2 teaspoons grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Heat water in a sous-vide water bath to 141 degrees F (60.5 degrees C).
2. Place each chicken breast between 2 sheets of heavy plastic wrap on a work surface.
3. Flatten chicken with smooth side of a meat mallet to 1/4-inch thickness.
4. Heat olive oil and butter in a skillet over medium heat.
5. Add onion, celery, and Cajon seasoning; cook and stir until onion is softened and translucent, about 5 minutes.
6. Place mozzarella cheese, mushrooms, egg, bread crumbs, parsley, and Parmesan cheese together with onion mixture in bowl of a food processor; blend into paste.
7. Spread paste onto one side of chicken breasts; roll breasts and secure with toothpicks.
8. Spread remaining paste and sprinkle Cajun seasoning onto rolled breasts; place in individual sealable plastic bags.
9. Seal bags (on low pressure if using a vacuum sealer), removing as much air as possible.
10. Cook chicken in the preheated water bath for 2 hours.
11. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
