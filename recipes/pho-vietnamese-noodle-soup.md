## Ingredients

- 2 (14.5 ounce) cans chicken broth
- 2  star anise pods, or more to taste
- ¾ tablespoon ginger paste
- 1 teaspoon sriracha hot sauce, or more to taste
- 4 ounces tofu, cubed
- ½ cup broccoli florets
- ½ cup sliced mushrooms
- ¼ cup chopped carrots
- ½ (8 ounce) package dried thin rice noodles
- 1 tablespoon chopped green onion

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken broth, star anise, ginger paste, and sriracha hot sauce in a stockpot; bring to a boil.
2. Add tofu, broccoli, mushrooms, and carrots and cook until vegetables are tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
3. Bring a separate pot of water to a boil.
4. Remove pot from heat and add rice noodles to the water; let stand until noodles are softened, about 4 minutes.
5. Drain water and rinse noodles under cold water.
6. Remove star anise from broth mixture.
7. Pile cold noodles into serving bowls and top with broth mixture; garnish with green onion.
