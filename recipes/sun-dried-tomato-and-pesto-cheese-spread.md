## Ingredients

- 4 cloves garlic, peeled
- 1 ½ cups fresh basil leaves
- 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
- ¼ cup pine nuts
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 2 ⅔ cups softened cream cheese
- ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
- 1 ⅓ cups sun-dried tomatoes, packed in oil, drained
- ⅓ cup tomato paste
- ¾ cup butter
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Chop the garlic in a food processor.
2. Mix in basil, lemon juice, pine nuts and olive oil.
3. Process until well blended.
4. Mix in 1/3 cup cream cheese and Parmesan cheese.
5. Blend using pulse setting until almost smooth.
6. Transfer the mixture to a medium bowl.
7. Coarsely chop sun-dried tomatoes in the food processor.
8. Mix in tomato paste and 1/3 cup cream cheese.
9. Blend until smooth.
10. Place 2 cups cream cheese and butter in a medium bowl.
11. Using an electric mixer, beat until fluffy.
12. Season with salt and pepper.
13. Lightly grease a 1 1/2 quart baking dish.
14. Line dish with plastic wrap so that the wrap extends over sides of the dish.
15. Evenly spread 3/4 cup cream cheese and butter mixture in the prepared dish.
16. Layer alternately with 1/2 the sun-dried tomato mixture, 1/2 cup cream cheese and butter mixture and 1/2 the pesto mixture.
17. Repeat layering, topping with remaining cream cheese and butter mixture.
18. Cover and chill in the refrigerator 8 hours, or overnight.
19. Carefully invert dish onto a platter and remove plastic to serve.
