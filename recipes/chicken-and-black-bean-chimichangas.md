## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 cups minced onion
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon dried oregano
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 2 (14 ounce) cans black beans, drained
- 1 ½ pounds shredded cooked chicken
- 2 cups salsa
- 3 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
- ½ cup sliced green onions
- ½ cup chopped cilantro
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 12 (8 inch) flour tortillas
- 1 cup vegetable oil

## Instructions

1. Heat a large saucepan over medium heat.
2. Pour in oil and add the onion and garlic.
3. Saute until they just start to become translucent, about 5 minutes.
4. Add in the oregano, cumin, and chili powder and cook for 1 minute longer.
5. Mix in the black beans, chicken, and salsa.
6. Bring to a boil and cook until heated through, about 5 minutes.
7. Remove from heat and stir in Cheddar, green onions, cilantro, and salt.
8. Using a warm oven or a hot burner, warm the tortillas until they become soft and pliable.
9. Lay out the softened tortillas on a work surface and divide the filling between the tortillas.
10. Fold the sides of each tortilla in toward the middle; roll up like a burrito.
11. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
12. Heat remaining oil in a saute pan over medium heat.
13. When the oil is hot, work in batches and brown each chimichanga on all sides.
14. Transfer chimichangas to a baking pan; place in the oven and bake until heated through, about 15 minutes.
