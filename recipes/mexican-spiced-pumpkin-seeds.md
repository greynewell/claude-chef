## Ingredients

- cooking spray
- 2 cups pumpkin seeds
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon ground cumin
- ½ teaspoon chili powder
- ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
- 1 pinch dried oregano, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil; spray with cooking spray.
3. Spread pumpkin seeds onto the prepared baking sheet; sprinkle with salt, cumin, chili powder, cayenne pepper, and oregano; toss to coat evenly.
4. Bake seeds in the preheated oven for 5 minutes; stir seeds.
5. Return to oven and bake until seeds are toasted, about 15 more minutes.
