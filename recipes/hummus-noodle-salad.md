## Ingredients

- 1 (8 ounce) package dried soba noodles
- 1 cup frozen shelled edamame
- ¾ teaspoon salt, divided
- ½ cup spicy hummus
- ¼ cup seasoned rice vinegar
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 tablespoon liquid aminos (such as Bragg®)
- 1 tablespoon finely chopped cilantro, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 teaspoon minced garlic, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 cup shredded cabbage
- ½ cup chopped roasted cashews
- 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
2. Add soba noodles; cook, stirring occasionally, until tender, 5 to 8 minutes.
3. Drain and let cool.
4. Bring a small pot of water to a boil; add edamame and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
5. Cook, stirring occasionally, until edamame turn bright green, about 5 minutes.
6. Drain and rinse with cold running water.
7. Whisk remaining 1/4 teaspoon salt, hummus, rice vinegar, olive oil, liquid aminos, cilantro, and garlic together in a large bowl.
8. Add soba noodles, edamame, cabbage, and cashews; toss to coat with hummus mixture.
9. Garnish with sesame seeds.
