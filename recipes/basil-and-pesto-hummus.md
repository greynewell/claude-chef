## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) garbanzo beans (chickpeas), drained and rinsed
- ½ cup basil leaves
- 1 clove garlic
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½ teaspoon balsamic vinegar
- ½ teaspoon soy sauce
- salt and ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Combine the garbanzo beans, basil, and garlic in a food processor; pulse several times.
2. Use a spatula to push mixture from sides of processor bowl.
3. Pulse the mixture again while drizzling in the olive oil.
4. Add the vinegar and soy sauce; pulse until combined.
5. Season with salt and pepper.
