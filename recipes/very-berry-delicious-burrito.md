## Ingredients

- 4  Mission® Large/Burrito-size Flour Tortillas
- 8 tablespoons JIF® Peanut Butter
- 4 tablespoons SMUCKER'S® Strawberry Jam
- 1 cup fresh blueberries
- 1 cup diced fresh strawberries

## Instructions

1. Spread each tortilla with 1/4 of each ingredient.
2. Roll into a burrito and cut in half.
3. Serve immediately or wrap in plastic wrap, place in refrigerator for later use.
