## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) package firm tofu
- salt and ground black pepper to taste
- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- 3  eggs, beaten
- 1 tablespoon cooking oil
- ⅓ cup chopped scallion stems
- ¼ cup finely chopped crab
- ¼ cup soy sauce, or as needed  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Place tofu onto a plate and place another plate on top.
2. Set a 3- to 5-pound weight on top.
3. Press tofu for 20 to 30 minutes; drain and discard the accumulated liquid.
4. Chop tofu into thin squares approximately 1 1/4 by 1/2 inch.
5. Season one side of tofu squares with salt and pepper.
6. Place flour in a shallow dish and place beaten egg in a separate shallow dish.
7. Coat tofu with flour first, then with egg, and place on a third dish.
8. Reserve leftover beaten egg.
9. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
10. Fry tofu in the hot oil for 2 minutes; sprinkle scallion on top.
11. Flip tofu, brush remaining egg on top and sprinkle with crabmeat.
12. Heat 2 minutes more and flip again.
13. Brush again with egg, cook 2 minutes.
14. Flip and coat again, for a total of 2 to 3 times.
15. Reduce heat or cook for less time if egg coating is in danger of burning.
16. Pour soy sauce into a bowl and serve with tofu.
