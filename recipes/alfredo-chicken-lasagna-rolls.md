## Ingredients

- 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
- 2  skinless, boneless chicken breasts
- 1 (1 ounce) package ranch dressing mix
- 9  lasagna noodles
- 1 cup Alfredo sauce, or as desired
- 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese, or to taste
- ¼ cup crumbled cooked bacon, or to taste  (Optional)
- 1 pinch dried oregano, or more to taste
- 1 pinch garlic salt, or more to taste

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine evaporated milk, chicken, and ranch dressing mix in a skillet over medium heat; cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center, 10 to 15 minutes.
3. Shred chicken.
4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
5. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
6. Drain and dry on paper towels.
7. Spread   over each noodle; top with shredded chicken.
8. Combine mozzarella cheese, bacon, oregano, and garlic salt in a bowl; sprinkle over chicken layer.
9. Roll each noodle around the filling and place rolls in an 8-inch baking dish.
10. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, 30 to 35 minutes.
