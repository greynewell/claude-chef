## Ingredients

- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vegetable oil
- ⅔ pound prawns, peeled and deveined
- 1 cup chopped green cabbage
- 1 cup chopped carrots
- 1 cup chopped zucchini
- 1 cup chopped fresh mushrooms
- 1 cup chopped broccoli
- 1 (16 ounce) package egg noodles
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 tablespoon minced garlic
- 10 ounces diced boneless chicken
- 2 tablespoons chicken bouillon granules
- 3 tablespoons soy sauce
- 2 teaspoons salt
- ¼ teaspoon sesame oil, to taste
- 1 pinch white sugar, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring a large saucepan of water to a boil; add 1 teaspoon salt and 1 teaspoon vegetable oil.
2. Add prawns, cabbage, carrots, zucchini, mushrooms, broccoli, and egg noodles; cook until tender, about 5 minutes.
3. Rinse under cold water; drain well.
4. Heat 2 tablespoons vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat; cook and stir garlic until fragrant, about 1 minute.
5. Add chicken; cook and stir until browned, 3 to 4 minutes.
6. Add prawns, vegetables, and egg noodles; cook and stir until heated through, 2 to 3 minutes.
7. Season mixture with chicken bouillon granules, soy sauce, and 2 teaspoons salt; mix well.
8. Drizzle sesame oil and sprinkle sugar over chow mein before serving.
