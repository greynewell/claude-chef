## Ingredients

- 4 large tomatoes, diced
- ½ cup chopped fresh basil
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
- ½ pound dry tagliatelle or wide fettucine pasta
- 1 tablespoon shredded Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Stir together the tomatoes, basil, red wine vinegar, olive oil, and oregano in a bowl; allow mixture to stand at least 10 minutes.
2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.
3. Once the water is boiling, stir in the tagliatelle pasta, and return to a boil.
4. Cook the pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until cooked through, but still firm, about 8 minutes.
5. Drain well.
6. Mix the hot cooked tagliatelle into the tomato mixture; sprinkle with Parmesan cheese to serve.
