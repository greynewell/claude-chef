## Ingredients

- 1 (16 ounce) container pico de gallo salsa
- 1 pound cooked small shrimp
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
- 1  avocado - peeled, pitted, and chopped

## Instructions

1. Mix pico de gallo, shrimp, and jalapeno pepper together in a bowl.
2. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate for flavors to blend, 8 hours to overnight.
3. Fold avocado into mixture just before serving.
