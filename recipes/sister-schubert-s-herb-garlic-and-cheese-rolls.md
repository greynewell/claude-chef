## Ingredients

- 1 (15 ounce) package Sister Schubert's Dinner Yeast Rolls
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 1 large clove garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon Italian herb blend
- 2 tablespoons chopped parsley
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
- ⅓ cup grated Parmesan cheese

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
2. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Combine olive oil, garlic, herb blend, parsley, salt and pepper in a bowl.
4. Brush each Sister Schubert Roll with oil mixture.
5. Sprinkle each roll with Parmesan cheese and transfer all to prepared pan.
6. Bake 8 to 12 minutes or until rolls are golden brown.
7. Serve warm.
