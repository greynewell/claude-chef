## Ingredients

- ½ cup mirin (Japanese sweet wine)
- ¼ cup miso paste
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- ½  lime, juiced
- 4  salmon fillets
- 1 tablespoon oil, or as needed

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
2. Whisk mirin, miso, soy sauce, brown sugar, and lime juice together in a bowl or high-rimmed tray. Immerse salmon fillets in the marinade. Marinate for 15 minutes.
3. Heat a well oiled grill pan over medium heat. Add the salmon fillets, reserving marinade in the bowl. Cook, flipping as needed, until grill marks form, 2 to 3 minutes per side. Remove from heat.
4. Place salmon fillets onto a baking sheet and spoon reserved marinade over each fillet.
5. Bake in the preheated oven until fish flakes easily with a fork, about 6 minutes.
