## Ingredients

- 1 cup water
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup whole wheat couscous
- 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
- 1 (15 ounce) can cannellini beans, drained and rinsed
- 1 cup chopped kale
- 4 cloves garlic, chopped
- ¼ cup whole salted almonds, halved
- ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
- salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. Bring water and butter to a boil in a saucepan; remove from heat.
2. Add couscous and stir well.
3. Cover saucepan and  let couscous sit until water is absorbed, about 5 minutes.
4. Fluff couscous with a fork.
5. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat; cook and stir cannellini beans, kale, and garlic in the hot oil until kale is wilted, 5 to 10 minutes.
6. Mix kale mixture into couscous; fold in almonds.
7. Spoon couscous mixture into 4 serving bowls; top with Parmesan cheese.
8. Season with salt and black pepper.
