## Ingredients

- 2 cups frozen corn
- 1 cup chopped cucumber
- ¾ cup seeded and chopped Roma tomato
- ¾ cup chopped green bell pepper
- ¼ cup diced red onion  (Optional)
- ½ cup chopped fresh cilantro, or to taste
- 1  jalapeno pepper, seeded and diced, or more to taste
- 2 tablespoons lime juice
- ¼ cup ranch salad dressing

## Instructions

1. Combine corn, cucumber, tomato, bell pepper, and red onion in a large salad bowl. Mix in cilantro and jalapeno.
2. Sprinkle lime juice over the salad. Add ranch dressing and stir. Cover and refrigerate until serving, at least 30 minutes.
