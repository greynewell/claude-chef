## Ingredients

- 3  skinless, boneless chicken breast halves, cut into strips
- 3 tablespoons cornstarch
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- ½ teaspoon ground ginger
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder, or to taste
- ½ pound spaghetti
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil  (Optional)
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 3 stalks celery, chopped
- 2  carrots, thinly sliced
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Combine chicken strips, cornstarch, soy sauce, ginger, and garlic powder in a large resealable plastic bag.
2. Coat chicken with marinade, squeeze out excess air, and seal bag.
3. Refrigerate for 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
5. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to the bite, about 12 minutes.
6. Drain.
7. Heat 3 tablespoons vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
8. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess; cook and stir in hot oil until chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes.
9. Stir in onion, celery, carrots, and broccoli.
10. Add 1 tablespoon vegetable oil, if needed.
11. Cover and cook until vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally, about 5 minutes.
12. Stir spaghetti into chicken and vegetable mixture; cover and simmer for 5 minutes more.
