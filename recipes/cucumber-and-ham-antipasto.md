## Ingredients

- 2  cucumbers - peeled, seeded, and cubed
- 10  cherry tomatoes, quartered
- 10  pitted kalamata olives
- ½ pound cubed ham
- ½ pound fresh mozzarella cheese, cubed
- ½ cup Italian-style salad dressing

## Instructions

1. Combine the cucumbers, tomatoes, kalamata olives, ham, and mozzarella cheese in a bowl.
2. Pour the Italian dressing over the mixture and toss to combine.
3. Chill until ready to serve.
