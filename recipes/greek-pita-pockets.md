## Ingredients

- ½ cup Greek-style (thick) unflavored yogurt
- 1  lemon, juiced
- 4 ounces bulk pork sausage
- 1 small onion, diced
- ⅓ cup Greek olives, diced
- ¾ cup wild mushrooms, chopped
- 1 cup fresh baby spinach leaves, packed
- 6  eggs, beaten
- ⅔ cup Nikos® feta cheese crumbles
- 2  pitas, halved crosswise

## Instructions

1. In small bowl, stir together yogurt and lemon juice; set aside.
2. In nonstick skillet over medium-high heat, cook sausage, stirring frequently and crumbling for 2 minutes.
3. Add onion, olives, and mushrooms; cook 4 minutes more.
4. Add spinach leaves and cook until wilted and sausage is fully cooked, about 1 to 3 minutes.
5. Add eggs and cook, stirring constantly until almost dry.
6. Remove from heat and stir in feta.
7. Stuff each pita half with feta-egg mixture.
8. Serve immediately with lemon yogurt.
