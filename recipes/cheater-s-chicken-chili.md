## Ingredients

- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- ½  onion, diced
- 1 (8 ounce) jar salsa
- ¼ cup water
- 1 pound rotisserie chicken, meat removed and diced
- 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained
- 1 teaspoon chili powder, or to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat; cook and stir onion in the hot oil until softened, 5 to 10 minutes.
2. Add salsa and water to onion and bring to a boil.
3. Reduce heat and simmer; add chicken, black beans, and chili powder and return to a simmer.
4. Cover saucepan and simmer chili until chicken is heated through, about 10 minutes.
