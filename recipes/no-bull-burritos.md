## Ingredients

- ½ pound ground beef
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- ½ teaspoon dried oregano
- 1 (16 ounce) can refried beans
- 6 (10 inch) flour tortillas
- 2 cups shredded Mexican blend cheese
- ½ cup prepared salsa

## Instructions

1. Place the ground beef in a large skillet set over medium-high heat.
2. Season with garlic powder and oregano, and cook, stirring to crumble, until no longer pink.
3. Drain off grease, then reduce the heat to medium-low and stir in the refried beans and salsa.
4. Cook until heated through.
5. Warm the flour tortillas for 2 minutes in the microwave, or until pliable.
6. Spoon 1/4 cup of the beef mixture into the center, top with shredded cheese, and roll up, folding in the ends when you get to the halfway point.
