## Ingredients

- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 2  serrano chile peppers, halved  (Optional)
- 2 cloves garlic, sliced
- 1 teaspoon chopped ginger
- 1 cup coarsely chopped onion
- 1 cup chopped potatoes
- 2 cups coarsely chopped cabbage
- 5 cups chicken stock
- 1 teaspoon garam masala, or to taste
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil in a saucepan over medium heat.
2. Cook and stir serrano peppers, garlic, and ginger in the hot oil until fragrant, 15 to 20 seconds.
3. Add onion; cook and stir until softened and transparent, 10 to 15 minutes.
4. Mix potatoes into onion mixture and cook for 2 minutes.
5. Add cabbage and cook for 2 minutes more.
6. Pour chicken stock over cabbage mixture; bring to a boil.
7. Reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are softened, 10 to 15 minutes.
8. Season soup with garam masala and salt.
9. Pour soup into a blender no more than half full.
10. Cover and hold lid down; pulse a few times before leaving on to blend.
11. Puree in batches until smooth.
12. Return soup to saucepan and cook until warmed, 2 to 3 minutes.
