## Ingredients

- ½ cup real maple syrup
- ½ cup ketchup
- ⅓ cup rice wine vinegar
- ¼ cup sesame oil
- ¼ cup olive oil
- 2 tablespoons yellow mustard
- 1 tablespoon sesame seeds, or more to taste
- ⅓ cup sliced green onions
- 1  egg, beaten
- ¼ cup low-sodium soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
- ½ teaspoon onion powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 pound ground turkey
- ½ cup panko bread crumbs
- 2 cups carrots, chopped  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
2. Combine maple syrup, ketchup, vinegar, sesame oil, olive oil, mustard, and sesame seeds in a medium-sized saucepan and bring to a simmer.
3. Simmer sweet-and-sour sauce for about 5 minutes and set aside.
4. Combine green onions, egg, soy sauce, ginger, onion powder, black pepper, and garlic powder in a mixing bowl.
5. Beat together until well mixed.
6. Add ground turkey and top with panko bread crumbs.
7. Mix well using your hands to combine all ingredients.
8. Use a standard cookie scoop to make uniform meatballs and place them in an even layer in an ungreased glass 9x13-inch pan.
9. Place chopped carrots evenly between meatballs in the pan.
10. Pour sweet-and-sour sauce evenly on top.
11. Cover with aluminum foil.
12. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes.
13. Uncover and bake and additional 10 minutes.
