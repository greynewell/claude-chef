## Ingredients

- 1  potato
- 8 slices white sandwich bread
- 2 cups milk
- ½ cup carp roe
- 1 small onion, chopped
- ¼ cup fresh lemon juice
- ½ cup olive oil, or more if needed
- 2  Greek olives  (Optional)

## Instructions

1. Preheat an oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C).
2. Prick the potato in several places with a fork and place on a baking sheet.
3. Bake the potato in the preheated oven easily pierced with a fork, 50 minutes to 1 hour.
4. Cool completely in refrigerator.
5. Peel and chop.
6. Place the bread slices in a shallow dish; pour the milk over the bread to cover completely.
7. Allow to soak a few minutes before squeezing as much moisture as possible from the bread, discarding the milk.
8. Blend the bread, potato, carp roe, onion, lemon juice, and olive oil together in a food processor until fluffy, about 1 minute.
9. Add more olive oil if needed to reach a desired consistency; chill.
10. Garnish with the Greek olives to serve.
