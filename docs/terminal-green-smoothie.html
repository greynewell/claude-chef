Terminal Green Smoothie

Prep 5min Cook 0min 1 servings 320 cal

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh spinach, packed
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder (30g protein)
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/2 cup ice
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add almond milk to the blender first. This prevents the blades from jamming on solids.
  2. Add spinach and blend on low for 10 seconds to break down the leaves.
  3. Add frozen banana, avocado, protein powder, and almond butter.
  4. Blend on high for 45 seconds until completely smooth. No chunks should remain.
  5. Add ice and blend for another 15 seconds for desired thickness.
  6. Taste and add honey if needed. Pour and serve immediately.

Notes

Frozen banana is non-negotiable. It provides the creamy texture without ice diluting the flavor. If your bananas are fresh, double the ice. The avocado adds healthy fats and makes the texture velvety.
